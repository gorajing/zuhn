## What Makes This Role Unique vs. Generic Enterprise SaaS

### 1. You're Selling Something That Changes Weekly
New model releases, capability expansions, pricing changes. Your product is a moving target. You need to stay current on model capabilities, benchmarks, and competitive comparisons. This is not selling Salesforce licenses.

### 2. Technical Buyers Are Your Primary Audience
CTOs, VP Engineering, ML leads, platform engineers. They will test Claude against GPT-4o and Gemini before meeting you. Your job is to understand their evaluation criteria and ensure Claude wins on the dimensions that matter to their specific use case.

### 3. Trust and Safety Are Sales Arguments, Not Marketing Fluff
Regulated industries (healthcare, finance, government) care deeply about data privacy, model transparency, and responsible AI. Anthropic's safety research (Constitutional AI, interpretability, alignment) is your competitive moat. Learn it, understand it, sell it.

### 4. Developer Adoption Is Your Wedge
Many enterprise deals start with developers already using Claude API. Your job is to find those internal champions, help them build the business case, and navigate procurement. This is bottom-up + top-down hybrid selling.
