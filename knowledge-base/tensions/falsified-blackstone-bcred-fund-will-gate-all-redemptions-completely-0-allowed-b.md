## Resolution
**Status: already_resolved** -- The prediction was deliberately aggressive and has been falsified. Blackstone BCRED maintained partial redemptions at 7.9% and invested $400M to stabilize. The underlying principle (PRI-260321-2DF0, opacity + leverage = fragility) remains sound; the prediction was too aggressive an extrapolation from a valid structural observation. The principle should be narrowed: private credit fragility is real but manifests as partial stress and managed containment, not catastrophic collapse, because large players like Blackstone have balance-sheet firepower to absorb shocks. Systemic risk materializes through cumulative degradation rather than sudden collapse in markets where dominant players can self-insure.

**Prediction:** The principle will be validated not by a single dramatic gate but by a pattern of rising default rates, narrowing redemption windows, and increasing fund self-investment across the private credit sector over 2026-2027.
