# Topic: sound-design

> 2 insights

- `INS-260320-D44B` [high] The foundational rule of video sound design: you should be able to hear everything you see -- nothing on screen is truly silent, and even empty rooms have ambient sound.
- `INS-260320-105E` [medium] The sound design workflow for video follows three steps: lay down temp music/score first, add creative SFX and foley second, then apply final audio treatment and mixing.
