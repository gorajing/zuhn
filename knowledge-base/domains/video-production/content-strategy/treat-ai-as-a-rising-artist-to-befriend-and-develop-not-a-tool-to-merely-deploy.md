After her career crisis and pregnancy forced a reset, Yoon decided to openly use AI in K-pop MV production at a time when the industry was reluctant to reveal AI usage (because the subjects being 'branded' were artists, not products). Her mental model was key: she treated AI the same way she'd treat a promising new artist she discovered — someone to get to know, become friends with, and develop together.

The result was IVE's All Day Project 'Famous' MV, which became a watershed moment for AI in K-pop. What previously took enormous time in design, modeling, texture application, and simulation was drastically reduced. AI even helped write the persuasion documents for clients. The industry reaction was massive — collaboration offers flooded in, and K-pop MVs broadly started using AI openly afterward. The reframe from 'tool deployment' to 'artistic collaboration' makes the difference between superficial usage and genuine creative expansion.
