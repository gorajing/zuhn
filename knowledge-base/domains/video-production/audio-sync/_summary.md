# Topic: audio-sync

> 2 insights

- `INS-260320-10E8` [high] Always clap once or use a clapboard at the start of each take -- the sharp transient creates a visible spike in both audio waveforms, making manual sync alignment trivial.
- `INS-260320-232D` [high] Set all audio devices to 48kHz when recording for video -- mismatched sample rates between camera and external recorder cause drift that worsens over longer recordings.
