The video highlights that Epoch AI, which provides a popular math benchmark, was funded by OpenAI — a company whose models are evaluated on that benchmark. This is a textbook conflict of interest that should make anyone skeptical of benchmark rankings.

The deeper pattern is that the AI industry lacks independent, credibly neutral evaluation infrastructure. When evaluation is funded by participants, there's pressure (conscious or not) to produce results favorable to funders. Practitioners should weight vibe-checks, real-world task performance, and independently-funded evaluations more heavily than headline benchmark numbers.
