The SBI data separates two ideas often blended together in AI-pricing narratives. Pure usage-based pricing is now around 21% of SaaS, while outcome-based pricing is 0.6%. Platform fees with usage included are even more common, showing a hybrid migration rather than a clean jump from seats to outcomes.

This weakens any belief that usage, task, and outcome pricing are interchangeable signs of the same completed transition. For Zuhn, the confidence update should be asymmetric: confidence can remain higher that AI pushes markets away from pure per-seat pricing, but confidence should be lower that the destination is primarily outcome pricing in the near term.
