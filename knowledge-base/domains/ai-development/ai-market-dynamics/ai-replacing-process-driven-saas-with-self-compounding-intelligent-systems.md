Jacco van der Kooij's framework distinguishes between traditional SaaS that automates existing processes and AI-native SaaS that builds intelligent systems capable of self-compounding improvement. The difference isn't incremental — it's architectural. Process-driven SaaS scales linearly with headcount and playbook execution, while intelligence-driven systems create feedback loops where each customer interaction improves the product for all users. This maps directly to the GTM revolution happening in 2025: AI-native companies are outpacing peers not just in product but in pricing models and role definitions. The implication for builders is that bolting AI onto existing SaaS workflows captures a fraction of the value compared to redesigning the system around intelligence as the core primitive.
