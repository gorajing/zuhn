The Terafab produces two chip types: edge inference (for Optimus robots and vehicles) and space-hardened compute (for orbital AI). The edge chip demand alone is staggering — if humanoid robots reach even 10% of Musk's 1-10 billion unit/year projection, that's 100M-1B chips annually, dwarfing current automotive chip demand. This reframes the NVIDIA/TSMC chokepoint: at this scale, no existing supply chain can serve you, so you must build your own. The parallel to SpaceX is exact — there was no rocket supplier ecosystem, so SpaceX built everything internally. Tesla is doing the same for chips.
