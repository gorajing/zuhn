Prime Intellect draws a powerful analogy: would you rather have the smartest person in history or someone who's been at your company for 30 years? Sometimes domain expertise and institutional knowledge matter more than raw intelligence. In AI terms, a prompt is a fresh conversation every time; training is how institutional knowledge compounds.

The implication for AI companies: your proprietary data and the feedback loops that generate it are your real competitive moat, not your prompt engineering. Companies that can encode their best practices, domain expertise, and accumulated learnings into training data (via fine-tuning and RL) will pull away from competitors relying solely on prompt-level customization of foundation models.
