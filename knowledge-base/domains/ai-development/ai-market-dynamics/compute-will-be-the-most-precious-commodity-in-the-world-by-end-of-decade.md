Altman opens with a bold claim: 'Compute is gonna be the currency of the future... maybe the most precious commodity in the world.' This frames the entire AI race as fundamentally an infrastructure and resource competition, not just an algorithms competition.

For investors and builders, this suggests that controlling compute resources — data centers, chip supply chains, energy infrastructure — may matter more than any individual model architecture. The companies that secure compute access at scale will have durable competitive advantages.
