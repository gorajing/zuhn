OpenAI generating $3.4B from ChatGPT subscriptions alone signals that consumer willingness to pay for AI is not hypothetical — it's proven at scale. This challenges the narrative that AI monetization is primarily an enterprise play. The subscription model creates predictable recurring revenue while building user habits that increase switching costs. For startups considering AI business models, this validates the direct-to-consumer subscription path, though competing against OpenAI's distribution advantage requires significant differentiation in vertical or workflow-specific value.
