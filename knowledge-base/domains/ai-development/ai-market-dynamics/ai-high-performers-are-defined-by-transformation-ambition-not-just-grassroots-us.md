McKinsey defines AI high performers as respondents reporting more than 5% EBIT impact and significant value from AI, representing about 6% of the survey. These organizations are more likely to use AI for growth or innovation as well as efficiency, and half say they intend to transform the business. This challenges adoption narratives that treat local tool pull as the main signal; the stronger predictor in this evidence is whether the company turns AI into a transformation agenda.
