The DORA 2025 findings directly pressure the broad version of INS-260322-1D52, the belief that AI breaks the Mythical Man-Month by letting companies throw money and compute at problems to close leads quickly. Google Cloud's summary says AI does not fix a team; it amplifies what is already there: strong teams become more efficient, while struggling teams expose and intensify their problems.

This is strong counterevidence because it comes from a large practitioner dataset and DORA's long-running software-delivery research program, not a pundit claim. It does not falsify AI leverage; DORA still observes positive relationships with throughput and product performance in 2025. The warranted movement is narrower: lower confidence in any claim that capital, GPUs, or AI tools by themselves dissolve coordination, workflow, and team-quality bottlenecks.
