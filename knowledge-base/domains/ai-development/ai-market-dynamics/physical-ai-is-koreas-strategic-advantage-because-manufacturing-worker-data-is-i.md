ChatGPT was trained on publicly available internet text and images. Physical AI (robots, autonomous systems) needs movement data that doesn't exist on the internet -- it must be created. American robots are trained by random part-time workers, but manufacturing robots need skilled workers' movement data: welding, assembly, precision tasks.

Korea's unique position: it still has both a living manufacturing sector (ships, semiconductors, even kimchi factories) AND veteran skilled workers in places like Ulsan and Changwon. If these veterans' movements are captured before they retire -- wearing motion-capture gear while welding, assembling, manufacturing -- this becomes proprietary training data for physical AI that only Korea possesses. Unlike internet data, this can be encrypted and used as a geopolitical bargaining chip in the post-globalization era.
