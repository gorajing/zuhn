The historical moat in equity research was access—proprietary data feeds, expert networks, analyst coverage. AI collapses this advantage because public data combined with structured prompting can now replicate much of what institutional research desks produce. The edge shifts from 'what data do you have' to 'how effectively do you synthesize and test hypotheses.'

This is a specific instance of a broader pattern: AI commoditizes information access while creating new advantages around orchestration and judgment. The winners are not those with the best models or the most data, but those who build the most effective multi-step analytical workflows. This applies beyond finance—any domain where information asymmetry was the primary moat faces similar disruption.
