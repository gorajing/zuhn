The prevailing 2024 narrative was that DeepSeek and similar releases had effectively closed the open-weight gap. The AI Index 2026 data shows the opposite for 2025: the top closed-weight model led the top open-weight model by 3.3% as of March 2026, up from just 0.5% in August 2024, and 6 of the top 10 models on the Arena Leaderboard are now closed-weight. The gap is not a race where open inevitably catches up — it is an oscillation driven by how aggressively closed labs deploy training compute. Strategy implication: any bet that assumes free open-weight models will be within months of frontier performance is fragile, and any bet that assumes open-weight will permanently lag is equally fragile. Open-weight access is intermittent, not secular.
