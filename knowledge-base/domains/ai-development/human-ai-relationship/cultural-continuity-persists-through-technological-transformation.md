Surya's sentimental closing: a Stanford foundation model for ocean modeling is named Samudra (Sanskrit for ocean); a foundation model for the sun is named Surya (his own name). Sanskrit dominated the Indian subcontinent thousands of years ago when most people were farmers. The continuity of culture from farmer to AI engineer, the eponymous use of ancient language for frontier technology — this suggests humans carry cultural anchors through every technological transition, and we should expect cultural continuity rather than cultural rupture in the AI era.

Application: technology adoption discourse often presumes radical discontinuity ('AI changes everything'). The empirical pattern is closer to 'humans adopt new tools while threading their cultural identity through them.' Useful counter-frame for thinking about institutional adoption of AI.
