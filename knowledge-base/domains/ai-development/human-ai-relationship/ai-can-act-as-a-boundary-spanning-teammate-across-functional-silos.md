P&G's result is especially relevant because the work required commercial and R&D perspectives. Without AI, participants leaned toward their native function: commercial workers produced more market-oriented proposals and R&D workers more technical ones. AI-enabled participants produced more balanced solutions even when working individually. For AI workflow design, this means the highest-value prompt or agent pattern may be 'import the missing discipline' rather than 'do my current discipline faster.'
