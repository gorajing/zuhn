Zuckerberg frames Meta's two strategic pillars as presence (AR/VR) and personalized AI (Llama/Meta AI). The convergence point is glasses: AI becomes truly useful when it has both global context (your preferences, history) and real-time local context (what's physically happening around you). Glasses are uniquely positioned to provide both.

This is a significant strategic bet -- it implies that whoever owns the glasses form factor owns the AI context pipeline. For developers and founders, this means the primary AI interface may shift from text boxes and voice assistants to an always-on ambient intelligence layer. Building for that paradigm requires different UX thinking than current chat-based AI.
