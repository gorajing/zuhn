The fab lab network (over 2,000 labs worldwide) follows the same democratization pattern as computing: mainframes became PCs became smartphones. CNC machines, 3D printers, and laser cutters are following the same cost and accessibility curve.

Gershenfeld's vision is that personal fabrication will eventually be as accessible as personal computing - anyone can make almost anything. This doesn't just change manufacturing; it changes the nature of consumption, education, and economic development. Communities that can fabricate their own tools, parts, and products become less dependent on global supply chains.
