This system can scan genomic regulatory space and propose binding changes or regulatory clusters, but the decisive constraints remain biological interpretation, assay design, crop context, and experimental validation. The field bottleneck changes from finding candidate mechanisms by hand to triaging model-generated mechanisms into wet-lab work.
