With AI providing tailored instruction across nearly any domain instantly, the historical moat of specialized knowledge erodes rapidly. Anyone with a browser can now access expert-level guidance that previously required expensive education or mentorship.

The competitive gap shifts downstream: from who has access to information toward who can adopt and apply it fastest. This means the bottleneck is no longer learning but executing — the ability to take AI-generated knowledge and convert it into tangible output before competitors do the same. Organizations and individuals who build systems for rapid skill deployment will outperform those who merely accumulate knowledge.
