For PRED-260407-5C97, Yale Budget Lab is counterevidence but not a clean falsifier. Recent college graduates show only a slight increase in occupational dissimilarity versus older graduates, and that trend may predate ChatGPT or reflect a generally slowing labor market. The same section explicitly notes compatibility with Brynjolfsson et al.'s evidence of possible early-career effects and warns about small CPS samples. Confidence in a dramatic 50% FAANG junior-SWE hiring collapse should move down modestly, not be discarded.
