Dario Amodei identifies critical thinking as the single most important
skill in an AI-saturated world. When AI can generate any content —
text, images, video, code — the differentiating human capability becomes
the ability to evaluate what's real, what's trustworthy, and what's
worth acting on. He emphasizes "street smarts" — not getting fooled by
fake content, not falling for scams, not adopting false beliefs. This
is fundamentally about epistemic hygiene: the capacity to question,
verify, and maintain accurate mental models when surrounded by
AI-generated output of varying quality and truthfulness.
