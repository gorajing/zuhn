Dario Amodei reveals that Anthropic has conducted internal studies on
AI deskilling — the degradation of human skills through AI reliance.
Their findings show that deskilling in coding is real, but the critical
variable is HOW you use the model, not WHETHER you use it. Some usage
patterns preserve and even enhance skills, while others cause measurable
degradation. He warns: "If we deploy AI carelessly, people could become
stupider." This is first-party research from the company that builds
the models, making it especially credible. The implication is that
mindful, deliberate AI usage patterns need to be developed and taught,
not just tool access.
