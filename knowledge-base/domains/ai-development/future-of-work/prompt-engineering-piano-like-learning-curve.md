Dario Amodei compares prompt engineering to playing piano: you can't
just read about it and become proficient. It requires hands-on practice,
iteration, and developing intuition for what works. Anthropic recognizes
this barrier and is actively working to lower it — Claude Code serves
coders, and "co-work" mode is being developed for non-coders. They also
have a "Ministry of Education" team creating learning resources. But the
fundamental insight is that effective AI use is a practiced skill with
a real learning curve, not something you pick up instantly. This argues
for investing time in deliberate practice with AI tools rather than
expecting immediate mastery.
