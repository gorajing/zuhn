The survey reports use-case cost and revenue benefits and broad innovation effects, but only 39% of respondents attribute any enterprise-level EBIT impact to AI, and most of those attribute less than 5% of EBIT. This is a useful bridge between pro-AI and skeptical evidence: AI can improve local tasks while still failing to move enterprise metrics because the surrounding workflow, review, governance, and allocation systems remain unchanged.
