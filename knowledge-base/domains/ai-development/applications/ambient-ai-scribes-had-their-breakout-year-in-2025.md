The AI Index 2026 Chapter 6 identifies ambient AI scribes as the single medical AI technology with clear real-world traction in 2025. AI tools that automatically generate clinical notes from patient visits saw broad adoption across multiple hospital systems in 2025. Physicians reported spending up to 83% less time writing notes, experienced significant reductions in burnout, and one hospital system reported a 112% return on investment. The structural reason this works while other clinical AI tools stall is that ambient scribes augment an existing workflow rather than requiring workflow replacement — the physician still conducts the visit, still makes the decision, still approves the output. It is the lowest-risk, highest-compensation form of medical AI deployment. The broader lesson for AI in regulated industries is that augmentation of a pain point (physician burnout from documentation) crosses the deployment bar far faster than replacement of a clinical decision, and the economics follow — a 112% ROI is rare in health IT, and it is being achieved by the least capability-demanding application of LLMs in medicine.
