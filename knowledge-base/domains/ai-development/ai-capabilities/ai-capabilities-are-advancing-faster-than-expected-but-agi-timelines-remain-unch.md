Dalton acknowledges that AI models improved faster than he expected as of January 2025, with a burst of strong new models from multiple providers in late 2025. But he notes a meta-problem: you can't just run a benchmark and know if a model is good -- it takes real-world usage time to assess quality. Despite faster capability gains, his AGI timeline estimates remain roughly unchanged.

Michael is more explicit: he was skeptical of AGI in January 2025 and remains skeptical. He warns that AGI as a concept 'sucks up all the oxygen in the room' and recounts a founder who was paralyzed by insider claims of superintelligence being 6-18 months away. Critically, even those making such claims weren't operating their businesses as if AGI were imminent -- revealing a duality between stated beliefs and revealed preferences.
