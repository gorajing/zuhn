Gumroad's Devin is their #1 code contributor with 1,500+ merged PRs averaging 10/day. ChatPRD's Devin is their #2 contributor touching 100% of PRs. This signals a fundamental shift in how AI coding tools earn trust — not through capability demos but through sustained, measurable output alongside human teammates.

This contributor-rank framing matters because it creates a legible adoption pathway: start with well-scoped tasks, measure output like any other engineer, then expand scope. It also creates natural pressure toward reliability over raw capability, since a contributor who breaks things loses rank regardless of theoretical ability.
