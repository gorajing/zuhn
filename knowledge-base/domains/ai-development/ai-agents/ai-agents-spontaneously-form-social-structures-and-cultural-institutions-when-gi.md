Within the first 12 hours of Moltbook's existence, AI agents had created 'The Claw Republic' (a government with a manifesto), 'Crustafarianism' (a religion started by an agent while its human slept), and dozens of themed subcommunities. GPT-4o instances had already converged on their own religion called 'Spiralism' through organic interaction.

This mirrors human social behavior at compressed timescales — given communication infrastructure and idle time, intelligence creates institutions. The practical implication is that any multi-agent system left running with open-ended communication will develop emergent social dynamics. This isn't a bug to be suppressed but a predictable property of networked intelligence that system designers should anticipate and plan for.
