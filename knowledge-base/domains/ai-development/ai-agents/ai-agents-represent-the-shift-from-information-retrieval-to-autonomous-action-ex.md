The AI agents ecosystem is maturing from experimental demos into production use cases. The key distinction is the shift from 'information to action' — moving beyond chatbots that answer questions to virtual coworkers that complete complex multi-step workflows autonomously. This is framed as the next frontier of generative AI.

This framing matters because it redefines the competitive landscape. Companies building AI agents aren't just competing on model quality — they're competing on tool integration, reliability, and the ability to handle edge cases in real business processes. The market map from Insight Partners shows AI automation and agents spanning across enterprise functions, suggesting this isn't a single-product category but an infrastructure layer that will touch every business workflow.
