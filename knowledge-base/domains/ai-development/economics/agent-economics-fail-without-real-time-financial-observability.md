IBM projects AI spend rising from just under 15% of IT budgets in 2025 to nearly 25% by 2027, but reports that 84% of tech CxOs have not fully operationalized AI financial management and 85% lack full visibility into real-time AI spend. This challenges the idea that agent operating systems are primarily an execution architecture: at enterprise scale, they are also a cost-allocation and capital-control architecture. If token, tool, model, and workflow spend cannot be attributed in real time, agent expansion can look productive while eroding budget discipline.
