The six LLM cost strategies — cheaper model testing, OpenRouter management,
AI-rewritten prompts, free tier usage, input filtering, cheap model
categorization, and batching — compound multiplicatively. Applied together
to production systems, they reduced monthly costs from $300+ to ~$10 while
simultaneously processing 10x more data. The key insight is that these
strategies are not alternatives — they stack. Each one removes a different
layer of waste.
