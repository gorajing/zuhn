Leyden frames AI not as another technology but as a civilizational step-change analogous to the steam engine. Mechanical engines amplified human physical power, creating the enormous prosperity of the industrial age. AI and digital computers amplify human mental power, and the prosperity this creates will dwarf what came before.

The ChatGPT launch in November 2022 may be remembered as the starting gun of the 'Age of AI' — using 'age' deliberately in the same sense as the Bronze Age or Iron Age, meaning a fundamental threshold that civilization crosses and never returns from. This is not incremental improvement but a qualitative shift in what humans can accomplish.
