x402's pitch is not just cheaper payments; it is instant programmatic access to APIs, content, microservices, and proxy services. The source also points toward discovery for buyers, including agents.

That implies a new competitive surface for services. Agents need to know what a paid endpoint does, what it costs, what evidence it returns, refund or failure semantics, latency expectations, and allowed reuse. Human landing pages become less important than machine-readable service cards attached to payable endpoints.
