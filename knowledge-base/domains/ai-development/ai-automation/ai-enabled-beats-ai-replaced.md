The best users of AI automation are "super AI enabled, not replacing
their entire job with AI." The people claiming "50 agents run my
company" are building slot machines — systems they don't understand
producing outputs they can't verify. The right model is augmentation
over replacement: keep the human in the loop for judgment calls,
creative decisions, and quality-critical work, while automating the
repetitive, mechanical parts. This is not a limitation of current AI
— it is a fundamental principle. The human provides the understanding
that makes the automation trustworthy. Without that understanding,
you are just adding complexity and uncertainty.
