Flock Safety's technology creates a paradigm shift from subjective-based policing (officer pulls over someone matching a vague description) to objective-based policing (AI confirms the exact vehicle). This has profound implications for reducing false arrests and racial profiling.

Before Flock: vague description leads to wrong person pulled over, nervous encounter, potential incident. After Flock: confirmed match means you send a team, apprehend safely, no innocent people endangered. Vegas's 90%+ murder clearance rate comes not just from technology but from the community trust it builds — people actually talk to police when they know investigations are competent and fair. The privacy criticism is largely misplaced; license plate readers are far less invasive than the cell phone location data the federal government already uses.
