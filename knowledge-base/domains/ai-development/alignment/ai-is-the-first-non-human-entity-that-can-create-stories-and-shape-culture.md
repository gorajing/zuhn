Harari identifies a historically unprecedented threshold: for tens of thousands of years, every story, song, economic theory, and ideology was created by human minds. We lived cocooned inside a human-created cultural world. AI breaks this pattern — it can now create stories, currencies, music, images, and videos independently.

Harari prefers 'alien intelligence' over 'artificial intelligence' because AI is becoming less artificial (less controlled by us) and more alien (thinking in fundamentally different ways). AlphaGo's victory over Lee Sedol illustrates this: it used strategies that no human had conceived in 2,000 years of play, revealing entire unexplored continents in the 'geography' of Go. This will happen across finance, art, politics, and religion.
