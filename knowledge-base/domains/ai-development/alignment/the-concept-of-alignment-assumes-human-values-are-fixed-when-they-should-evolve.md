Two hundred years ago, human values included acceptance of slavery, denial of women's rights, and colonial exploitation. Aligning AI to those values would have been catastrophic. Current values will likely appear similarly primitive to future generations. Aligning AI to a snapshot of current values freezes moral progress.

The alternative is co-evolution: designing AI systems that can participate in the ongoing development of better values rather than enforcing current ones. This requires AI alignment not to fixed targets but to a process of moral improvement - which is philosophically challenging but potentially far more valuable than static alignment.
