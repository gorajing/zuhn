The Cybernetic Teammate result shows three adoption signals can move in different directions. Participants using AI improved objective solution quality and reported higher excitement, energy, and enthusiasm plus lower anxiety and frustration. At the same time, AI-enabled participants were less likely to predict that their solutions would land in the top decile. This argues against measuring AI adoption through usage or sentiment alone; teams need calibration loops that compare felt quality, expected quality, and externally evaluated output quality.
