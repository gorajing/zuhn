Every major technology budget shift (TV to digital, client-server to cloud) required rebuilding the entire measurement stack. Google and Facebook's ad revenue wouldn't have grown as quickly without 15+ years of attribution infrastructure (DoubleClick, Comscore, Omniture, Nielsen's digital migration).

Enterprise AI is at the same inflection point: $700B is being spent, 70% of IT leaders believe they're wasting money, and 85% feel they have only 18 months to become AI leaders or fall behind. Yet there's no system to measure whether any of it works. The opportunity: build the measurement and governance layer — not as a gatekeeper, but as an accelerator. Companies that prove AI tools work will unlock far more budget than gatekeepers who restrict spending.
