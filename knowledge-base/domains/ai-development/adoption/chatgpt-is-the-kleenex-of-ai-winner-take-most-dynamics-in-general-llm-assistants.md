The a16z consumer team presents data showing the general LLM assistant market is trending toward winner-take-most. ChatGPT leads with 800-900M weekly active users. Gemini sits at roughly 35% of ChatGPT's scale on web, 40% on mobile (boosted by Android distribution). Claude, Grok, and Perplexity trail at 8-10% of usage each.

The stickiness data is striking: only 9% of consumers pay for more than one LLM, and under 10% of ChatGPT users even visit competitors during the year. ChatGPT has become the category brand — 'the Kleenex of AI.' However, challengers can win by specializing: Claude owns the hyper-technical user, Perplexity carved out search/browser territory, and viral model moments (Nano Banana, VO3) can create temporary surges. The enterprise channel also matters — ChatGPT enterprise usage grew 8-9x YoY, and workplace adoption locks in consumer habits.
