IBM reports that two-thirds of surveyed CIOs and CTOs are accountable for AI systems they do not fully control, while 70% say business teams deploy technology faster than IT can track. This challenges agent-operating-system optimism because the missing layer is not just better autonomous execution; it is enterprise-wide visibility into who deployed which agents, what they can access, and which systems they can affect. For practical AI deployments, observability and inventory should be treated as first-class product surfaces before expanding autonomy.
