The McKinsey evidence pressures the view that top-down AI programs mostly create adoption theater. High performers are three times more likely than peers to report strong senior leadership ownership and commitment to AI. The key qualifier is what leadership does: role modeling, continued funding, budget reprioritization, workflow redesign, KPI tracking, and business-process embedding are different from a usage dashboard mandate.
