# Topic: synthesis

> 3 insights

- `INS-260321-A6A6` [high] 808 bass was originally untuned and felt through car Alpine systems — now it must be intentionally distorted and mixed to be heard on laptop speakers, fundamentally changing production technique.
- `INS-260320-CDE4` [high] The Moog DFAM has no MIDI implementation -- sampling its output into a MIDI-capable device is the primary way to integrate it into a broader production setup.
- `INS-260320-77C2` [high] Sample analog synth sounds (like Moog DFAM) into a digital sampler/sequencer (like Elektron Digitakt) to get warm analog timbre with precise digital sequencing and pattern control.
