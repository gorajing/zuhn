To learn a genre deeply, search YouTube for '[genre name] mixset' and find videos of DJs playing live sets. A 60-minute set contains roughly 30 curated songs, and those 30 songs were selected from hundreds the DJ listened to. This means each mix set is a concentrated distillation of deep genre knowledge, delivered as pure listening experience rather than academic explanation.

The key is repetition — keep listening to genre mix sets until the sonic signature is internalized. Then when you encounter that sound elsewhere, you can immediately place it. This is the difference between knowing a genre name intellectually and recognizing it instinctively. The approach also leverages the DJ's professional expertise as a filter, since DJs cannot perform without extensive listening in their specialty genre.
