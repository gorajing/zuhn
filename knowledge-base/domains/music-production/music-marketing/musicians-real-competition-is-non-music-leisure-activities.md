Zoo Labs emphasizes that musicians must redefine their competitive landscape. The audience deciding whether to attend a show is not choosing between Band A and Band B — they're choosing between a concert, dinner with friends, Netflix, or staying home with a book. This is a Jobs-to-Be-Done insight applied to live music.

This reframing has immediate strategic implications. Marketing, pricing, and experience design should benchmark against the broader leisure economy, not just other musical acts. A band's value proposition competes with the comfort of a couch, which means the live experience must offer something irreplaceable — community, energy, presence — that passive entertainment cannot match.
