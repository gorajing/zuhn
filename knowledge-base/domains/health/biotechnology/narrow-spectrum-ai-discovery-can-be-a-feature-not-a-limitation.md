The source positions N. gonorrhoeae as a pathogen with increasing resistance to all recommended antibiotics and frames the output as selective antibacterial compounds against difficult-to-treat strains. In antibiotics, narrow targeting can matter because broad activity is not automatically the best clinical or ecological goal.

The general AI lesson is that domain constraints should shape the objective. An application can be more valuable when it optimizes for a narrow operational envelope with high stakes than when it tries to be broadly capable.
