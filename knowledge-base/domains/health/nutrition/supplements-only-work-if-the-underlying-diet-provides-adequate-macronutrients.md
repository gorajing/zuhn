Berg spent years taking 100+ vitamins daily while on a vegan diet with chronic fatigue, insomnia, and arthritis. None of it worked because his macronutrient foundation was wrong — insufficient protein and fat. The fix was not more supplements but switching to animal protein and fat as energy source. Key lesson: supplements are the finishing touch on a good diet, not a substitute for one. Protein from plants was not satisfying and led to persistent energy crashes. Fat should be the primary energy source, not carbohydrates.
