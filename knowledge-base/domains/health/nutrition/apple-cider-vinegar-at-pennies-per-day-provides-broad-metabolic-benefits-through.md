Acetic acid in apple cider vinegar: 1) Slows gastric emptying, reducing post-meal blood sugar spikes by 20-30%, 2) Stimulates stomach acid production (many digestive issues come from too little acid not too much), 3) Enhances absorption of calcium, magnesium, and iron, 4) Supports bile flow for fat digestion, 5) Has antimicrobial properties against pathogens. Protocol: 1-2 tablespoons in a large glass of water before meals. Use a straw to protect tooth enamel. Must be raw, unfiltered, with the mother. Not a substitute for dietary changes but a powerful amplifier of other interventions. At roughly 10 cents per day, it is the most cost-effective health intervention available.
