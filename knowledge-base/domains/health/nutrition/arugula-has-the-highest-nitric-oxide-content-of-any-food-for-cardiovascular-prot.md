Nitric oxide is a vasodilator that relaxes arteries lowers blood pressure reduces clotting risk and improves circulation throughout the body. Arugula produces more nitric oxide than any other food. It is also a cruciferous vegetable with anti-cancer properties and is low in oxalates (safe for kidney stone-prone individuals). Best consumed with extra virgin olive oil which enhances phytonutrient extraction. As a salad base it is a direct upgrade from romaine or iceberg lettuce.
