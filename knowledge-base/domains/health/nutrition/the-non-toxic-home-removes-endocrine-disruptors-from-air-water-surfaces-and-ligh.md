Saladino built what he calls 'the healthiest house' by systematically removing: 1) Air toxins — replaced synthetic furniture (off-gassing VOCs/formaldehyde) with solid wood and natural materials, added air purification, 2) Water — whole-house filtration removing chlorine, fluoride, and heavy metals, 3) Kitchen — eliminated Teflon/non-stick (PFAS), plastic containers (BPA/phthalates), replaced with cast iron, stainless steel, and glass, 4) Cleaning products — replaced synthetic cleaners with vinegar, baking soda, and essential oils, 5) Lighting — replaced LED/fluorescent with incandescent and red light panels to preserve circadian rhythm, 6) Personal care — eliminated conventional toothpaste, deodorant, and skincare containing parabens, triclosan, and synthetic fragrances.
