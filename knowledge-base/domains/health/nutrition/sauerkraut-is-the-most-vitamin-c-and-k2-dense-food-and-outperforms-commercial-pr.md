Raw sauerkraut (must be unpasteurized — shelf-stable versions are heated and kill microbes) is a probiotic powerhouse superior to yogurt kefir and bottled probiotics. At 1.9 net carbs per cup it is extremely low carb. Contains vitamin U (S-methylmethionine) which heals stomach and intestinal ulcers by rebuilding the mucous layer. Sulforaphane provides anti-cancer properties and suppresses H. pylori. The fermentation microbes produce vitamin K2 which removes calcium from soft tissues including arteries and joints. Half a cup to one cup daily is sufficient.
