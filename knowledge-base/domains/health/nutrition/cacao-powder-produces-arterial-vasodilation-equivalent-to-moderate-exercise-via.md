Unlike beetroot powder which directly provides nitric oxide, cacao stimulates the body's enzymes to produce more — making the effect cumulative over time. In a study of 50-80 year olds, 14 days of daily cacao consumption produced arterial thickness comparable to people 20-30 years younger. Cacao also reversed arterial stiffness. Key protocol: use raw cacao powder (not roasted cocoa), mix in warm (not boiling) water, consume 2 hours apart from coffee since caffeine vasoconstricts and counteracts the benefit. Can be combined with beetroot powder for synergistic nitric oxide boosting.
