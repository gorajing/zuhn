If drinking coffee: (1) Choose organic, shade-grown, mold-free beans to minimize mycotoxin and pesticide exposure. (2) Never take creatine with coffee -- caffeine may impair creatine absorption; separate by 4+ hours. (3) Supplement extra magnesium to offset caffeine-driven mineral excretion. (4) Restrict to morning hours to protect sleep architecture. (5) Don't count coffee toward daily fluid intake as it's mildly dehydrating.

Saladino personally does not drink coffee, noting that most people's perceived need for it masks underlying fatigue from poor sleep, nutrient deficiencies, or metabolic dysfunction.
