Berg developed kidney stones despite not eating high-oxalate foods because he was severely magnesium deficient. Magnesium controls calcium throughout the body — without enough, calcium binds with oxalates (which the body also produces endogenously, not just from food). Magnesium also prevents muscle spasms, Charlie horses, cramps, and insomnia. Modern water treatment removed magnesium from city water that our ancestors got from well water, creating widespread deficiency. Supplementing magnesium both prevents new stones and can shrink existing ones.
