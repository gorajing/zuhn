ChatGPT and most dietary guides recommend spinach, nuts, beans, and grains as top magnesium sources. However, the magnesium in these foods is largely non-bioavailable. Spinach contains oxalates that bind magnesium, almonds and grains contain phytic acid that chelates it, and beans have similar antinutrient issues.

Superior bioavailable magnesium sources include: meat (25mg/100g, but most people eat 500g+/day), coconut water (50-60mg/cup), milk and orange juice (25-30mg/cup), bananas, and avocados. These foods lack the antinutrients that block absorption. Factors that deplete magnesium include insulin resistance, alcohol, caffeine, proton pump inhibitors, and excessive sweating.
