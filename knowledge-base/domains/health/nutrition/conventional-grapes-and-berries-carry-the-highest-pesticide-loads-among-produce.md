The dirty dozen list identifies produce with the highest pesticide residues when grown conventionally. Grapes and berries consistently rank among the worst. Thick-skinned fruits (avocados, bananas, oranges) are safer to buy conventional because the peel provides a barrier.

Budget strategy for Costco: buy organic for high-pesticide items (berries, grapes, leafy greens), conventional for protected items, grass-fed ground beef in bulk, pasture-raised eggs, and raw cheese. This approach maximizes nutrient density per dollar while minimizing toxin exposure.
