Research increasingly shows that microplastics have infiltrated the food supply, with sea salt ranking among the most contaminated everyday foods. This matters because salt is consumed daily in nearly every meal, creating compounding exposure over time.

The actionable insight is that switching to verified microplastic-free salt sources (like mined mineral salts) is one of the highest-leverage, lowest-effort dietary changes a person can make. Unlike many health optimizations that require lifestyle overhauls, this is a simple product swap with potentially outsized long-term benefit given the emerging research on microplastic health effects.
