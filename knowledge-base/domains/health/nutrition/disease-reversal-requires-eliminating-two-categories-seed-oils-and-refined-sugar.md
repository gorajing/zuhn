The disease reversal blueprint reduces to two core eliminations and one addition. Eliminate: (1) Seed oils, which damage mitochondria through linoleic acid accumulation causing proton leak and reduced ATP. (2) Refined sugar/HFCS, which causes gut dysbiosis and endotoxemia driving inflammatory insulin resistance.

Add: single-ingredient whole foods -- meat, organs, fruit, honey, vegetables, dairy. These provide the nutrients (taurine, creatine, carnosine, vitamins, minerals) that support mitochondrial function, hormone production, and immune health. Combined with adequate sleep, morning sunlight, and regular movement, this addresses the root causes of diabetes, obesity, cardiovascular disease, and many cancers. The solution is simple even though the molecular mechanisms are complex.
