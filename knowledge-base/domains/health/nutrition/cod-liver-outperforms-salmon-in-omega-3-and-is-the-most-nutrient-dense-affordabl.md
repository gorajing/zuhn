Wild-caught salmon provides approximately 2000mg omega-3 per 3oz serving vs cod fish meat at 171mg — but cod LIVER far exceeds salmon. Lab testing confirmed canned products retain their omega-3 despite heat processing. Salmon selenium content naturally binds and removes mercury eliminating contamination concerns. For protein with fat the 80/20 ratio is optimal — lean protein spikes insulin more than protein with its natural fat. Whey protein powder causes the highest insulin spike of any protein source because it contains zero fat.
