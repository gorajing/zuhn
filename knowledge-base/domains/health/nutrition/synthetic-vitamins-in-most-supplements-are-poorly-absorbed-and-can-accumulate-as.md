The 10 vitamins to avoid: 1) Synthetic folic acid (use methylfolate instead — synthetic blocks folate receptors especially in MTHFR mutations), 2) Synthetic vitamin E as dl-alpha-tocopherol (displaces natural mixed tocopherols), 3) Cyanocobalamin B12 (contains cyanide molecule; use methylcobalamin), 4) Calcium supplements without K2 (deposits in arteries not bones), 5) Iron supplements without confirmed deficiency (excess iron is oxidative), 6) Synthetic beta-carotene (linked to increased cancer in smokers), 7) High-dose synthetic vitamin A (liver toxic), 8) Vitamin D without magnesium and K2 (creates calcium issues), 9) Zinc without copper (long-term zinc depletes copper), 10) Fiber supplements (often synthetic, cause bloating; eat real vegetables instead).
