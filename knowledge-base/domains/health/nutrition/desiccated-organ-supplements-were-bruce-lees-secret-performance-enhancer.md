Historical records suggest Bruce Lee supplemented with desiccated organ capsules -- liver, heart, and other organs concentrated into pill form. Organs contain uniquely dense concentrations of nutrients critical for athletic performance: CoQ10 (heart), heme iron and copper (liver), B12, folate, vitamin A, and unique peptides.

This ancestral approach to performance nutrition contrasts with modern synthetic supplement stacks. Desiccated organ capsules provide a practical alternative for those who won't eat whole organs, delivering the same nutrient profile in convenient form. The approach aligns with the broader principle that whole-food animal nutrition provides what synthetic supplements attempt to replicate piecemeal.
