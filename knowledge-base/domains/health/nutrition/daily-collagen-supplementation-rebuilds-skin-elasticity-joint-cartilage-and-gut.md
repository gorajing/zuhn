Collagen makes up 30% of body protein — it is the structural scaffold for skin, joints, tendons, gut lining, and blood vessels. After 25, collagen production drops ~1% per year. Collagen peptide supplements provide glycine, proline, and hydroxyproline in a pre-formed ratio the body can directly use. Studies show: improved skin elasticity and hydration in 4-8 weeks, reduced joint pain in osteoarthritis, and gut lining repair (useful for leaky gut). Best absorbed on an empty stomach with vitamin C (required cofactor for collagen synthesis). Bone broth is a whole-food alternative. Collagen combined with intermittent fasting amplifies both collagen synthesis and autophagy.
