Every recipe in Hormozi's system requires literally zero cooking: Greek yogurt + Crystal Light + cereal is mixed in the container it comes in. Lox goes on pre-toasted bread. Jerky and string cheese are grab-and-go. Even dishes are eliminated by eating from the yogurt container.

This is a behavioral design insight disguised as a recipe video. Most diet advice optimizes for nutritional content while ignoring the actual bottleneck: the activation energy required to prepare food. When preparation friction approaches zero, compliance approaches 100%. Hormozi — a business systems thinker — applies the same principle he uses in business: remove friction from the desired behavior rather than trying to increase motivation to overcome it.
