Inner thigh fat and cellulite are hormonally driven by estrogen dominance. Estrogen promotes fat storage in the thighs and hips. Spot exercises cannot override hormonal signaling. Cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, kale, cabbage, Brussels sprouts) contain DIM (diindolylmethane) which helps the liver metabolize and clear excess estrogen. Combined with intermittent fasting to lower insulin (which also promotes fat storage) and a low-carb diet, this hormonal approach addresses the root cause rather than the symptom.
