Heart Rate Variability breath practice involves breathing through the nose only — inhale for 5.5 seconds, immediately exhale for 5.5 seconds, with no pause between cycles. Continue for 10-20 minutes. The key is the continuous rhythm with no gaps between inhale and exhale.

Bayer reports this creates a parasympathetic reset so effective that some people compare it to pharmaceutical muscle relaxants. It can be done during meetings, in the car, or as a dedicated daily practice. The accessibility makes it one of the highest-ROI biohacking tools — requires zero equipment, zero cost, and can be done anywhere.
