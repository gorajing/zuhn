Wireless earbuds operate on the same radiofrequency band as microwave ovens and cell phones. EMF detectors show comparable microwatts per square meter readings from AirPods when pinging or actively connected as from a microwave oven at a few feet distance. The critical difference is duration and proximity: AirPods sit directly adjacent to the brain for hours.

The two types of EMF to be aware of: RF-EMF (radiofrequency from WiFi, cell phones, Bluetooth) and ELF-EMF (extra low frequency from electrical outlets). Wired headphones eliminate RF exposure to the head. While the long-term effects of chronic RF-EMF exposure are still debated, the precautionary principle suggests minimizing direct brain exposure through wired alternatives.
