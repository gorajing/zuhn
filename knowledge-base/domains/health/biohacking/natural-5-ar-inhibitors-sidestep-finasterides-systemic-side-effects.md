Finasteride's side effects (libido loss, erectile dysfunction) come from its potency and systemic reach — it shuts down DHT body-wide, including in genital tissues where DHT is needed for normal sexual function. Natural 5-AR inhibitors like saw palmetto, pumpkin seed oil, EGCG (green tea), peppermint oil, and rosemary oil have been shown to inhibit 5-alpha reductase but are weaker and often applied topically, creating a more localized effect. The tradeoff: they're less reliably effective than finasteride, but for users who experience or fear finasteride's side effects, they represent a reasonable step in a stacked protocol. The confidence here is low because mechanistic plausibility is strong but head-to-head efficacy data is weak — these should not be framed as equivalents to finasteride, only as safer complements or alternatives when tolerability is paramount.
