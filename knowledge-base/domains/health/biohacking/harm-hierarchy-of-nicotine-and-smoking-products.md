Paul Saladino ranks common nicotine and smoking products by harm level. Cigarettes are worst due to pesticides and additives in commercial tobacco. Vaping carries risks from oils linked to certain types of pneumonia. Marijuana lowers androgens in men and is broadly net-negative for hormonal health.

High-quality cigars with pesticide-free tobacco leaf are considered the least harmful option -- not an endorsement but a relative ranking. Nicotine pouches, while avoiding combustion risks, are highly addictive and function as drugs rather than supplements. None of these products are recommended for regular use.
