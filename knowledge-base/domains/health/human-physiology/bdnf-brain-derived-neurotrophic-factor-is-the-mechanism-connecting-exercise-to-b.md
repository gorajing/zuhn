Big Think presents BDNF as the specific protein-level mechanism. Studies show people doing 3 months of spin class had higher mood states AND more growth factors flowing through their brains than people who played video Scrabble for 3 months. The growth factors metaphor: 'like a watering can up and down your hippocampi.' Even 10 minutes of walking initiates BDNF secretion. The hippocampus and prefrontal cortex are the structures most affected.

Application: for cognitive optimization protocols, exercise is the highest-leverage intervention because the mechanism is specific (BDNF) not generic. Prioritize aerobic exercise that elevates heart rate over passive cognitive interventions (puzzles alone). The order of leverage: regular aerobic exercise > mentally challenging activities > supplements > cognitive training games.
