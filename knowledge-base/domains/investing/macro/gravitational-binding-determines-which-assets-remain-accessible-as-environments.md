The universe's expansion, accelerated by dark energy, means that only gravitationally bound structures — the Local Group — remain accessible. Everything beyond drifts away faster than any conceivable technology could bridge. The limit isn't engineering capability but the fundamental structure of the expanding medium itself.

This maps to any expanding system where connectivity depends on binding strength. In markets, platform ecosystems expand and fragment — only assets within your economic 'gravitational pocket' (your network, your regulatory jurisdiction, your technology stack) remain accessible. In talent markets, as industries expand and specialize, only people within your professional orbit remain recruitable. The strategic implication is to identify your binding forces early and strengthen them before expansion makes unbound elements permanently unreachable.
