The video describes a future where red dwarfs have cooled, white dwarfs have become black dwarfs, and the universe is permanently dark. In this scenario, rotating black holes are the only objects still capable of yielding usable energy through the Penrose process or superradiant scattering. A civilization positioned near one could survive for trillions of years.

This frames an extreme version of resource concentration economics: when all alternative sources are exhausted, the entity controlling the last viable resource holds absolute leverage. The pattern applies at shorter timescales too — as fossil fuels deplete, whoever controls the transition energy infrastructure (nuclear, fusion, renewables) captures disproportionate value. Terminal asset classes don't need to be efficient; they just need to be the only option left.
