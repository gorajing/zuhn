Hart's Fact A is the observation that no alien civilization currently cohabits Earth with us. While we cannot conclusively say a distant exoplanet lacks life, we can be highly confident no alien civilization is on Earth. Even this seemingly weak claim has powerful implications.

Even at current spacecraft speeds (Voyager 1/2), the galaxy could have been colonized many times over during its 13-billion-year history. Since this clearly has not happened, it constrains the possibilities: either galactic civilizations are extremely rare, or they never develop the will or capability for aggressive expansion. This makes Hart's Fact A arguably the strongest empirical data point in the entire field of astrobiology.
