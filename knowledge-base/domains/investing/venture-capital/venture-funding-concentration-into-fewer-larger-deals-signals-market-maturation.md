Venture funding reached $94.6 billion in Q2 2025 for the third consecutive quarter above $90B, representing a 53% increase from 2024. However, the headline number masks a structural shift: capital is flowing into fewer, larger deals rather than being distributed broadly across the startup ecosystem.

AI, hard tech, and defense are absorbing the majority of this capital. This concentration pattern has implications for founders outside these sectors — the fundraising environment may feel tight even as aggregate numbers hit records. For investors, the trend toward larger checks means the bar for meaningful participation in top deals continues to rise.
