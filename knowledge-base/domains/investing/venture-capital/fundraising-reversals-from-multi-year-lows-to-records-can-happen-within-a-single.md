The Q1 2026 data provides a striking example of how quickly private market sentiment and activity can reverse. 2025 was the weakest fundraising year since 2017; Q1 2026 exceeded $80B in a single quarter—more than the entire 2025 US VC total. This compression of cycle reversal into a single quarter reflects several dynamics: pent-up LP demand, a catalyzing narrative (AI), and a handful of marquee closes signaling that the market had reopened. The lesson for founders and fund managers is that waiting for clear trend confirmation means missing the window—the reversal is already priced in by the time it becomes consensus. Early movers who launched fundraises in late 2025 were best positioned.
