CB Insights data shows CVC volume at its lowest since 2018, yet median deal size is up and AI dominates the top deals. Salesforce Ventures leads CVC activity, reinforcing the pattern that corporate investors are being highly selective but writing larger checks when they do deploy—and those checks are overwhelmingly going to AI.

For non-AI founders seeking CVC backing, this data suggests a structural headwind: corporate investors are consolidating around AI bets that have strategic overlap with their own product roadmaps. The opportunity for non-AI sectors is to frame deals as AI-enabling infrastructure rather than standalone plays. For AI founders, the CVC channel is increasingly viable as strategic investors feel competitive urgency to maintain deal access in the category.
