The current VC market is overwhelmingly directing capital toward AI and sustainable energy, effectively starving other sectors of funding. This dual-thesis concentration mirrors previous cycles where consensus bets (social mobile in 2012, crypto in 2021) led to oversupply of capital chasing similar deals, compressing returns.

For founders outside these two categories, the funding environment is hostile. For those inside it, competition for attention is fierce but capital is abundant. The strategic implication is that contrarian bets in neglected sectors may offer better risk-adjusted returns, while the consensus sectors will see inflated valuations and eventual correction.
