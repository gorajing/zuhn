The job listings in this roundup include multiple remote VC positions (Topology Ventures, GetFresh Ventures, Serena, The Aligned Fund) alongside traditional hub-based roles and explicit fellowship programs (Forbion). This wasn't the norm even five years ago when VC recruiting happened almost exclusively through warm intros within SF/NYC/London networks.

This democratization has a compounding effect: as VCs come from more diverse backgrounds and geographies, their deal flow diversifies too. Scout programs effectively turn founders, operators, and domain experts into distributed antenna arrays for the fund. For aspiring VCs, the implication is that building a visible track record of insight in a specific domain (via writing, angel investing, or operator experience) matters more than geographic proximity to Sand Hill Road.
