Dealroom's State of Global VC report identifies the Middle East and Southeast Asia as gaining meaningful traction in 2024, moving beyond the 'emerging market premium' narrative into genuine deal flow centers. This isn't purely about sovereign wealth fund recycling — it reflects growing local startup ecosystems, regulatory modernization, and demographic tailwinds (young, mobile-first populations).

For founders, this means new fundraising geographies to consider and new market-entry opportunities. For investors, it signals that the traditional Silicon Valley → Europe → Rest of World capital flow pattern is fracturing. Bain's Global VC Outlook corroborates this, showing venture funding resilience driven partly by these new geographies alongside sector bets in climate tech and generative AI.
