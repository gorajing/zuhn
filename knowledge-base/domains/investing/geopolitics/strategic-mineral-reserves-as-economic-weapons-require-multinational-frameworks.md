Project Vault's $12B strategic mineral reserve goes beyond simple stockpiling by combining EXIM Bank loans with private capital and signing bilateral critical minerals frameworks with 11 countries simultaneously. This acknowledges that China's 90% dominance in mineral processing cannot be countered by any single nation's reserves alone.

The hybrid model — government-backed loans plus private consortium capital, with multi-national supply frameworks — represents a new template for geopolitical resource competition. Unlike the existing National Defense Stockpile (military-only), Project Vault acts as a commercial shock absorber with price floor guarantees for manufacturers. The FORGE initiative and Pax Silica partnership signal that critical mineral security is being treated as a diplomatic and economic priority, not just a defense one.
