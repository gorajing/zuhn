RFK Jr synthesizes Jefferson ('the tree of liberty must be watered with the blood of each generation') with his father's belief that 'this is not a German problem — we're all inches away from barbarity.' The institutions of democracy are what keep us safe, not our nature. These institutions face constant pressure from forces that chip away at rights.

This has direct application to organizational governance: any system of checks and balances — corporate, governmental, or personal — degrades when not actively maintained. Complacency is the precursor to fragility. The generation that inherits functioning institutions without understanding what maintains them is the generation most likely to lose them.
