Florida's billionaire attraction strategy goes beyond zero state income tax: homestead exemption protects primary residences from creditors (unlimited value), friendly regulatory environment, and political leaders personally courting wealthy relocations. The mayor of Miami personally courted Elon Musk.

This creates a race-to-the-bottom dynamic: states competing for wealthy residents by offering progressively more favorable terms, which reduces the tax base other states need to maintain services. The billionaires benefit from infrastructure funded by previous tax revenue while contributing less to future maintenance.
