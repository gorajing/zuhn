Suleiman documents a systematic pattern: Palestinian casualties are footnotes while Israeli casualties are headlines; daily occupation is invisible until an escalation forces coverage; over 600 Palestinians killed in 2023 alone before October 7th never made Western headlines. The result is an American public that sees conflicts begin at the point of escalation rather than at the root causes.

For anyone evaluating geopolitical situations — investors, entrepreneurs, policy thinkers: the media narrative you receive is almost certainly incomplete. Reading 'beyond the headlines' isn't optional for accurate understanding. When one side controls journalist access, reviews media tapes before publication, and the other side's journalists are killed (100+ in this conflict), the information environment is structurally compromised.
