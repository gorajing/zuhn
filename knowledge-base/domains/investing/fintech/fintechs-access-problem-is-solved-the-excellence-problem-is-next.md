Plaid CEO Zach Perret frames fintech's trajectory in two phases. Phase 1 (2014-2022) solved access: getting loans online, opening bank accounts on mobile, digital remittances. This is largely complete.

Phase 2 is about excellence: credit scoring that reflects actual free cash flow rather than just repayment history, embedded finance everywhere (BNPL, cards, wallets), and AI-powered agentic financial services where getting a mortgage means talking to an AI application because it's the most efficient path. The winners from the boom-bust cycle (Robinhood at $100B, SoFi at $35B, Revolut at $75B) survived by rebundling from point solutions into full-stack financial platforms.
