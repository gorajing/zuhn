Ackman identifies AI as fundamentally changing the investment landscape because it enables rapid disruption of previously stable businesses. Two 19-year-olds can now raise capital, access infinite bandwidth, contract global engineers, and build virtual companies that threaten established incumbents.

His strategy for navigating this: own businesses where you can't foresee AI-driven disruption (restaurants, music rights), or own the AI infrastructure companies themselves at reasonable prices (his Alphabet thesis). The restaurant industry has been his best track record because the physical moat of fresh food supply chains and brand loyalty resists digital disruption in ways that pure software businesses cannot.
