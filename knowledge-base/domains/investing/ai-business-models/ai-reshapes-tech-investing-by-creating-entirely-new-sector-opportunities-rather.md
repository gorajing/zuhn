The traditional approach of evaluating AI companies through the lens of existing tech sectors (SaaS, marketplaces, etc.) misses the fundamental disruption AI creates. AI doesn't just improve existing categories — it creates entirely new ones and collapses others, requiring investors to develop novel evaluation frameworks.

This means investors who simply add 'AI' as a tag to their existing thesis will underperform those who recognize that AI reshapes the entire investment landscape. The shift demands new due diligence approaches focused on data moats, model differentiation, and vertical integration depth rather than traditional SaaS metrics like ARR multiples alone.
