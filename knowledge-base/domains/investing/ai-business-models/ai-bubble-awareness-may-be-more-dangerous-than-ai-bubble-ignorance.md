Bubble awareness creates a dangerous game of musical chairs: investors who 'know' it's a bubble stay invested hoping to exit before the crash, which sustains the bubble longer than ignorance would. When sentiment finally shifts, everyone tries to exit simultaneously because they were all waiting for the same signal.

This is worse than a genuine bubble of ignorance where corrections happen gradually as individuals independently discover overvaluation. Coordinated awareness creates coordinated exits, producing sharper crashes. The 'smart money' strategy of staying invested in a known bubble while planning to exit early fails when everyone has the same plan.
