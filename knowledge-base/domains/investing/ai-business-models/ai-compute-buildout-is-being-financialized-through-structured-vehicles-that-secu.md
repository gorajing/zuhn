xAI assembled a ~$20B financing round where NVIDIA invests ~$2B into an SPV that buys NVIDIA GPUs and leases them back to xAI. The SPV structure provides risk mitigation through asset-backed securitization — debt holders are secured by the physical GPU assets, and the SPV retains ownership after the five-year lease. Simultaneously, OpenAI and AMD structured a partnership using 160 million shares of AMD stock as partial payment for 6 GW of GPU capacity.

This represents a maturation of AI infrastructure funding from simple equity raises to sophisticated financial engineering. The pattern mirrors how telecom and energy infrastructure was financed — through asset-backed structures that separate operational risk from hardware ownership. It also creates vendor lock-in by tying supply cadence to financing milestones, making the chip vendor's roadmap a financial covenant rather than just a product relationship.
