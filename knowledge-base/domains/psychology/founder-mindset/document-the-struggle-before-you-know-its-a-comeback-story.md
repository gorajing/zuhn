Hormozi's biggest regret is not documenting his early struggle because he was ashamed. He compares it to overweight people avoiding photos — avoidance doesn't change reality, it just deprives your future self of the artifact. The actionable frame: if you believe you're going to win, early documentation becomes the asset your later story is built on (Kanye's early footage is his example). If you don't win, the documentation still helps you face reality. Either way, the cost of documenting is near zero and the upside is asymmetric. The default should be 'document everything' with the same discipline as a scientist recording experimental notes.
