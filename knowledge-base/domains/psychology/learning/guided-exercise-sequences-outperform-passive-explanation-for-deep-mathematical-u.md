The pedagogical insight is that there's a ceiling to what passive consumption — even beautifully animated passive consumption — can achieve. The first proof is presented as a visual narrative (satisfying, memorable) while the second is presented as guided exercises (harder, but deeper). By including both approaches in one video, it implicitly demonstrates the spectrum between accessibility and depth. The practical application: when teaching or explaining complex ideas, the optimal format depends on the goal. For intuition-building, narrative works.
