The overview effect is one of psychology's most consistent findings: astronauts from every country, culture, and background report the same cognitive shift upon seeing Earth from space. National borders become invisible. Conflicts appear absurd. The fragility and unity of Earth becomes viscerally obvious.

This isn't just a pleasant feeling - it represents a genuine cognitive restructuring where identity expands from individual/national to planetary. The practical implication is that perspective literally determines what problems you see and what solutions you consider. Most human conflicts are visible only from ground level; they disappear from orbital perspective.
