The physicist cites the standard neuroscience explanation for alien abduction reports: during REM, the prefrontal cortex (rational evaluation) is dampened, the visual cortex is active, and descending motor pathways are inhibited to prevent dream-enactment, leaving the subject immobilized yet experientially vivid. This combination generates a sensed-presence 'intruder' phenomenology that historically manifested as ghost, witch, or old-hag encounters in pre-alien cultures and now manifests as gray-alien encounters in post-1970s American culture. The trauma reported by 'contactees' is real; the explanatory inference to extraterrestrial origin is the part that fails. This is a general principle: when testimony is real but the external cause is missing, look for endogenous neural mechanisms first.
