In photosynthesis, the electron transport chain pumps protons into the thylakoid, creating a concentration gradient. Protons then flow back out through ATP synthase, and that flow generates ATP — the cell's energy currency. The thylakoid is literally charged like a battery.

This gradient-as-stored-energy pattern is universal. Market inefficiencies are information gradients — arbitrageurs extract value by letting information 'flow' to equilibrium. Skill gaps between teams create talent gradients that drive hiring flows. Temperature differences drive heat engines. Recognizing 'where is there a maintained imbalance?' is a reliable way to identify where energy or value can be extracted, and 'what is maintaining this gradient?' reveals what would collapse the opportunity.
