In the light-dependent reactions, the entire point of the electron transport chain is to pump hydrogen ions into the thylakoid, creating a concentration gradient. When protons flow back through ATP synthase, that gradient powers the creation of ATP — the cell's energy currency. The membrane doesn't generate energy; it stores it by maintaining an imbalance.

This gradient model maps cleanly to other domains. Information asymmetry between markets is a gradient that arbitrageurs exploit. A skill gap between what you know and what an industry needs is a gradient that commands premium compensation. Pent-up demand behind a regulatory barrier is a gradient that explodes when deregulation opens the gate. The mental model suggests two strategic questions: where are gradients building up (opportunity detection), and what barriers are about to become permeable (timing).
