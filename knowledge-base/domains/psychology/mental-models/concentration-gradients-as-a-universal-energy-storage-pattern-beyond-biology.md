In photosynthesis, the thylakoid membrane maintains a concentration gradient of protons. When protons flow back through ATP synthase to equalize, that flow drives energy production. The membrane's role is critical: without a barrier maintaining the imbalance, no useful work gets extracted.

This gradient-across-barrier pattern appears everywhere. Information asymmetry in markets creates trading opportunities until the gradient equalizes. Expertise differentials drive consulting value. Attention concentration in media creates influence gradients. The key insight is that energy (or value) isn't in the particles themselves—it's in the maintained imbalance. Systems that can build and selectively release gradients capture more value than those that let equilibrium happen passively.
