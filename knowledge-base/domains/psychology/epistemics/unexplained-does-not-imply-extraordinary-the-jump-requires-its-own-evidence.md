When reviewing UFO reports, the physicist argues that the persistent conflation of two distinct claims — 'I do not know what that was' and 'that was an alien craft' — is the core reasoning error. Most unexplained sightings, when examined, collapse into Venus sightings, aircraft, lens artifacts, or perceptual illusions. The residual genuinely unresolved cases still carry no positive evidence linking them to extraterrestrials. Treat residual mystery as ignorance to be reduced, not as a free pass to the most extravagant explanation on the menu.
