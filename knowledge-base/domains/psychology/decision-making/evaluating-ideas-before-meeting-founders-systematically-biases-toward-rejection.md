When Fred Wilson said his partnership was 'having a debate' about Airbnb, PG immediately pushed back: 'I'd recommend having the debate after meeting them instead of before. We had big doubts about this idea, but they vanished on meeting the guys.' This is a specific procedural insight about how evaluation order affects outcomes.

Abstract idea evaluation activates analytical, critical thinking — the mode that finds flaws. Meeting founders activates social cognition and pattern recognition for competence, determination, and insight. YC's own experience was that the idea seemed weak in the abstract but the founders were so impressive that doubts vanished. By debating first, Fred's partnership anchored on skepticism, making it harder for the founders to overcome that initial frame even in person.
