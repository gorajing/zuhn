RuBisCo is estimated at 40 billion tons on Earth at any given time, making it the most abundant protein on the planet. Plants adopted a pure volume strategy: if each molecule only works half the time, make astronomically more of them. This wasn't a failure of optimization — it was the optimal response given the constraint that no better carbon-fixation enzyme evolved.

This challenges the common bias toward efficiency optimization. In many domains — sales outreach, content creation, early-stage experiments — the winning strategy is high volume of 'good enough' attempts rather than perfecting a single approach. The key condition is that no fundamentally better mechanism is available and the cost of producing more units is lower than the cost of developing a superior unit.
