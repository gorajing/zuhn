The framing here (powerful states = parasympathetic, primal states = sympathetic) maps onto well-documented neuroscience: sympathetic activation prioritizes threat response over exploratory cognition. Bayer's claim is that this creates a mechanical contradiction in grind culture — the physiology of desperate pursuit is the same physiology that removes access to the creative, pattern-matching, opportunity-spotting cognition required to close the gap. The intervention is state-management, not more effort.
