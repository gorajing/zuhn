The energy required to suppress shadow material is substantial and grows over time. What you're most afraid to reveal (ambition, vulnerability, anger, creativity, desire) is exactly what needs expression for psychological wholeness. The fear of these qualities is proportional to their transformative potential.

Shadow integration doesn't mean acting out every suppressed impulse - it means acknowledging these qualities exist, understanding their purpose, and finding constructive expression for them. The person who integrates their shadow doesn't become darker - they become more complete and require less energy to maintain their identity.
