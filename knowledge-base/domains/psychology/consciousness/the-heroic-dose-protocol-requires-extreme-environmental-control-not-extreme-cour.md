The Johns Hopkins heroic dose protocol (30-40mg psilocybin, roughly equivalent to 5g dried mushrooms) produces experiences of profound ego dissolution and mystical-type experiences. Dr. Johnson emphasizes that the safety record of these sessions — zero serious adverse events across hundreds of sessions — is entirely attributable to the clinical framework, not pharmacological safety.

The protocol includes extensive screening (excluding psychosis-prone individuals), multiple preparatory sessions building trust with guides, a controlled physical environment (comfortable room, eyeshades, music), and trained guides who provide reassurance without directing the experience. This reveals that the variable determining safety isn't dose magnitude but environmental control — a principle applicable far beyond psychedelics to any high-intensity intervention where the context of delivery matters more than the intervention itself.
