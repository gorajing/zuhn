In the same MRI study, Samsung users showed no positive neural response to Samsung news — only reverse empathy toward Apple (positive brain response to negative Apple news). Critically, when asked directly, these users did not report the feelings their brain scans revealed.

This dissociation between neural activity and self-report has broad implications beyond marketing. It demonstrates that introspection is an unreliable guide to our actual motivations. People who claim to make purely rational purchasing decisions — or who say they're 'not into brands' — may be just as identity-driven, simply through an anti-brand identity they can't see.
