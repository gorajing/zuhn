Smith describes noticing that his fellow liberals stopped using the word 'liberal' and started saying 'progressive' — initially seeming like a defensive rebranding against Fox News attacks. But the label change actually accompanied (and perhaps enabled) a fundamental shift in values: from tolerance to activism, from equality to equity, from anti-discrimination to institutionalized discrimination. The relabeling created a continuity illusion that prevented members from noticing how far their movement had drifted. This is a potent cognitive bias applicable far beyond politics — in companies, 'pivoting' can disguise mission drift; in personal development, adopting new terminology for your goals can mask that you've abandoned the original intent. The antidote is periodically comparing current positions to original stated values, not just current labels.
