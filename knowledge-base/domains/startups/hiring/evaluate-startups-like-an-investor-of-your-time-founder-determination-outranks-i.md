Graham's advice for choosing between competing startup offers reframes the decision as an investment: you're investing your time, so ask which company you'd buy stock in. The key signal is founder quality, with determination ranked above raw intelligence.

This heuristic cuts through the noise of compensation packages, office perks, and technology stacks. Determined founders navigate pivots, survive downturns, and find product-market fit through sheer persistence. Smart but undetermined founders often quit when the inevitable hard phases arrive. For early employees whose equity upside depends on company survival, this ordering is critical.
