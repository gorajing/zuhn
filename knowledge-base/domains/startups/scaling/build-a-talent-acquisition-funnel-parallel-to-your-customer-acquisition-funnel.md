Service businesses at scale are supply-constrained, not demand-constrained. The same structure used for customer acquisition must be built in parallel for talent acquisition: applicant lead generation, nurture, interviewing (conversion), onboarding, and retention/ascension of employees. You need to know your talent funnel metrics (applicant CTRs, show rates, close rates) as well as you know your customer funnel metrics.

The ROI math makes this obvious: if a salesperson costs $200K/year and generates $1M in gross profit, that's a 5:1 return plus enterprise value multiplier (e.g., 8x). Spending $200K to get $8M in enterprise value is better than any market investment. The best talent is always in the future, not behind you -- continuous recruiting is the unlock.
