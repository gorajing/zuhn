Moddoc discovered counterintuitively that reducing team sizes dramatically increased output. Their senior care vertical was validated and scaled to 50 million won in monthly sales by just 2 people in under 4 months. The broader business unit shrank to nearly one person but generated 3x the revenue.

The founder's explanation: 'What matters about a team isn't the number of members, but rather the team members who have the desire to take ownership and make things work no matter what.' Small teams with full ownership verified hypotheses faster, generated revenue faster, and validated business viability before scaling headcount. Only after proving profitability and scalability did they hire.
