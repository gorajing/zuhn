Legor grew from zero to unicorn valuation ($5.5B) in 2 years, but Junestrand frames this as merely completing the 0-to-1 phase. He describes being 'still on the starting line' because the legal work market is so enormous that 750+ clients and billions in valuation barely scratches the surface.

This mindset—treating unicorn status as the beginning rather than the destination—is what separates companies that plateau from those that reach massive scale. The 1-to-10 journey requires different strategies than 0-to-1 (more sales infrastructure, different product maturity, enterprise relationships), and the 10-to-100 phase requires yet another transformation. Companies that celebrate unicorn status as an achievement rather than a starting point often stall.
