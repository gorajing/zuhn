Every entrepreneur says they want to be AI-first, but most are just using ChatGPT for emails. The prerequisite is being data-first: having complete data on all elements of your business with proper architecture in place. Only then can you add AI layers with reinforcement training to actually automate work.

Hormozi's practical sequence for a service business: (1) Create operating leverage through offshore talent giving existing team 2-3x capacity, (2) Build the data layer capturing all business operations, (3) Layer AI on top to further increase operating leverage, (4) Use freed capacity to sell again. This is the once-in-a-generation move -- equivalent to the cloud computing boom 25 years ago that created the last wave of billionaires.
