Song's philosophy is unambiguous: if you want to be the best company in the world, you have to work the hardest. She draws a direct parallel to athletics and music—elite performers practice non-stop, and tech startups are no different.

EliseAI doubled revenue consistently for four years and passed $100M ARR. The recipe was grinding, not genius. Do all the sales yourself so you understand exactly what customers want. Handle all support so you feel every pain point. Take the 4am shifts because that's what the business needs. The companies that become market leaders aren't the ones with the cleverest strategy—they're the ones that simply outwork everyone else.
