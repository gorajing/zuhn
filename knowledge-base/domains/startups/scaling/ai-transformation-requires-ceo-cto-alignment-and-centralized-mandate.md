The CTO wrote a long email to Jack Dorsey arguing for centralized AI investment and company-wide transformation. Jack flew to Sydney for a two-day deep dive, and they emerged fully aligned. This top-down alignment enabled the subsequent organizational restructuring and cultural change that made AI adoption possible.

The CTO's approach was progressive: starting with 2-5 engineer special projects as bets, then gradually centralizing the org structure, and now having Goose used by the majority of the company. But the foundational requirement was executive alignment and mandate. Without the CEO's conviction and the CTO's technical vision working in concert, the transformation would have been blocked by organizational inertia.
