Voje distinguishes ECP from ICP: a system only compounds if pointed at the right people, and early that's the ECP — sub-segments with burning pain, higher risk tolerance, willing to co-design, not blocked by year-long compliance/procurement. If your eventual ICP is a regulated enterprise they require case studies, testimonials, and pilots before signing, so you can run out of runway while still talking to them. Independently corroborates the KB's pain-severity x willingness-to-pay beachhead matrix and the founder-led, ECP-first market-entry principles.
