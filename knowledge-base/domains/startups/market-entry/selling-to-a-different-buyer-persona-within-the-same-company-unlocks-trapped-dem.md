The entire outsourcing industry was built around a CFO mental model: contract workers save money. This created a race to the bottom on pricing and a self-fulfilling prophecy where only low-value work got outsourced. A.Team recognized that the same companies had a completely different mental model in their CEO and product leadership: they would pay premium prices for exceptional talent that accelerates innovation.

This is a broadly applicable pattern. When an entire market is anchored to one buyer persona's priorities, switching to a different decision-maker within the same organization can completely reposition the value proposition. You're not changing the customer company — you're changing which person's budget and mental model you're selling to. The CFO sees cost; the CEO sees speed and capability. Same supply, radically different willingness to pay and type of engagement.
