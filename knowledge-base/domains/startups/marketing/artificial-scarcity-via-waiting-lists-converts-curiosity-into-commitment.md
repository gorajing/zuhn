Always-available products trigger indifference; gated products trigger curiosity and fear of exclusion. Claire's homepage had no payment button — prospects had to register to join a waiting list, which selected for commitment and amplified demand via social proof as the list grew. The mechanism is psychological: when people cannot act immediately, the threat of missing out replaces the friction of deciding. This works specifically because most SaaS products default to instant purchase; deviating from that norm is itself the signal. Deploy waiting lists for pre-launch or relaunches where you control supply — not for commoditized offerings where friction leaks buyers to competitors.
