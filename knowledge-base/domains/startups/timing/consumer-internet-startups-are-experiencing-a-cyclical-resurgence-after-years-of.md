The venture capital industry has been heavily skewed toward B2B/enterprise for several years, but data on consumer internet unicorn foundings shows clear cyclical patterns. Waves of consumer unicorns cluster around major technology shifts — the web (Amazon, Google), mobile (Uber, Snap), and now AI (ChatGPT).

This cyclical pattern suggests that the current AI wave is re-opening the consumer startup window. Founders considering B2C ideas should note that these windows are time-bounded — the companies that define each wave are typically founded within a 3-5 year period around the enabling technology's emergence. The historical timeline from 1995-2023 shows that consumer unicorns aren't dead, they're cyclical.
