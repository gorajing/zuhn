Pastan: whether you address segments is a business question, not an analytics one. Be opinionated about the specific audience you drive value for and focus ruthlessly on making that experience amazing, then extend outward from champions (a designer brings Framer into an enterprise; then serve the adjacent content/SEO persona). Don't close your eyes and chase TAM. Independently corroborates the KB's focus / rapid-vs-evergreen / do-less-better principles from the activation angle.
