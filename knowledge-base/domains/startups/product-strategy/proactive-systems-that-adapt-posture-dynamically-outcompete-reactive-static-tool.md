Veriti's core insight is that cybersecurity should mirror wartime strategy — the appropriate defensive posture changes based on current threat conditions. Static security tools apply the same rules regardless of context, forcing companies to choose between being over-secured (adding friction to employees and customers) or under-secured (leaving gaps). Dynamic posture management resolves this tradeoff by adjusting in real-time.

This proactive-vs-reactive distinction is a recurring pattern in product strategy. Markets where conditions change rapidly and the cost of being wrong is high create natural demand for systems that adapt autonomously. The same principle applies to fraud detection, supply chain management, and infrastructure scaling. The key product insight is that AI's value is highest not in replacing human decisions but in making continuous micro-adjustments at a speed and frequency that would be impossible manually.
