fidu combines tuition collection, loan programs, communications, and learning management into one platform for schools. This bundling strategy mirrors patterns seen in other vertical SaaS winners: once you handle both the money flow and the operational workflow, you become the system of record that administrators cannot abandon without massive disruption.

The key insight is that financial services (tuition, insurance, loans) create particularly strong lock-in because they involve regulatory compliance, payment integrations, and family relationships that are painful to migrate. When those financial rails are bundled with the day-to-day operational tools teachers and admins already depend on, the switching cost compounds multiplicatively rather than additively.
