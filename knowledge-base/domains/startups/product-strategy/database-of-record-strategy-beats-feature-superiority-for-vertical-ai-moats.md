NFX prescribes a specific architectural strategy for vertical AI companies: build around a database of record — a single source of truth for the industry. While horizontal incumbents may have more data in aggregate, they rarely possess the highest-quality contextual data that delivers genuine insights for each specific vertical. In many industries, the most valuable data is unstructured (which AI can now process) or locked in hard-to-access silos. If acquiring this data requires deep domain expertise, that creates an immediate structural advantage over horizontal players who see the industry 'in black and white' while vertical players see it 'in color.' The plan of attack is to build around that database, improving AI and UX iteratively. However, NFX notes an important caveat: data alone is not an effective long-term moat — the real defensibility comes from the compounding workflow capture enabled by that data foundation.
