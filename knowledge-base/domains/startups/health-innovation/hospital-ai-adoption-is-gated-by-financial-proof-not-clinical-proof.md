Arlene Jones, Director of Pharmacy, described presenting an AI product that would save $3 million annually to her hospital's financial committee three times before gaining approval. The committee kept asking 'why not more?' and demanding additional proof of concept despite clear value.

This reveals that the primary bottleneck for AI in healthcare is not clinical validation or regulatory approval but institutional financial conservatism. Hospitals operate under extreme budget pressure and their technology review boards apply much higher ROI thresholds than other industries. Entrepreneurs targeting hospital systems must build their pitch around financial outcomes rather than clinical improvement, and budget for a multi-quarter sales cycle with repeated presentations to the same committee.
