In a single newsletter issue, health-adjacent raises include Alan ($4.5B valuation, health insurance), Mendaera ($73M, robotic healthcare), Qure.ai ($65M, AI diagnostics), Sabadell Asabys II (€180M health tech fund), and a dedicated PitchBook Digital Health Report. This concentration across insurance, robotics, AI diagnostics, and dedicated fund vehicles suggests the sector isn't riding a hype cycle.

The pattern indicates healthcare digitization operates on infrastructure-rebuild timescales similar to fintech's multi-decade transformation of banking. Founders entering health tech should plan for long deployment cycles but can draw confidence from the breadth of capital committed across the value chain—not just in trendy AI applications but in operational infrastructure like robotic surgery and insurance platforms.
