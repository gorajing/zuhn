The model was trained on 38,650 experimentally tested molecules and judged through wet-lab validation, mechanism work, and infection models. That loop is much harder to copy than the abstract idea of applying AI to drug discovery.

The startup lesson matches Zuhn's standing principle that application-layer data matters more than temporary capability leads. In AI biotech, the defensible asset is the assay-linked data and validation pipeline that compounds with every experiment.
