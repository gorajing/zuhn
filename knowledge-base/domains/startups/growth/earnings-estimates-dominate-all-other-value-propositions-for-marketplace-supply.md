At Airbnb, the earnings estimate was consistently the most effective value proposition for converting visitors into hosts — by an order of magnitude over alternatives. Any time the team hid or obscured it, growth measurably dipped. This insight generalizes beyond Airbnb: when recruiting supply-side participants in any marketplace, the primary question they need answered is 'how much money will I make?' Abstract benefits like community, flexibility, or brand prestige are secondary. The implication is that marketplace builders should invest heavily in making income estimates accurate, prominent, and personalized rather than leading with lifestyle or brand messaging.
