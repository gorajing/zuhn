Geneva University Hospital needed to transform 1,900 beds and 11,000+ employees into a COVID-19 center with days of runway. Using the Team Alignment Map — a visual tool that forces teams to identify objectives, translate them into joint commitments, and confirm resources — the operations department planned the transformation in a single intensive day.

The key mechanism is that visual tools eliminate the ambiguity that slows traditional planning. When you physically write commitments on post-its and assign them on a whiteboard, there's no hiding behind vague agreements. The hospital team identified 3 main challenges, generated 32 concrete tasks, and began executing immediately. Traditional planning processes would have taken weeks of meetings to reach the same clarity.
