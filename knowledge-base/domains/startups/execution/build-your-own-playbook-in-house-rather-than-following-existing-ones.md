Fuse is working on fusion energy technology that nobody has built before. There's no playbook to follow from existing nuclear companies or other startups. JC had to develop methodologies, testing approaches, and commercialization strategies from scratch.

This applies more broadly to any frontier technology domain: the existing playbooks from adjacent industries will be directionally useful but specifically wrong. The founders who succeed in frontier domains are the ones who build their own playbooks through systematic experimentation rather than trying to adapt frameworks from better-understood markets. Fuse published results in Nature—proving their approach works—by developing their own methodology rather than following established nuclear industry practices.
