The entire procrastination system Urban describes has one lever: the Panic Monster. If you can wake it up, you can get things done. The implication for unstructured work is clear — you must build deadline infrastructure that creates genuine consequences.

This means public commitments (tweeting a launch date), financial stakes (non-refundable deposits on conference booths), accountability partners who will actually hold you to commitments, and structured review cycles. The key is that artificial deadlines must carry real consequences — telling yourself 'I'll finish by Friday' doesn't work because the Monkey knows there's no actual threat. The deadline needs teeth.
