Krieger's Stanford education in Symbolic Systems (combining CS, design, philosophy, and psychology) shaped his core product philosophy: everything you build should solve a problem for somebody. This principle guided Instagram's creation and his work at Anthropic.

The lesson extends beyond just 'talk to users'—it's about deeply understanding the context around why software exists. In the AI era, this means not building AI features because they're possible, but because they solve genuine friction in people's lives. The best products emerge when builders understand both the technology and the human context.
