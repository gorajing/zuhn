Zhang's product looked 'really rough' and was built in just two weeks. Within a day, over 100 people were using it. Then users doubled, revenue surged, and they knew they'd hit something. The lesson: in the AI era, things change every day. If you wait too long for perfection, you may never launch.

Creati AI went from launch to 10M downloads and $10M ARR in one year. The speed was possible because they launched before the product was polished, learned from real user behavior, and iterated rapidly. In AI specifically, the underlying models improve constantly, so today's rough product becomes tomorrow's polished one without you having to do anything. Launch early, learn fast, ride the model improvement wave.
