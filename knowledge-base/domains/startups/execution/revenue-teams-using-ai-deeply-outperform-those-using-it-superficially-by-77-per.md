The highest-performing revenue teams in 2025 diverged from the traditional growth playbook of hiring more reps. Instead, they focused on output per rep by deeply integrating AI into their workflows — not just adding chatbot tools, but fundamentally redesigning processes around AI capabilities. Gong's data shows this approach yielded roughly 77% higher revenue per rep compared to average teams.

This has immediate implications for startup resource allocation: investing in AI-native workflow design beats investing in headcount expansion. The key differentiator isn't whether a team uses AI, but how deeply it's woven into the actual selling process. Surface-level adoption (occasional use of AI writing tools) produces marginal gains, while systematic workflow redesign produces step-function improvements.
