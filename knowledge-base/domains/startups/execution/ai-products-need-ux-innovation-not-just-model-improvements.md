The underlying AI capabilities are improving dramatically, but UX is not accelerating at the same rate. This gap between what AI can do and what users can effectively access through the interface is the biggest opportunity in AI product building.

The power drill analogy is apt: nobody buys a drill for the drill—they buy it for the hole. Similarly, nobody uses AI for the model—they use it for the outcome. The companies that win will be those that build interfaces making AI's power accessible and reliable, not those with the most powerful models. This is why chat-only interfaces are insufficient—they put all the burden on the user to figure out how to extract value.
