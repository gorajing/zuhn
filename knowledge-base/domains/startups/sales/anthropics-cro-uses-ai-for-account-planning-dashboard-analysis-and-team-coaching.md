Kate Jensen shares specific ways Anthropic's own sales team uses Claude daily. First, account planning: feeding executive podcasts, published quotes, news mentions, and account context into Claude, then asking 'what should I ask this person? how should I position this?' This instantly elevates meeting preparation quality — the 'lowest common denominator just came up' across the entire team.

Second, visual dashboard analysis: taking screenshots of sales dashboards (all Claude models now have vision) and asking 'what's something interesting from this week that wasn't there last week?' or 'where would you double-click if you were a sales leader looking at this data?' This accelerates the highest-value analytical work.

Third, coaching: managers use call transcripts and email threads to skip 'tell me what's happening in the account' and go straight to 'how did you show up? what can we learn? how do we refresh our training based on this interaction?' This creates rapid iteration cycles.

For anyone interviewing at Anthropic for a sales role, demonstrating fluency in these exact use cases — and showing you already use Claude this way — would immediately signal cultural fit with Kate's team.
