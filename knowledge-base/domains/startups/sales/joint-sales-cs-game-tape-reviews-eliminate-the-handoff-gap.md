Most sales reps have never seen an onboarding call despite selling for years. They have no idea what happens after they submit the deal. Meanwhile, CS teams resent sales for overpromising. This creates a toxic divide that hurts both retention and sales conviction.

Hormozi adopted the software industry practice of joint weekly game tape reviews: both teams on the same call, watching each other's recordings. Sales sees the onboarding process and gains data that makes them more credible in pitches. CS sees why customers arrive with certain expectations and can suggest adjustments. CS even reveals capabilities the sales team didn't know existed ('we could easily do that').

The result is a seamless experience from click to close to onboarding. For enterprise AEs specifically, sitting in on customer success and implementation calls for your accounts builds the knowledge that separates mediocre reps from trusted advisors who can speak to the full customer journey.
