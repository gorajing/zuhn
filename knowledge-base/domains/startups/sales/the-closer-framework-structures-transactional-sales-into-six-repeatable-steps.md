Hormozi's CLOSER framework is designed for one-call or two-call closes but the principles apply to any sales conversation. Clarify forces the prospect to articulate why they showed up (reminding them of their own motivation). Label establishes the gap between current state and desired state. Overview past pain uses the deprivation principle -- expanding awareness of what they've already tried and failed at to increase urgency.

Sell the vacation is the key insight: you pitch the destination (Maui), not the journey (TSA, turbulence, bad food). The three-pillar pitch should take only 2 minutes -- 90% of the conversation should be about the prospect's pain, not your product. Explain away concerns is objection handling, trained back-to-front so closers master payment collection and the ask before learning discovery.

For enterprise sales specifically, this framework maps to: qualify the pain (Clarify/Label), build the business case around cost of inaction (Overview pain), demo the transformation not the features (Sell vacation), and handle procurement objections (Explain concerns).
