When prospects ask for a free trial, Bosmeny recommends reframing: 'We don't do free trials, but we do annual agreements where you can opt out in the first 30-60 days if you're not happy.' This subtle change is night-and-day different in outcomes.

Free trials give you none of what a startup needs — no revenue, no validation, no real commitment. At the end of the trial, you have to sell them all over again from scratch. The cancellable annual agreement gives the prospect the same safety net they wanted, but gives you actual revenue, a signed commitment, and real usage data. It also filters for serious buyers — someone unwilling to sign even a cancellable agreement was never going to convert from a free trial either.
