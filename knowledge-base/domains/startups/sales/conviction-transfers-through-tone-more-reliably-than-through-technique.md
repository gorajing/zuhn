Hormozi discovered that his gym's sales team had absurdly high close rates on one specific day: weigh-out day, when customers came in to show their transformation results. After watching 20 people lose 20-30 pounds, the sales reps had absolute conviction that the program worked. That conviction transferred to every prospect they spoke to.

The lesson: you can drill tone, emphasis, and pacing (and you should -- scripts should have underline for 'slow down,' caps for 'emphasize,' italics for 'create space'). But technique without conviction sounds hollow. Reading testimonials, witnessing customer wins, and understanding the full customer journey builds the belief that makes technique automatic.

For anyone entering enterprise sales: genuine conviction in your product is table stakes. If you don't believe your solution actually solves the customer's problem better than alternatives, your tone will betray you regardless of how polished your talk track is. Choose companies and products you can authentically champion.
