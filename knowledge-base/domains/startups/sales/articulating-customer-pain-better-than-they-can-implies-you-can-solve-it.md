Hormozi presents a specific research method: extract reviews from books in your niche and pull quotes that articulate customer pain in their own language. The principle is that 'pain and persuasion only exist in the specific, never the vague.' Generic problem descriptions ('struggling with your business?') have zero persuasive power compared to specific articulations that make the prospect think 'that's exactly my situation.'

This is a fundamentally different approach from benefit-led selling. Instead of promising outcomes, you demonstrate understanding. The mechanism is trust-by-empathy: precise problem articulation signals deep domain expertise, which creates an implied guarantee that you've seen this problem enough times to solve it. With modern AI tools, this research process — mining reviews, forums, and customer conversations for exact language — can be dramatically accelerated, making this tactic more accessible than ever.
