Hormozi argues that 'latency beats magnitude 7 days a week and twice on Sunday.' Wealthy customers won't be motivated by saving money but will pay dramatically more for speed: priority response, first access to new offerings, pulling someone off another job to serve them immediately.

The tactical application is to take your current delivery timeline and cut it by 50-66% for the premium tier. Then systematically map every friction point in the customer journey and eliminate them one by one — this is 'reverse engineering ease.' The premium price funds paying vendors and partners more to prioritize your customers, creating a self-reinforcing competitive advantage that compounds over time.
