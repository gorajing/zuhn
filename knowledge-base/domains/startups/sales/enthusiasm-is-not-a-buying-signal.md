Copeland: the most common mistake isn't altitude, it's qualification — teams are excited others want to buy and don't pressure-test it. Enthusiasm is not the same as reason to buy; without a solvable pain that is painful enough to solve now, you'll just have pleasant conversations with people who love talking about your product and nothing closes. Sharpens the KB's qualification-discipline and regime-dependent (which buyer/when) judgment.
