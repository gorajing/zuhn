Hormozi identifies a specific hack: extract reviews from books in your niche to find the exact quotes and language customers use to describe their pain. When your marketing mirrors their internal monologue, it creates an instant credibility transfer — 'pain and persuasion only exist in the specific, never the vague.'

This is distinct from generic empathy statements. The technique requires finding the precise vocabulary, metaphors, and emotional framing that actual customers use, then deploying those in your sales copy. The AI application is using tools to bulk-extract and analyze review language at scale, making this technique dramatically more accessible than manual research.
