Michelin's fleet management approach demonstrates the principle: CEOs care about cost reduction and profitability, fleet managers care about compliance and operational efficiency, and truck drivers care about safety and fuel consumption. A single pitch deck cannot serve all three.

The critical strategic question is sequencing—which stakeholder do you validate with first? Many B2B startups fail by perfecting solutions for end users who have zero purchasing authority. The playbook says to identify which stakeholder has the most critical influence on the buying decision and test with them first, even if end users are easier to access.
