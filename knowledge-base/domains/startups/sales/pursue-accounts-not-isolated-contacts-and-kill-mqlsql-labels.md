Kramer: we have the data now, so stop waiting for inbound and net-fishing — do account research, niche the audience, and systematically pursue fitting accounts; attribution matters less than whether they engaged with your content and what activities happened. Because it's a multi-person sale, contacts-in-isolation thinking fails. MQL/SQL labels create unnecessary territorialism and a false linear handoff; they're milestones, not handoffs, and outbound now happens early from SDRs, GTM engineers, and marketing at once. Strongly corroborates the KB's 'targeting beats copy / accounts and intent over volume' principles (e.g., the 60/20/20 targeting rule).
