Most brands treat product quality as a checkbox rather than a competitive advantage. In 2026, this is fatal: customers review everything instantly, graders and reviewers will call out bad products publicly, and on Amazon, poor reviews destroy rankings permanently. The subscription business model only works if the product actually delivers — no one keeps auto-ordering something that does not work.

The founder of Physicians Choice takes close to a hundred supplements daily and has clinical gut health test results described by doctors as the best they have ever seen — the result of years of taking his own products. This level of authenticity — genuinely using and believing in what you sell — is not just marketing. It ensures the founder has a personal stake in product quality that goes beyond business metrics. The principle is simple: if you would not take your own product every day, you probably should not be selling it. Marketing can drive first purchases, but only product quality drives the repeat purchases and reviews that sustain a brand.
