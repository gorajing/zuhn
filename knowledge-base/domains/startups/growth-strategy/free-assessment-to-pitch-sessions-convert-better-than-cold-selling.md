The free session isn't charity — it's a structured sales process. You open with a diagnostic to understand the student's current level, deliver one concrete win so they feel progress, then transition: 'This is how I teach. Did you like that?' The prospect has already experienced the value, making the close natural rather than adversarial.

After the free cohort (5-10 students), you extract three assets: honest feedback to improve your method, testimonials for social proof, and referrals to fill paid slots. The testimonials become content that the students themselves post, creating organic reach. This flywheel — free session → testimonial → referral → paid students — is a zero-capital acquisition engine that works especially well for services where the transformation is visible and shareable.
