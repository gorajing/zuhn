Kevin Kelly's 1,000 True Fans thesis received concrete validation through Lenny's newsletter trajectory. He launched paid subscriptions and reached 450 paid subscribers within six weeks (~$56K ARR), crossed 1,000 paid at about 4 months, and by year-end had over 3,000 paid subscribers earning more than his PM Lead salary at Airbnb.

The economics are instructive: at Substack's typical $10/month price point, 1,000 paid subscribers generates ~$120K ARR before platform fees. This demonstrates that for high-expertise creators in B2B niches, the true fans threshold for full financial independence is achievable and the surplus from fans beyond 1,000 creates genuine wealth. The key enabler was Substack's infrastructure removing all the friction of payments, delivery, and subscriber management.
