The authors describe their strategy as 'simple but really hard: we create and find the best resources for founders and VCs worldwide and give them before anyone else — always trying to add value.' This give-first approach, maintained consistently across multiple channels, built an audience of 200k+ subscribers and generated the social proof and trust needed to get into competitive deal flow.

The principle at work is compounding reciprocity. Each piece of valuable content creates a small trust deposit with each reader. Over time, these deposits compound into a relationship where the audience actively wants to help the creators succeed — whether by sharing deals, making introductions, or co-investing. This is fundamentally different from transactional networking because the value delivery happens publicly and at scale, creating social proof that multiplies the trust effect beyond direct recipients.
