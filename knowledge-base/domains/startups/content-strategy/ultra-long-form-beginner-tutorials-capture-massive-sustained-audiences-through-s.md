This 4.5-hour video has accumulated 8.9 million views since 2018 — roughly 1.1M views per year sustained over 8 years. This longevity comes from search dominance: when someone searches 'C# full course for beginners,' a single comprehensive result wins over a 20-part playlist because it requires zero navigation decisions from the viewer.

For content creators and developer advocates, this suggests a counterintuitive strategy: invest heavily in one definitive resource rather than spreading effort across many smaller pieces. The compounding SEO value of a single authoritative URL outweighs the theoretical benefits of a modular series. This applies to documentation, blog posts, and any educational content — the comprehensive single resource often wins.
