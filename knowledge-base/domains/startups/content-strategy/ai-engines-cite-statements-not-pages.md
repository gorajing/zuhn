Traditional SEO optimizes at the page level: meta titles, backlinks, domain authority, keyword density. AI answer engines operate differently — they scan for discrete, citable claims that directly answer a query and can be attributed. A well-structured paragraph with a clear factual assertion is more valuable to AI citation than a comprehensive but meandering article.

This shifts the craft of content writing from breadth-and-authority toward precision-and-directness. Each section of a post should function as a standalone answer to a probable question. FAQ structures, numbered lists, and tightly scoped definitions all improve AI citability because they make the extractable statement obvious. This is a durable principle that applies regardless of which AI engine dominates.
