Coconote tested multiple approaches to save users during the cancellation
flow: discount offers, 3-month subscription pauses, and trial extensions.
The trial extension — simply asking "Do you just need more time?" —
retained the most users by a significant margin. It works for two
reasons. First, it keeps users on auto-renew rather than training them
to expect discounts (which creates a perverse incentive to cancel for
a deal). Second, it addresses the real reason many users cancel: they
have not used the product enough yet to feel the value, not that they
do not want it. Giving more time lets them experience the value
organically. The 3-month pause and discount offers both underperformed
because they either removed the user from the product entirely or
eroded willingness to pay full price.
