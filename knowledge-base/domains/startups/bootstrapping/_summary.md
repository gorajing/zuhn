# Topic: bootstrapping

> 6 insights

- `INS-260320-20F2` [very_high] Momentum is oxygen for a startup — get traction before perfecting anything. Ship a basic product that "sort of works" and charge from day one. 45 days to $100K ARR, 4 months to $1M, 5 months to $2M. Momentum compounds — more capital, more confidence, more investment.
- `INS-260320-B7DA` [very_high] Charge premium from day one — $99/year for a student app when conventional wisdom said they won't pay. Premium pricing signals reliability and trust. Raising from $99 to $129 actually increased BOTH users and revenue.
- `INS-260320-00C2` [very_high] Trial extensions beat discounts for saving cancellations — "Do you just need more time?" retained the most users. Keeps users on auto-renew without training them to expect discounts. Outperformed both discount offers and 3-month pause options.
- `INS-260320-8688` [high] No premature optimization — don't worry about LLM costs or unit economics until you have the growth problem. Used startup credits early, optimized later. Almost everything gets cheaper at scale. Don't let cost anxiety prevent growth.
- `INS-260320-E044` [high] Protect early ideas from smart skeptics — conventional wisdom about what won't work is based on different products. Imagine your idea like a seed — shelter it from harm so it can sprout. Better framework: what happens IF it works?
- `INS-260320-39A3` [high] Keep acquisition talks secret — shelter your team and family from signal-to-noise until the wire hits. Deals fall apart in the last week — premature sharing creates damaging noise. A great leader shelters others from unnecessary anxiety.
