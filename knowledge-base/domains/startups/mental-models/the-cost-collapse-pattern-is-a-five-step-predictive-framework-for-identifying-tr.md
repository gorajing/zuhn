The pattern runs: (1) an enabling technology makes movement dramatically cheaper, (2) unit economics shift by an order of magnitude, (3) access democratizes from elites to masses, (4) unpredicted industries and institutions emerge, (5) geographic power reshuffles as incumbents who assume permanence lose their lead. This sequence played out with ocean sail, railroads, automobiles, and containerization.

The framework's practical value lies in steps 3-5. Once you identify a cost collapse in progress (step 2), you can predict that democratization will follow and that the most valuable opportunities will be in unpredicted second-order effects. This means positioning in infrastructure and enabling layers rather than trying to predict specific applications. The framework also warns that current leaders aren't guaranteed to win — they must build durable institutional moats during their window of advantage.
