The speaker claims middle management is over because its core job, coordination, is exactly what AI should now do; AI-native companies are built from individual contributors (builders, operators) plus directly-responsible individuals — a single named human per outcome, never a committee or group. He notes this is bleeding-edge and unproven ('I'm not sure anyone has a truly self-improving company in every function yet'). A falsifiable org-design claim about AI-native structure to track against future evidence.
