Moddoc accumulated 2 million MAU and hundreds of thousands of verified reviews, but revenue remained in the tens of millions of won. The founder realized the problem: when asked 'who is your customer,' he could only answer 'everyone in South Korea.' The persona was too broad to drive paying behavior.

The breakthrough came when they noticed ophthalmology reviews were growing disproportionately fast. By going deep into one vertical -- understanding the specific lens types (Clareon, Fenopix), the massive price variance (800K to 10M won for cataract surgery), and creating comparison tools -- they created density that converted. Revenue went from 1B won in 2023 to 7B in 2025. Expansion is easy; making it dense is what's hard.
