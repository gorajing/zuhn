Banu built SiteGPT from idea to launch in 2 weeks and hit $10K MRR in the first month. His advice: don't spend months perfecting before launch. Ship with just the core feature, then let users tell you what to build next. This approach worked so well that his side project outgrew his main product (Feather at $6K MRR), which he then sold for $250K to focus entirely on SiteGPT.

The pattern: rapid launch -> validate demand -> let traction guide focus. Months of pre-launch building is often just fear of market feedback disguised as perfectionism.
