Oh Jeong-hun's journey from selling golf balls (failed — kicked out of communities for self-promotion) to selling frozen dumplings (succeeded — organic audience on Kakao Story/Naver Band) demonstrates the 'pre-prototype' concept from The Law of Fire. The key insight: he didn't test the product, he tested the demand hypothesis using minimal resources.

The same principle applied to his housing business: before building a platform, he operated a single share house and received 30 inquiries before opening, with 10 wanting contracts sight unseen. This small test proved the hypothesis. If the hypothesis is wrong, no amount of product quality, sales, or marketing can save the business — so testing the hypothesis must come before everything else.
