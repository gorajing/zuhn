The Portfolio Map prototype includes visual annotations for trends and trajectories of each business model — whether it's growing, shrinking, or shifting position on the two axes. This seemingly simple addition addresses a critical failure mode in strategic planning: the inability to detect gradual decline.

Kodak's analog film business didn't collapse overnight. It slowly migrated from the top-right (profitable + sustainable) to the top-left (profitable but disruption-prone) over years. Without explicit trajectory mapping, the high profitability masked the leftward drift. By the time the disruption became obvious, it was too late to renovate the model. Regular portfolio reviews with trajectory annotations create an early warning system that financial metrics alone cannot provide.
