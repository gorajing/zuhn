Biotech investing sentiment swings between favoring platforms (early 2020s, low rates) and favoring assets (post-2022, high rates). Companies that restructure to chase current sentiment waste time and confuse their narrative. The better approach is positioning on the platform-asset continuum in a way that naturally satisfies both camps.

This is a generalizable principle: in any industry with cyclical investor preferences, the winning strategy is building genuine structural advantages that tell a compelling story in any market environment. Platform companies that can point to clinical progress satisfy asset-focused investors, while their optionality and pipeline satisfy platform-focused ones. The pendulum becomes irrelevant when your fundamentals speak to both sides.
