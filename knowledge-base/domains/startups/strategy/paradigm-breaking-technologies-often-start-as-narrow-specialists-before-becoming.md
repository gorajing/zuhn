The video explicitly notes that quantum computers 'will probably not replace our home computers' but are vastly superior in specific areas: database search (Grover's algorithm provides square-root speedup), breaking encryption (Shor's algorithm), and quantum simulation of molecules. This mirrors the historical pattern of transformative technologies: electricity started powering specific industrial machinery before becoming universal infrastructure; GPUs started rendering graphics before becoming the backbone of AI training. For startup strategy, this means the highest-value quantum plays in the near term are vertical applications in the domains where quantum advantage is proven, not horizontal platform plays. The 'will it be a specialized tool or a revolution?' framing itself reveals a false dichotomy — it's typically both, sequentially.
