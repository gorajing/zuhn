Six competing landing page tools all market themselves on speed of setup — minutes, not days. This convergence means the technical barrier to running a demand test has effectively disappeared. Any founder can have a landing page live within an hour.

The implication is that validation failure is now almost entirely a function of offer design, copywriting, and traffic acquisition rather than technical ability. Teams that treat landing page experiments as a 'build the page' task miss the point — the hard work is crafting a compelling value proposition and driving the right traffic to it. This shifts the required skillset for early-stage validation from engineering toward marketing and positioning.
