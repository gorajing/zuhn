Traditional lean startup methodology preaches build-measure-learn, but the 'build' step still took weeks to months even for an MVP. AI app builders collapse this to minutes — you can generate a functional, shareable prototype in 10-15 minutes and put it in front of real users the same day.

This doesn't just speed up the existing cycle; it fundamentally changes what's rational. When prototyping costs 3 months, you optimize by planning extensively before building. When it costs 15 minutes, the rational strategy inverts: build five different versions and test all of them rather than carefully planning one. The cost of being wrong drops so dramatically that extensive upfront planning becomes wasteful compared to rapid empirical testing.
