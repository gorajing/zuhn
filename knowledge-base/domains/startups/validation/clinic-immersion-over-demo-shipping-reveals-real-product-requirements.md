The founder deliberately delayed launch for four months of clinic immersion across four sites despite investor pressure to generate revenue. This immersion revealed that providers were burned out on AI point solutions and deeply skeptical — they'd seen the same demos recycled since 2016. By switching from pitching to listening and involving providers in the design process via design thinking, the most resistant staff became the biggest champions. The key insight is that in high-trust domains like healthcare, the gap between a working demo and a production-ready product is enormous, and only sustained field observation closes it. Investors pushing for speed would have produced another failed point solution.
