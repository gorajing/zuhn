The article describes founders using ChatGPT to scan thousands of posts across Reddit, product reviews, and forums to identify recurring pain points—like freelancers complaining about poor mobile features or hidden fees in project management tools. This represents a fundamental shift in market validation methodology.

Traditional customer discovery relies on interviews with dozens of people, introducing selection bias and small sample sizes. AI-powered review mining can process thousands of data points in minutes, surfacing patterns that would take months of manual research. The key tactical move is using structured prompts like 'What do [target users] gripe about with [existing tools]?' to extract specific, actionable frustrations that become product specifications. The risk is that AI may over-index on vocal minorities, so founders should still validate findings with direct customer conversations.
