The graduated pricing model solves multiple problems simultaneously. Starting free removes all buyer friction, letting you acquire your first customers purely on the strength of your offer. Each batch of five customers at a higher price point tests willingness to pay while your service quality improves from accumulated feedback. The transition signal is referrals — when free customers start sending friends, that's market validation that you're good enough to charge. This approach also creates genuine scarcity once free and discounted slots fill up, giving you ethical leverage to charge premium rates. The framework continues past full price into premium territory because there are no rules — if you're delivering 10x value, you can charge accordingly.
