YC funded Airbnb despite thinking it was a bad idea — purely because they liked the founders. This wasn't an anomaly but a systematic approach: at the pre-product stage, there is no product to evaluate and no growth numbers to analyze, so founder quality is the only available signal. This has a counterintuitive implication for founders seeking early funding: optimizing your pitch deck or TAM slide matters far less than demonstrating the personal qualities investors are pattern-matching against — determination, authenticity, and intellectual honesty. For investors, it means building deep expertise in reading people is more valuable than developing sector expertise at the seed stage.
