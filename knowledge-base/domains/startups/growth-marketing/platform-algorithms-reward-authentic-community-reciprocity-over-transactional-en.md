The Product Hunt ecosystem operates on reciprocity — makers who participate in discussions, upvote products they genuinely like, and offer feedback to others for at least 30 days before their launch earn organic support when it's their turn. The algorithm favors engagement from seasoned accounts, meaning last-minute account creation by supporters provides diminished returns.

This mirrors a broader pattern in platform-based growth: algorithms increasingly detect and reward authentic participation over transactional behavior. Cold-messaging influencers the night before a launch is far less effective than weeks of genuine engagement with their content. The same principle applies to beta users — those who've had genuinely good experiences become natural advocates without needing to be 'activated.'
