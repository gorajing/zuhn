Elena Verna (Head of Growth, Lovable) says PLG is resurgent because AI compresses activation to a prompt box, so you must put a lot of product in people's hands upfront — even CISOs/CTOs evaluate by using it. AI makes trials genuinely expensive (no more near-zero SaaS margins), so the discipline is to reclassify product usage as a marketing expense, not minimize it. Lovable gives away millions in software yearly (free for any hackathon, no requirements) and reports it as their best channel by far. Corroborates the KB principle that product-led adoption and owned-experience beat rented attention, with a concrete operator data point.
