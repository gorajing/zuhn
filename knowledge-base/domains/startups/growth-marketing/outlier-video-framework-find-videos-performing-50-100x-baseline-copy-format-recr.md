Sandy Lee's exact growth system that took her from 200 to 12,000+ subscribers in a month: (1) Install Vid IQ Chrome extension. It surfaces an 'outlier score' for every video — how much above the creator's average performance that specific video is performing. A video at 50x or 100x the creator's normal performance is a strong signal that the FORMAT is working (since the creator's other content variables are roughly constant, the outlier signals what made THIS video resonate). (2) Set up daily-email alerts for new outliers in your niche. Sandy gets a daily email: 'this video is popping up, you got to learn from this video.' (3) Have AI analyze the outlier: title pattern, thumbnail composition (face position, color, text), hook structure (Sandy's framework breaks hooks into 7 elements: pattern interruption, mirror to viewer, reveal opportunity, expose gaps, promise transformation, authority establishment, transition), body framework. (4) Click 'generate your own version' — AI produces a script/thumbnail/teleprompter preserving the working format but expressing your voice. (5) Repeat daily. Two extensions of the framework: (a) Read comments on outlier videos — these surface specific viewer questions/gaps the outlier creator didn't address, which become your differentiation angle. Sandy got her highest-performing video from a comment gap: 'creators talk about Claude websites but don't explain HOW TO MAKE MONEY with them.' (b) Use your OWN past outliers as inspiration too — what worked for you last month tells you what will work next month. The general principle for any growth motion: don't ideate from scratch. Identify performance outliers in your domain, analyze why they work, then produce your authentic version. This applies to content (YouTube, blog posts, LinkedIn), sales (high-conversion email patterns, demo structures), product (high-engagement UI patterns), and design (high-conversion landing page structures).
