Social media has shifted from a follower graph (you see posts from people
you follow) to an interest graph (the algorithm surfaces content it
predicts you'll engage with). This means a creator with zero followers
can post a TikTok that reaches 8 million views and drives 150,000
signups — which is exactly what happened with Coconote. The implication
for growth strategy is profound: stop optimizing for follower count and
start optimizing for content quality and hook strength. A small creator
who makes great content will outperform a large creator who makes
mediocre content every time on the for-you page.
