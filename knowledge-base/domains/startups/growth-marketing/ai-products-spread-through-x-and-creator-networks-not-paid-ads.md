Paid customer acquisition has become expensive and competitive enough that AI startups now follow a different distribution path: first the product shows up in a small AI community on X, then AI news accounts pick it up, then the buzz cascades into Instagram and creator platforms where creators organically use the tool and generate content about it. This is cheaper, higher-signal, and self-reinforcing in ways paid ads are not. Founders who budget for paid ads before they have landed an X moment are inverting the funnel — they should instead optimize the product for shareability and launch in the right community first.
