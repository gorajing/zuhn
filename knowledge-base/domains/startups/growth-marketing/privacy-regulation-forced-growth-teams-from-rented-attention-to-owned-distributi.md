Apple's App Tracking Transparency alone cost Meta $10B in ad revenue in 2022 by destroying signal quality for lookalike audiences and attribution. Cookie deprecation completed the shift. For startups, this meant renting attention through paid channels became dramatically more expensive and less predictable.

The structural consequence is that growth teams can no longer rely on third-party tracking, lookalike audiences, or attribution hacks. Sustainable growth now requires first-party data collection, real engagement loops, and product-embedded distribution. This isn't a temporary disruption — it's a permanent regime change in growth economics. Teams that still budget primarily for paid acquisition are fighting against a structural headwind that will only intensify as privacy regulation expands globally.
