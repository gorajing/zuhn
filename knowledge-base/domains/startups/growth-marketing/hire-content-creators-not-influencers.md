The alpha in creator-driven growth is working directly with individual
creators, not agency-represented influencers. Look for creators with
Gmail addresses in their bios and 5-10K followers with strong engagement
ratios. Once a creator signs with an agency, their rates inflate to
market price and eliminate the cost arbitrage you need. Coconote built
its entire $6.7M ARR growth engine on this approach — a tight roster of
small, talented creators who could produce authentic, high-converting
short-form content at a fraction of what agency talent would cost.
