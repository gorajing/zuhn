Andrews challenges the assumption that founders must build personal brands: not every founder needs one, and resisting it is fine. The alternative is empowering employees as creators. Curry's (UK electronics retailer) had the CEO publicly attribute Q4 revenue increases directly to employee presence on TikTok. DoorDash similarly leveraged employee storytelling. Andrews herself doesn't know who the Curry's CEO is — but she knows the brand and its employees. The principle: people build trust with people, not logos. If the founder is uncomfortable on camera, find someone in the organization who isn't. The result is authentic storytelling that builds connection and trust while the founder stays focused on building the product.
