The best marketing copy is not written by marketers — it comes from
your customers. Coconote systematically asked users how they would
describe the product to a friend. The overwhelming majority said
some variation of "never miss a key detail." They took that exact
phrasing and put it on their App Store listing. This approach works
because customers describe benefits in language that resonates with
other customers. Internal teams tend to describe features or use
jargon. When you listen to how customers describe you and then say
it back to prospects, you are speaking their language. The practice
is simple: survey users, look for repeated phrases, and use them
verbatim in your most important conversion surfaces.
