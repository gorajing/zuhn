The four channel categories — inbound, outbound, paid, and partnerships — have different risk profiles at different stages. Paid channels pre-PMF burn cash testing messaging on an audience you haven't validated. Inbound and outbound channels, while slower, generate direct customer feedback that refines your positioning and ICP simultaneously.

The sequencing matters: start with 1-3 channels where you see early organic signals (people finding you without paid promotion), test and refine messaging there, then double down on winners before adding paid amplification. This approach means your paid spend is informed by proven messaging and validated audience segments rather than hypotheses. The framework categorizes channels into inbound (content, SEO, community), outbound (cold outreach, sales), paid (ads, sponsorships), and partnerships (integrations, co-marketing) — each with different feedback loops and capital requirements.
