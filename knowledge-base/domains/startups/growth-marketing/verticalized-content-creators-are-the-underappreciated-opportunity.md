Cheung points out that everyone talks about verticalized AI SaaS (AI for law, AI for accounting) but nobody talks about verticalized content creators. Yet The Rundown AI grew to 2M+ subscribers with 50% open rates by becoming the definitive source for AI news—not tech news broadly, not startup news, specifically AI.

The strategy: become the go-to person in a specific niche that's moving too fast for generalists to cover. Cheung became the 'newsletter guy' which gave him access to interviews with Mark Zuckerberg and Sam Altman despite having no journalism background. The moat isn't expertise—it's consistent, high-signal content delivery in a domain where the audience can't keep up on their own.
