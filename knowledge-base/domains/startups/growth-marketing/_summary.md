# Topic: growth-marketing

> 8 insights

- `INS-260320-446F` [high] Hire content creators, not influencers — if their email is at an agency, you're too late. The alpha is in working directly with individual creators (Gmail in bio, 5-10K followers, good engagement). Agencies eliminate the arbitrage you're trying to capture.
- `INS-260320-A467` [high] Follower count doesn't matter in short-form video — only content quality matters. After social media switched from follower graph to interest graph (algorithmic for-you page), a TikTok with zero followers can get 8M views and drive 150K signups.
- `INS-260320-630D` [very_high] Frame your product as a solution to a problem, not a novel toy — toys don't convert. A video framing Coconote as a fun toy got 41M views and 4.5M likes but terrible conversion. Videos positioning it as a study solution converted far better.
- `INS-260320-080B` [very_high] Use your customers' own words as marketing copy — ask how they describe your product. The majority said "never miss a key detail" so they put exactly that on the App Store. Listen to how customers describe you, then say it back.
- `INS-260320-A1D3` [high] Build Navy SEALs, not the Navy — keep your content team small, tight, and hands-on. 5-10 content creators max, meet with each 1:1, share what converts vs what doesn't. A large team dilutes quality; a small elite team compounds.
- `INS-260320-70FA` [high] Marketing to the buyer, not just the user — target the mom, not the student. Students use Coconote but parents often pay. Early viral content was "my mom just changed my life" — other moms seeing it would buy it for their kids.
- `INS-260320-DADA` [high] You can't pour fuel on organic fire — viral UGC doesn't automatically work as paid ads. A separate clip agency creating performance-specific creatives outperformed repurposing organic viral videos. Organic and paid are different channels.
- `INS-260320-1B38` [high] Double your onboarding length — 15 screens increased trial starts by 16%. Longer onboarding (more investment, personalization, social proof) beats short onboarding. Move login AFTER the paywall — having login first caused 10% drop-off.
