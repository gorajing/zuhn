Bayer's Catalyst Fund team co-created a data privacy and business model development framework with their legal and compliance colleagues. This meant innovation teams could run projects within pre-approved guardrails rather than negotiating with legal departments from scratch for every new initiative.

This addresses a hidden bottleneck that most innovation programs ignore: legal and compliance friction. Companies typically focus on idea quality or team capability, but the organizational interfaces between innovation teams and core business functions (legal, finance, procurement) often silently kill projects through delay and cost. The leverage is in designing these interfaces once rather than fighting them repeatedly.
