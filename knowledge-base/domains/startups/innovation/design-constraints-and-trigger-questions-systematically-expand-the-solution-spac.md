Strategyzer's toolkit includes design constraint cards and trigger question sets (for customer jobs, pains, gains, pain relievers, and gain creators) that function as structured prompts for innovation. Rather than asking 'what should we build?', these tools ask specific provocative questions that force teams to consider dimensions they would otherwise ignore.

This reflects a deeper insight about creative process: unconstrained brainstorming tends to converge on obvious, first-order ideas because teams default to their existing mental models. Structured constraints — like 'what if this had to be free?' or 'what if the customer never interacted with it directly?' — force exploration of solution spaces that wouldn't emerge naturally. The Strategyzer approach of bundling these into reusable card decks makes this creative discipline repeatable rather than dependent on having a skilled facilitator.
