Junestrand competed seriously in Dota 2 (5-player competitive game) and credits gaming with developing his leadership style. In Dota, you must collaborate with 4 other players who may be much smarter than you in specific areas. The team must work seamlessly to achieve goals together. Sound familiar?

His leadership principle: a CEO's core job is attracting people smarter than themselves across different fields, then making the band play together. When they start working together, something new unlocks. Junestrand's gaming background also taught him that greatness requires grinding—not just more hours, but smarter hours with higher intensity than anyone else.
