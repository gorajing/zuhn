The 2021-era 15-18x ARR multiples are gone permanently, replaced by a more disciplined market where Q1 2026 multiples range from ~4.8x for bootstrapped moderate-growth companies to 10-12x for high-growth outliers running competitive processes. The critical insight is that this correction didn't compress the range — it widened the gap between average and exceptional exits.

This widening means the same variables that have always mattered (growth, retention, process) now create dramatically larger dollar differences. A $5M ARR company at 5x versus 9x is the difference between a $25M and $45M exit. The practical implication is that founders who understand and optimize these levers early capture disproportionate value compared to those who treat valuation as an afterthought.
