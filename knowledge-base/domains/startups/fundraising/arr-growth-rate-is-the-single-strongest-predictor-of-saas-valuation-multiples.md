While multiple factors influence SaaS valuations, the regression analysis across 63 private transactions reveals that ARR growth rate carries the highest statistical weight. This means founders optimizing for exit value should prioritize sustainable growth above other metrics — though the growth must be calculated correctly as annualized actual run-rate, not projected.

The distinction between actual and projected growth matters more than most founders expect, because buyers' diligence models will recalculate growth using their own methodology. Founders who inflate growth figures with projections face repricing during diligence. The implication is that authentic, well-documented growth tracking is both the most important metric to optimize and the one most likely to be scrutinized.
