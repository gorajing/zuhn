The conventional startup wisdom of 'raise when you hit milestones' breaks down during macro policy shocks. When tariffs, interest rate shifts, or regulatory changes freeze VC deployment, even milestone-hitting startups find themselves unable to close rounds. The reverse is also true — policy tailwinds can create fundraising windows independent of product progress.

This means founders need a dual-track awareness: product development milestones AND macro-policy monitoring. Building 18-24 months of runway before anticipated policy disruptions, maintaining warm investor relationships during freezes, and being ready to accelerate fundraising when policy clarity returns are all tactical implications of this insight.
