Hormozi's earning formula has three variables: total value created × negotiation ability × scarcity of others who can create that value. The third variable is the moat. A great salesperson creates millions in value, but if someone else will do it for less, the position disappears. The defense isn't learning one 'incredibly secret unique skill' — it's the skill SET, the stack that emerges from your specific path.

To replicate Hormozi's stack, someone would need to have been a competitive fitness athlete, owned multiple gyms, done national turnarounds, built a licensing business, launched supplement and meal prep companies, started a software company, executed exits, and started a PE firm. Each individual skill is learnable, but the combination creates exponential uniqueness. This means time invested broadly across complementary domains isn't wasted — it's building an increasingly unreplicable asset.
