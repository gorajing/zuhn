Herjavec argues that AI is simplifying every business process, which means competitors can clone your product faster than ever. In this environment, the things that can't be copied — brand identity, storytelling ability, and accumulated media credibility — become disproportionately valuable as competitive moats.

This reframes the traditional startup advice of 'build a better product.' When execution is commoditized by AI tools, the founders who invest early in brand-building, media presence, and narrative craft will compound advantages that pure-product companies cannot match. It's a shift from technical moats to narrative moats.
