Voje: we're all sold the same system, so standing out is mission-critical. Three things cut through: (1) proprietary signals defined from your product analytics (an activated account inviting five colleagues is the moment sales should reach out); (2) proprietary research even big teams skip (don't fear spending a lot of time on one email); (3) human touch — people don't mind AI content if it adds value; the question is 'is this relevant to me?' She cites 3–5x, sometimes 10x conversion for one researched message vs fifty templated. This near-verbatim corroborates the KB's L2/L3 principles that the proprietary, manually-discovered signal is the moat and the 0->80 AI / 80->100 human boundary.
