Misha Laskin argues that if you solve autonomous coding, you solve the broader superintelligence problem. His reasoning: code is the universal medium through which all other capabilities can be built. Reflection AI combines large language models with reinforcement learning (the same technology behind AlphaGo) to create autonomous coding agents.

This is a bold strategic thesis—rather than pursuing general intelligence directly, focus on the domain (coding) that can recursively improve all other AI capabilities. The combination of LLMs for broad knowledge and RL for focused optimization is what Reflection believes will bridge the gap between useful copilots and truly autonomous systems.
