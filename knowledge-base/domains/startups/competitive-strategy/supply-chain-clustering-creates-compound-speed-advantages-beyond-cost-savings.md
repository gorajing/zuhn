Anker's CEO explains that prototypes arrive in 3 days in Shenzhen versus 2+ weeks when working remotely with the same factories. This speed advantage compounds: faster prototyping means faster product-market fit, which means faster revenue, which attracts more talent and suppliers to the cluster. The insight extends beyond manufacturing—any ecosystem where rapid iteration matters (Silicon Valley for software, Hollywood for film) benefits from geographic density of complementary capabilities. The clustering effect is self-reinforcing: each new entrant makes the cluster more valuable for everyone else, creating a moat that individual cost advantages cannot overcome.
