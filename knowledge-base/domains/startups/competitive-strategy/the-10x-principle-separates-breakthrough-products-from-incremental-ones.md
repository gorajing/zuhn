Senkut was Google's first PM and helped launch Gmail. The core principle: Gmail wasn't slightly better than Hotmail—it was 10X better with vastly more storage, better search, and a fundamentally different approach. This 10X principle became Felicis Ventures' investment thesis across 45+ unicorns.

The principle applies beyond products to entire business strategies. In a world where AI commoditizes incremental improvements, the bar for venture-scale success is creating something so dramatically better that it makes existing solutions look broken. Shopify, Canva, and Notion—all Felicis investments—followed this pattern.
