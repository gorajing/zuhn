Enterprise software is shifting away from all-in-one ERP suites toward best-of-breed specialized tools. This unbundling is driven by enterprises demanding faster innovation cycles and more flexibility than monolithic systems can deliver.

This pattern is significant because ERP replacement cycles happen roughly once per decade, and each cycle creates a window where startups with superior point solutions can displace incumbent modules. The strategic playbook is to identify which ERP functions are most poorly served by the monolith, build a 10x better standalone tool, then expand horizontally once embedded in the enterprise workflow.
