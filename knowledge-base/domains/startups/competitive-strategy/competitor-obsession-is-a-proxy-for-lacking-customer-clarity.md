Joly echoes Bezos: Amazon focused on customers while competitors focused on Amazon. Best Buy's partnership with Amazon — giving them exclusive Fire TV rights in Best Buy stores — stunned media who expected the two to be mortal enemies. Instead, both recognized that a TV is a considered purchase best experienced in-store.

The deeper pattern is that competitor obsession is a symptom, not a strategy. When you deeply understand your customer's job-to-be-done, you see competitors as potential ecosystem partners who enlarge the pie. The Best Buy-Amazon partnership worked because both companies understood the customer's purchase journey better than the media's zero-sum framing.
