Traditional SaaS pricing (per-seat, per-API-call) fundamentally undervalues AI products because the value delivered can be orders of magnitude higher than the marginal cost. Outcome-based pricing—charging a percentage of cost savings, revenue generated, or problems solved—aligns the startup's revenue with the actual value created, unlocking 15-30x revenue potential compared to conventional models.

This pricing model also creates a powerful competitive moat: incumbents locked into per-seat pricing can't easily switch without cannibalizing existing revenue, while AI-native startups can boldly offer 'pay only for results' guarantees that incumbents can't match. The key implementation challenge is defining measurable, attributable outcomes that both vendor and customer agree on.
