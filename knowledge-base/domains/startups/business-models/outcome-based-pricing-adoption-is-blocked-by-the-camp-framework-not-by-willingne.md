The CAMP framework identifies why outcome-based pricing remains aspirational for most SaaS companies. Consistency fails because different customers value different outcomes from the same product. Attribution is fuzzy when multiple tools contribute to a result. Measurability requires deep integration with customer systems that many vendors lack. Predictability suffers because volatile outcome volumes make CFOs unable to forecast spend.

The practical insight is that OBP should be treated as a progressive destination rather than a starting point. Smart companies first instrument outcome measurement, build attribution trust over time, and only switch to OBP contracts when data is airtight. Intercom's Fin succeeded with per-resolution pricing because support ticket resolution is one of the rare outcomes that cleanly satisfies all four CAMP criteria.
