Multiple Gumloop hires followed the same pattern: a customer from
Instacart quit to join, a customer from WebFlow quit to join, a
customer from Shopify quit to join. These people already loved the
product and saw the vision — they had conviction that cannot be
assessed in an interview. They understood the problem space from the
user side, had strong opinions about what the product should become,
and were willing to take a pay cut and career risk because they
genuinely believed in the mission. "You have to build something
amazing to make the best people want to join you." The implication:
your product quality is your recruiting pipeline. The best hires
are not found through job postings — they find you because they are
already using and loving what you built.
