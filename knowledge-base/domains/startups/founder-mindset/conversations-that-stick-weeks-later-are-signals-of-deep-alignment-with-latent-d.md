Michael Seibel notes that people say things to you all the time, but when a specific remark sticks and you can replay it weeks later, something deeper is going on. Three separate conversations — with Justin Kan, his wife Sarah, and Steve Huffman — all lodged in his mind because they resonated with a decision he had already half-made. This is a practical heuristic for anyone stuck on a big life decision: track which casual remarks refuse to leave your head. They are pointing at the answer you already know.
