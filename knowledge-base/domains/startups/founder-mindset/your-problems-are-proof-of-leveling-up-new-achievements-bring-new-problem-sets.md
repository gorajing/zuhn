Manson reframes the relationship between success and struggle: you wanted the job, now you have the stress; you wanted the relationship, now you have the arguments. This does not mean something went wrong — it means you leveled up and have a new problem set.

For founders, this is critical. The problems at $1M ARR are entirely different from the problems at $0 — but they are still problems. Struggles never get easier; you just get better at bearing them. The trap is waiting for some level where things stop being hard. They will not. The practice is learning to be grateful for the new problems because they are evidence of the growth you asked for.
