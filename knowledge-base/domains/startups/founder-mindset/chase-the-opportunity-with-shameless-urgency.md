When Rakhmetzhanov had his mini office hour with Paul Graham, PG was leaving. Instead of accepting the end of the interaction, Arlan ran to his car yelling for 3-5 more minutes. PG was annoyed and said 'just email me.' Arlan emailed instantly with the subject line 'the kid who stopped you in the end.' The result: PG invested.

The email subject line was so unusual that PG actually opened it and read about the business. PG evaluates both the business opportunity and the founder—and Arlan's shameless urgency demonstrated the kind of founder energy that investors recognize as a positive signal. The people who break through are the ones willing to be uncomfortable in pursuit of their goals.
