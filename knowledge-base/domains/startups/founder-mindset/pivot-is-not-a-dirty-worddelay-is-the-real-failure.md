Windsurf pivoted from GPU virtualization (with a couple million in revenue) to AI-powered code after GPT-3.5 launched in 2022. Mohan's biggest regret isn't the pivot—it's not doing it three months earlier. Many founders hate the word 'pivot,' but Mohan calls it 'awesome.'

The key insight: be humble about your ideas. Some idealism is necessary, but there's no reason to believe an idea is great until it's validated. The faster you fail and pivot, the faster you find what actually works. Windsurf went from 8 employees doing GPU virtualization to a million-developer AI IDE by embracing pivots as a feature, not a bug.
