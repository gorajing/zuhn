Wu describes working 9-10 hour days at a hedge fund and being miserable when called in on weekends. Now she works every waking second, 7 days a week, and has never been happier. The difference isn't the hours—it's ownership and alignment.

Her advice is deceptively simple: do what feels right. When you own a piece of something and grow it from the ground up, the excitement is intrinsic. If the stable career path leaves you hollow while others' entrepreneurial journeys (even failing ones) excite you, that's the signal. Don't rationalize staying for golden handcuffs or 'learning'—that's just delayed action.
