Lucy Guo's routine is extreme: wake at 5:30am, gym, work until 8-9pm, dinner, more work until 11pm-1am. She chose her house for proximity to the gym and office (5 minutes each). She doesn't scroll TikTok, watch TV, or watch movies.

But the key insight isn't the hours—it's the discipline framework. YC tells founders to only do three things: work out, talk to customers, and build. Guo follows this literally. Working out isn't about health—it's a discipline test. If you can get up and train when you feel terrible, you've proven you can do anything that day. The physical routine creates the structure that enables the cognitive work.
