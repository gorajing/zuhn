Norton's applied-AI lead watched BDRs work and collapsed everything between calls into one box, AI PCR (pre-call research). Result: BDRs make 150–250 calls/day still hyper-prepared; historical average closed-won rose from ~$72k to ~$120k per rep per month, with a top BDR producing $174k closed-won cold in a single month (~$2M/yr). The mechanism is giving reps only the 'Glengarry' leads. Reinforces the KB principle that AI's job is to remove wasted motion on a working motion, not to manufacture one.
