The panel distinguished between a technology trajectory and an investable product window. Self-healing or self-improving behavior may become part of how software is built, especially as models help improve other models and agents, but the panelist had not yet seen a clean productization path for a standalone company around the abstract capability.

The strategic implication is to embed self-improvement where the customer already has a painful workflow, not sell recursion as the product by itself.
