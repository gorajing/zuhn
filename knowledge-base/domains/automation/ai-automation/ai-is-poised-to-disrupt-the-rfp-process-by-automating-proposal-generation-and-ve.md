The Request for Proposal process is notoriously slow and document-heavy, making it a natural target for AI automation. Analyst Tom Tunguz identified this as a near-term AI use case with clear ROI: AI can ingest a requirements document and generate a compliant, detailed proposal draft in minutes versus the days or weeks required today.

For enterprise software companies, winning RFPs is often a critical sales motion, meaning this capability is both a productivity tool and a competitive differentiator. Teams that automate proposal generation can respond to more opportunities with higher quality responses, while those that don't will find their human-written proposals look underprepared in comparison.
