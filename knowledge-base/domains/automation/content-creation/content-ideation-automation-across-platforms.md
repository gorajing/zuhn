Chase Hannegan's n8n automation grew him from 0 to 175K+ followers in
under 12 months by automating the content research process. The
workflow scrapes YouTube, Reddit, Twitter/X, and the web daily to
identify content outliers and trending topics. It analyzes transcripts,
upvotes, comments, and keywords, then filters out shorts and spam.
The output is a daily report with trending topics for a specific niche
plus detailed content ideas including scripts, hooks, storylines, and
pain point solutions. The system is trained on proven storytelling
principles to avoid generic AI output. Technical stack: n8n, OpenAI,
Perplexity, Airtable, and email delivery for daily digests.
