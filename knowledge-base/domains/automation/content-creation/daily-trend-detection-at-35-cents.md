The content ideation automation runs daily for under $0.35 per run,
replacing hours of manual research. The low cost comes from using
efficient API calls: scraping public data from YouTube, Reddit, and
Twitter is cheap, and the LLM processing (via OpenAI) only needs to
summarize and score already-filtered content rather than generate from
scratch. The workflow is fully customizable -- add subreddits, keywords,
or platforms to expand coverage. At $0.35/day, the annual cost is ~$128
for daily automated trend intelligence that would otherwise require a
human researcher spending hours per day. The n8n template is available
free at go.n8n.io/content-idea-template.
