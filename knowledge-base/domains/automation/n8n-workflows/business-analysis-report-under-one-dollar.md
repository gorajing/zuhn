Submit a URL and get a professional business analysis report for ~$0.20.
The n8n workflow uses Firecrawl for web scraping, Perplexity and Google
Gemini as AI analysis agents, and outputs to Google Docs and PDF via
Gmail. Reports include: business overview, target audience personas
(demographic and psychographic), brand personality and UVP, customer
journey mapping (awareness through loyalty), and E-E-A-T analysis.
Designed for agencies, freelancers, and consultants. The free version
excludes keyword research; a paid version adds DataForSEO integration.
