Hormozi's formula: 30-day cash collected should equal 2x CAC + COGS. This means each customer finances their own acquisition and the next customer's within one credit card billing cycle. He used this to go from $1,000 in the bank to $10 million in sales in 10 months, achieving 100:1 return on ad spend by continuously reinvesting. The 30-day window is critical because it aligns with interest-free credit card cycles — anyone can access this capital regardless of starting wealth. Most businesses don't do this because they accept industry-standard payment terms (net-60, monthly billing) as immutable, when in reality offering prepayment incentives like quarterly billing with a 5% discount can pull cash forward dramatically.
