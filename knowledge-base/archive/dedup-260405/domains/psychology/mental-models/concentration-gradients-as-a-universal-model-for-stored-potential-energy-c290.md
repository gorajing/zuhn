In photosynthesis, the thylakoid membrane charges like a battery by pumping protons to one side, creating a concentration gradient. The only escape route is through ATP synthase, which harnesses that flow to produce ATP. The energy was always there in the gradient — the enzyme just provides the constrained channel.

This is a transferable mental model: stored potential exists wherever there's an imbalance across a boundary (price differentials between markets, attention surplus in one channel vs. scarcity in another, knowledge asymmetry between expert and novice). The value capture happens not from creating the imbalance but from being the constrained pathway through which it resolves. Marketplaces, brokers, and educators all function as ATP synthase — they don't generate the energy, they position themselves at the bottleneck where gradients equalize.
