The narrative that scaling laws are dying has been repeatedly falsified. Gemini 3 Deep Think's jump from 45.1% to 84.6% on ARC-AGI-2 — nearly doubling in three months — is the clearest recent evidence that test-time compute scaling is a genuinely new regime of capability growth, separate from and additive to pre-training scaling.

This matters for anyone building on top of AI: capabilities are not plateauing. The practical implication is that use cases that seem out of reach today (autonomous scientific research, novel benchmark tasks) may become routine within a year. Building products with 'current model ceiling' assumptions baked in is increasingly dangerous planning.
