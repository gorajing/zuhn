Bonanno's flexibility sequence has three steps drawn from research. Step 1 — Context Sensitivity: stop and identify the specific problem in this moment, not the broad catastrophe. 'My life is ruined' is too big to solve; 'I am not sleeping well' is solvable. Step 2 — Repertoire: choose one strategy from your personal toolbox of coping mechanisms. Step 3 — Feedback: monitor whether it is working.

The critical insight is at the feedback step: many people try one thing, it fails, and they conclude they cannot cope. The flexibility sequence says go back to Step 2 and try a different strategy. You may need several attempts. If nothing works, return to Step 1 and redefine the problem more narrowly. This iterative approach builds mastery and agency — each attempt, even failed ones, is your choice and teaches you about your tools.
