Lenny's Newsletter has 1M+ subscribers who are product managers, founders, and engineers—exactly PostHog's target market. By offering an exclusive deal unavailable anywhere else, PostHog acquires customers through a channel with pre-existing trust and relevance that cold advertising cannot manufacture. The framing as a 'Product Pass' bundled with other high-value tools also creates FOMO: the $16,500 deal is only available to annual subscribers, driving subscription upgrades for Lenny while driving trials for PostHog. This kind of partnership works best when the newsletter audience and the product's ideal customer profile are tightly aligned. Developer and PM tools are particularly well-suited to this model because their buyers are information-dense and trust peer recommendations heavily.
