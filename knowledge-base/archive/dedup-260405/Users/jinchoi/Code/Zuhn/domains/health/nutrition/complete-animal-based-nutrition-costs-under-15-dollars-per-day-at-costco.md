A practical Costco-based animal-based diet template for under $15/day: 1-1.5 lbs grass-fed ground beef (~$7-9), 3-4 pasture-raised eggs (~$1.50), 1 quart whole milk (~$1.50), seasonal fruit (~$2-3), and butter for cooking (~$0.50). This provides complete protein, fat-soluble vitamins, minerals, and carbohydrates.

Bulk buying at Costco significantly reduces per-unit costs versus regular grocery stores. Freezing excess ground beef in portions enables weekly shopping trips. This contradicts the common objection that healthy eating is prohibitively expensive.
