Andreessen Horowitz's 2025 enterprise AI report shows companies shifting decisively from custom AI development to buying ready-made solutions. This signals enterprise AI has crossed from experimentation to deployment phase, where reliability and speed-to-value matter more than bespoke capabilities.

This pattern mirrors previous enterprise tech waves (cloud, SaaS) where early custom approaches gave way to standardized products. For founders, this means the window for horizontal AI platforms is closing while vertical, domain-specific AI products with clear ROI stories are becoming the primary investment target.
