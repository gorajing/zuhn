Chips are 65% starch + 30% seed oil. Crackers are 70% starch + 25% seed oil. Cereals are 80% starch + sugar. Bread is 75% starch + 15% seed oil. When starch combines with seed oil under heat, it creates compounded inflammatory damage worse than either alone. 65% of calories young children eat are ultra-processed, rising to 70% for teenagers. Adults are at 55%. The focus on sugar alone misses that starch is consumed in far higher quantities and is arguably more dangerous.
