As AI agents execute more cross-system workflows, the raw output becomes a commodity, but the trace of how decisions were made—who approved what, which exceptions fired, which data sources were consulted—is unique and hard to reconstruct. A 'context graph' layer that logs this lineage creates a new category of proprietary data that grows more valuable as the system runs. Startups positioned in the execution path, not just the inference layer, can accumulate this moat. This mirrors how CRMs became systems of record by capturing relationship history rather than just contact data: the log is the asset.
