PitchBook data reveals AI is capturing over half of all venture capital dollars in 2025, with $192.7B flowing to AI out of $366.8B total. Anthropic's $13B raise exemplifies the mega-round dynamics driving this concentration. This creates a two-tier funding market where non-AI startups face structurally harder fundraising environments regardless of their merit.

For founders, this means non-AI companies must demonstrate exceptional capital efficiency or find creative ways to incorporate AI into their value proposition. For investors, the concentration raises questions about whether the non-AI segment represents a contrarian opportunity or a value trap. The pattern mirrors previous tech concentration cycles (mobile in 2012, cloud in 2015) but at unprecedented scale.
