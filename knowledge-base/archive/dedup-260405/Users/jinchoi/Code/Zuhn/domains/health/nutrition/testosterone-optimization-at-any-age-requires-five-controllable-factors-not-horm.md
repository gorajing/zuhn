Five testosterone optimization levers: 1) Eliminate seed oils (linoleic acid disrupts steroidogenesis — testosterone is made from cholesterol), 2) Eat adequate cholesterol (eggs, organ meats — cholesterol is the raw material for testosterone), 3) Optimize sleep (7-9 hours — testosterone production peaks during deep sleep), 4) Heavy compound lifting (squats, deadlifts — mechanical stress triggers testosterone release), 5) Sunlight on skin (vitamin D is a hormone precursor; UV exposure on torso increases testosterone). Additionally: zinc from red meat, boron from fruit, and avoiding endocrine disruptors (plastics, fragrances). Saladino argues TRT should be a last resort because exogenous testosterone suppresses natural production permanently.
