For this week's edition, I’ve curated a collection of the best essays on startups, written by the most respected founders and venture capitalists worldwide. Starting, executing, and scaling a startup is anything but a ride in the park, and learning from those who have been in the trenches (and come out on top) is a great way to keep inspired and get insights on what it takes to make a startup successful.I’ve divided the collection into five categories:Startup Ideation and ExecutionFundraising and Investor RelationsScaling and GrowthTeam and CultureFailure and Lessons LearnedEach of these categories has a variety of essays by the featured authors listed below, some of which are the co-founders of Y Combinator, PayPal, LinkedIn, and tier-one VCs like Vinod Khosla and Garry Tan.
