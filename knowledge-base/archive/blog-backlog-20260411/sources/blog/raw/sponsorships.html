Sponsorships

The VC Corner is built for B2B companies selling to decision-makers.

Founders choosing their next tools.
Operators owning budgets.
Investors influencing portfolio spend.

If your product has a high LTV, long sales cycles, or benefits from brand trust over time, this is where sponsorships compound.

The VC Corner reaches:

▫️ 130,000+ subscribers on Substack
▫️ 130,000+ subscribers on LinkedIn
▫️ 90,000+ guaranteed views per article
▫️ 2–4M weekly impressions on LinkedIn

This is not mass reach. It’s concentrated attention from people who buy, recommend, and influence B2B software.

Why The Audience Converts

Readers trust The VC Corner because the content is practical, opinionated, and written from inside the ecosystem.

Most are:
▫️ Startup founders from Pre-Seed to Series C
▫️ Venture capital investors and angels
▫️ Senior operators leading teams and budgets

They read to stay sharp, not to be entertained. That makes brand awareness here unusually durable.

Sponsors benefit because:
▫️ Your brand is seen repeatedly by the same high-value readers
▫️ The endorsement feels editorial, not transactional
▫️ Trust transfers gradually, not via one click

This is especially effective for tools where being remembered matters as much as immediate conversion.

How Sponsorships Are Positioned

I don’t sell banner ads.

Each sponsor is framed in context, with a clear reason why founders and operators should care now. The placement sits in the top section of the newsletter, where attention is highest.

Each partnership includes:

▫️ Premium image banner + logo
▫️ Up to 500 characters of custom copy, written natively
▫️ Clear positioning around the problem you solve
▫️ Custom CTA button with tracked links
▫️ Up to 3 CTAs
▫️ Optional amplification on my personal LinkedIn account

I limit volume and avoid category overlap. Most sponsors run multi-month or annual partnerships.

Who This Is For

Sponsorships work best for:
▫️ B2B SaaS and infrastructure
▫️ Developer tools
▫️ AI, data, security, and fintech products
▫️ Companies with high LTV and strong word-of-mouth dynamics

Long-term partners include Attio, Vanta, Framer, Lovable, and Notion.
