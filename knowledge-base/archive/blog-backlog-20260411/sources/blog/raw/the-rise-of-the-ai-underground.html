The Rise of the AI Underground
The NFX Team ·@NFX ·May 2023 ·AI
The NFX Team ·@NFX

Everyone in Hayes Valley is all in on generative AI.

The headlines tell stories of big valuations and a race to keep up with the next version of ChatGPT+.

But what’s really happening day in and day out at hacker houses and AI social clubs is more electric than that. It’s an entirely new way of thinking and building.

And we’re still in the first wave of it. There are at least two more waves yet to come.

As part of our own recent AI hackathon, the NFX content team spent 48 hours going from house to house, office to office, talking with top founders about their work — what’s hard, what’s exciting, and what will never be the same again.
