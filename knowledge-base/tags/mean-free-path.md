# Tag: mean-free-path

- `INS-260330-85AA` Aerogel insulates better than air despite being 99% air because its pores are smaller than air molecules' mean free path.
- `INS-260330-B6DE` When pores are smaller than air's mean free path, gas molecules can't diffuse heat normally, making a 99.8% air material a better insulator than air itself.
