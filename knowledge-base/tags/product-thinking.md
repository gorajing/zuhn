- `INS-260327-A608` Great products start by identifying real problems, not by showcasing technology capabilities.
- `INS-260514-D155` Restructure GTM as a product team — salespeople are PMs surfacing customer signal, GTM engineers are engineers building frameworks to act on it, and the whole team runs cross-team experiments instead of solo tradecraft.
- `INS-260330-38B9` Singapore explicitly designed its education, healthcare, housing, and legal system to attract multinational corporations — treating the entire nation as a product built for a specific customer segment.
