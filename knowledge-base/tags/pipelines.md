# Tag: pipelines

- `INS-260405-F3C0` Photosynthesis separates light capture (producing ATP/NADPH) from carbon fixation (the Calvin Cycle) because raw photon input is too volatile to drive complex chemistry directly.
- `INS-260404-596E` The electron transport chain converts photon energy into usable ATP through a series of progressively lower-energy steps rather than one big reaction.
- `INS-260404-D916` The electron transport chain deliberately loses energy at each step to power useful work like ATP synthesis, rather than converting photon energy to sugar in one step.
- `INS-260403-6129` Breaking value conversion into intermediate carrier steps (ATP, NADPH) enables flexible downstream use at the cost of per-step energy loss.
