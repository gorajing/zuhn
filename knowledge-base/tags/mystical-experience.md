# Tag: mystical-experience

- `INS-260330-887A` Hopkins data shows that patients who rate their psilocybin session as a mystical-type experience show the strongest and most durable therapeutic improvements, regardless of diagnosis.
- `INS-260330-EE16` Johns Hopkins research shows that the intensity of mystical-type experiences, not just drug dose, predicts whether patients achieve lasting therapeutic benefits from psilocybin.
