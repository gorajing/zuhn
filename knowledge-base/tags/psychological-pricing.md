# Tag: psychological-pricing

- `INS-260327-875C` A $200 purchase feels expensive; four payments of $50 feels manageable — same cost, perceived as dramatically cheaper.
- `INS-260327-F26E` Subscriptions work psychologically because they decouple the pain of payment from the moment of consumption — you don't feel each Netflix watch or Spotify listen as a purchase.
- `INS-260327-8389` Starbucks's 170,000+ possible drink combinations create the illusion that your order is uniquely 'you' — a psychological trick that justifies complexity pricing.
