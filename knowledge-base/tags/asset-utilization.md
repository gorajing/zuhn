- `INS-260412-1E19` Budget airlines schedule 30-45 minute turnarounds and fly planes all day nonstop, ensuring the most expensive asset is always generating revenue rather than sitting idle.

- `INS-260412-F8AE` FedEx uses retired passenger aircraft because their planes only fly ~2 hours/day versus airlines' 12+ hours — cheap purchase price matters more than fuel efficiency at low utilization.
- `INS-260412-B625` Banked hubs trade lower plane utilization for shorter connections that capture more premium connecting passengers.
