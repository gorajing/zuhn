# Tag: pro-rata

- `INS-260330-3559` Pro-rata rights let investors maintain their ownership percentage in future rounds, which means founders bear a greater share of dilution.
- `INS-260403-6BA6` Pro rata rights are meaningless without reserves — ask about reserve percentage, deployment status, and investment period timeline.
- `INS-260403-4ADC` Not exercising pro-rata rights in breakout companies costs VCs far more than their failed bets.
