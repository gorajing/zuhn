# Tag: behavioral-data

- `INS-260322-A36C` When a measure becomes a target, it stops being accurate — AI productivity must be measured through passive behavioral data (who actually uses the tools) married with outcomes, not self-reported surveys.
- `INS-260625-CDEA` LLMs encode what people say, not what they do, so accurate human simulation requires collecting behavioral data (RCTs, life-story interviews) that the frontier models structurally lack.
