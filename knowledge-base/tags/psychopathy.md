# Tag: psychopathy

- `INS-260325-6DD8` Psychopathy affects ~1% of adults, is caused by both genetics and environment, and is treatable through CBT, medication, and parent management training.
- `INS-260325-A9E0` Clinical psychopathy affects about 1% of adults, as common as schizophrenia, meaning you have almost certainly encountered psychopathic individuals.
- `INS-260325-EC93` Psychopathy is difficult but not impossible to treat; CBT, medication, and parent training all show effectiveness.
- `INS-260325-D82A` Psychopathy's defining feature is 'The Mask of Sanity' — an outwardly normal, charming exterior concealing profound deficits in emotion and genuine caring about others.
