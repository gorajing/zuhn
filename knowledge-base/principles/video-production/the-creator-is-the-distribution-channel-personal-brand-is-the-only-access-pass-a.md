Holding a camera is a universal access pass that gives front-row seats to history. Building in public attracts your ideal audience while filtering everyone else. MrBeast proved that obsessing over universal human experiences creates geography-independent content. AI augments this human center rather than replacing it -- treating AI as a creative collaborator opens new production possibilities. The creator's identity, access, and relationships are the non-replicable assets; everything else in the production stack is commoditizing.
