Professional video sound design follows a precise three-layer workflow: temp music/score first, creative SFX and foley second, final mix third. Every visible element must have a corresponding sound. Technical foundations (48kHz sample rate matching, clapboard sync) prevent drift and misalignment that compound over longer recordings. Most creators treat audio as an afterthought, which is why professional audio is the single fastest way to distinguish amateur from professional video.
