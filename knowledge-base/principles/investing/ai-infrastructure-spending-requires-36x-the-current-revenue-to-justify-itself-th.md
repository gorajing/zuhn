Bain estimates $2T in revenue needed to justify AI spending, but only ~$55B exists — a 36x gap. $178.5B in data center credit deals in 2025 alone, funded by junk-grade debt, for customers that are unprofitable AI startups. Data centers take 1-2 years to build but GPUs become obsolete in 3-6 years. CoreWeave projects $23B negative free cash flow in 2026. Traditional SaaS scales cheaply per user, but AI products have marginal costs that increase with usage — Anthropic spends $8-$135 per dollar of Claude Code subscription revenue. When OpenAI and Anthropic file S-1s, forced financial transparency will likely trigger a WeWork-style reassessment. This is not a software disruption story — it's a financial leverage story with a clear historical pattern.
