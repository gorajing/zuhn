Tokenized debt outpaces tokenized equity 3x because credit's predictable cash flows map cleanly to smart contract enforcement, while equity tokens still route through SPV wrappers with ambiguous governance rights. Stablecoins capturing 90%+ of the $135B market proves that the dollar-denominated settlement layer is the real use case. NYSE integrating blockchain settlement into its existing matching engine signals that incumbents with regulatory trust will own this infrastructure, not crypto-native startups.
