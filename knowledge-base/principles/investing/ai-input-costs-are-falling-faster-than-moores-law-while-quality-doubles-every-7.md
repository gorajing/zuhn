AI model input costs have declined 99%+ in two years while quality doubles every 7 months. Unlike the dot-com broadband buildout funded by leveraged telecoms, today's $400B+ AI infrastructure buildout is funded by the most profitable companies ever created (Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta), making systemic risk fundamentally lower. Energy is the current bottleneck (driving nuclear interest), but once solved, cooling becomes the next constraint. Consumer AI (ChatGPT) is proving stickier than B2B model access because switching a family's habits is harder than switching an API call. Companies secretly integrate classical symbolic tools while telling investors they're just scaling neural networks. 70% of fund managers now see AI as overvalued. For AI startups to beat incumbents, they need reimagined UI + new data sources + disruptive business model — all three, not just one.
