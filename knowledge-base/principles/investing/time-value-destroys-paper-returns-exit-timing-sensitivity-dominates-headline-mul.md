MOIC flatters slow capital while IRR exaggerates quick wins. Exit structure details like lock-ups, earn-outs, and IPO discounts erode the gap between paper valuation and cash. Shifting exit year by one year can change lender IRR more than adjusting coupon by percentage points. The same return multiple represents wildly different investment quality depending on capital lock-up.
