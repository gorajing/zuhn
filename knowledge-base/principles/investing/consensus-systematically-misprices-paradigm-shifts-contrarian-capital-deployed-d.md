Transformative technologies deterministically create bubbles because consensus models cannot predict nonlinear growth. The best returns come from deploying capital when others retreat — a16z raised during the 2009 crisis, Milner invested at 'insane' Facebook valuations, and the most covered company in history (Apple) was still mispriced 3x by consensus after iPhone. Judge investment quality at time of decision, not by waiting 10-15 years for outcomes, because paradigm shifts make backward-looking frameworks obsolete.
