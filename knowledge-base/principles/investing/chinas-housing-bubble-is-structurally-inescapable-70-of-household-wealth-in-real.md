Chinese households save 40% of income but funnel 70% of wealth into real estate because stocks are volatile and banks are distrusted. Beijing knows the bubble exists and broadly knows the fixes, but real estate is so intertwined with GDP that any correction risks economic slowdown. This creates a structural trap: the bubble cannot be deflated without destroying the only asset class the population trusts.
