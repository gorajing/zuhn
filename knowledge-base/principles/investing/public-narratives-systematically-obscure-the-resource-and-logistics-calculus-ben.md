The Iran operation is about clearing a logistics corridor, not religion or revenge. Venezuela, Iran, and Greenland moves are about creating oil chokepoints against China, not the surface justifications. W.R. Grace lobbied to redefine 'asbestos-free' rather than fight regulations directly. Western media overestimates Iranian regime fragility by amplifying diaspora voices over internal reality. The pattern is that ideology, morality, and crisis framing are consistently deployed as narrative cover for resource control and logistics optimization — and investors who decode the map beneath the narrative see moves 2-3 turns ahead.
