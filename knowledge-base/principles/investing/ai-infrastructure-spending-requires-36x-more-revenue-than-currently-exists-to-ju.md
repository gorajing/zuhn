Bain estimates $2T in revenue needed but only ~$55B exists. $178.5B in data center credit deals in 2025 alone, funded by junk-grade debt for unprofitable AI startups. Coreweave (backed by Nvidia, Blackstone, JP Morgan, Goldman) projects $23B negative free cash flow in 2026. Unlike Uber's 2-3x subsidized-to-real price increase, AI would require 5-7x increases into a range users have never budgeted for. The crisis comes from credit exposure, not from the technology failing.
