Prediction markets hit $6B weekly volume (100x since pre-2024 election), Kalshi's $11B valuation validates the category, and a Fed working paper found their probability distributions outperform Bloomberg consensus and fed funds futures for CPI and rate decisions. More traders create better price signals which attract more traders — a classic liquidity network effect. They threaten traditional platforms like Robinhood because betting on events delivers the same dopamine as day trading but with more engaging narratives. Regulatory infrastructure, not technology, remains the primary barrier to expansion.
