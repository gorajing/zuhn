In deep geopolitical conflicts, each side builds an internally coherent story from the same historical events, while media systems show different populations fundamentally different versions of the same reality. The real information gap is not what facts exist but which facts are emphasized, omitted, or emotionally charged -- and recognizing this framing asymmetry is prerequisite to any accurate geopolitical analysis or investment thesis.
