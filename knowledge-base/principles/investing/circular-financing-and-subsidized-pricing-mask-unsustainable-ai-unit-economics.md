Nvidia investing $30B in OpenAI while OpenAI buys Nvidia chips is circular financing that inflates apparent demand, with 70% of fund managers now seeing AI as overvalued. Anthropic has earned $5B against $10B spent and OpenAI's ratio is worse — both lose money on every user query. Companies secretly integrate classical symbolic tools while telling investors they're just scaling neural networks, meaning the actual cost of reliable AI output is higher than the narrative suggests.
