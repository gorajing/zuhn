Multiple billion-dollar-plus funds in Q1 2026 explicitly target defense, dual-use technology, and national security, a category barely visible in VC five years ago. Project Vault pairs $12B in reserves with bilateral agreements across 11 countries for mineral security, and defense-industrial catch-up requires allied co-manufacturing because domestic reshoring is too slow. JPMorgan's $1.5T commitment signals private banks are assuming industrial policy roles governments are too slow to fill, fundamentally blurring the line between private capital allocation and state strategic planning.
