BNPL debt isn't counted in traditional consumer debt metrics, meaning actual consumer leverage is significantly higher than reported. Auto lending has fragmented across so many products that even regulators can't track total exposure. Corporate debt hit $14T not from productive investment but from financial engineering — companies borrowed to buy back stock while hollowing out operations. 2025 had the highest corporate bankruptcies ever outside a recession. The pattern is consistent: innovative financial products expand access (good) while hiding total leverage from the measurement systems designed to detect risk (dangerous).
