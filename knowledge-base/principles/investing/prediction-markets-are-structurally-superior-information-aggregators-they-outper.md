Prediction markets aggregate dispersed knowledge into price signals with superior accuracy because they incentivize calibrated beliefs through real financial stakes. Kalshi's probability distributions outperform Bloomberg consensus, and the 100x volume growth to $6B weekly demonstrates genuine demand for information markets. The same liquidity network effects that power exchanges compound here: more traders create better price signals, which attract more traders.
