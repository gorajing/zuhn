The nuclear command system allows launch in seconds with a 6-minute presidential decision window, leaders have repeatedly demonstrated ignorance of its mechanics, and tactical nuclear weapons create an escalation ladder with no stable rungs. Deterrence theory assumes rational actors with perfect information in a system designed for speed over deliberation -- making nuclear risk the most systematically underpriced tail risk in any geopolitical model.
