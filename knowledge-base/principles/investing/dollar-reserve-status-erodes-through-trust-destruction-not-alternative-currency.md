Allied trust in US commitments is the foundation of dollar reserve status, and transactional foreign policy directly threatens it. De-dollarization by China and Russia is selective and gradual -- reducing dollar use where politically necessary while maintaining it where convenient -- making sudden collapse unlikely but slow erosion real. Simultaneous Treasury yield increases and dollar weakness indicate money leaving America, and the US government is paradoxically undermining its own dollar dominance by attacking stablecoin projects that would cement USD as the default digital currency.
