Young people with small capital bases get dramatically higher ROI from skill investment than from financial markets. Wealthy people consistently invest in domains where they have deep personal expertise rather than diversifying into unfamiliar sophisticated vehicles. The optimal strategy shifts from human capital to financial assets as your capital base grows. Every $250/month compounds to ~$1M by retirement, but only after your earning capacity has compounded first.
