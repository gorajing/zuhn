ASML is the sole manufacturer of EUV machines, NVIDIA locked 70% of TSMC's 3nm capacity, and state espionage concentrates on technologies with multi-decade monopolies like jet engines. When a critical technology has exactly one supplier, that supplier becomes a strategic asset more powerful than military bases — semiconductor export controls now function as economic sanctions, and Musk's recursive single-building fab aims to break the dependency entirely. Unlike physical infrastructure chokepoints (ports, canals), these technological monopolies are harder to route around because the knowledge itself is the bottleneck.
