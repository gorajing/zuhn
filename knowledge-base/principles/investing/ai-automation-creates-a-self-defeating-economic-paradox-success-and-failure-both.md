AI infrastructure spending sustains jobs and wealth effects today while simultaneously building the automation that eliminates those jobs tomorrow. If AI fails, the wasted investment causes economic collapse; if it succeeds, automation displaces workers who are also consumers. The likeliest outcome is not mass unemployment but extreme concentration of wealth among infrastructure owners while the working majority stagnates -- politically explosive but economically stable enough to persist.
