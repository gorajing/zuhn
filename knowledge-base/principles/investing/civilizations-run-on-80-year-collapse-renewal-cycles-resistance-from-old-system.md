American history shows roughly 80-year cycles of systemic collapse followed by 25-year innovation booms. Every transformation triggers fierce resistance from those invested in old systems — from Southern slaveholders to modern petro-states — but this resistance is a recurring pattern, not evidence that transformation will fail. Recognizing the cycle position (collapse phase vs. renewal phase) is more valuable than analyzing individual catalysts, because the cycle itself is the dominant force.
