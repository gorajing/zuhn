Expert forecasts perform no better than random guessing, 30% of panic sellers never return to equities, and loss aversion makes routine 5-10% dips feel catastrophic. The only unsolved variable in investing is human behavior -- the data on returns, inflation, and market cycles is well-established. Success comes from minimizing behavioral errors (the loser's game framework), not from superior analysis or stock selection, because the information edge has been competed away while the behavioral edge persists indefinitely.
