When populations, market participants, or institutional actors are given no hope, no political agency, and face increasing pressure over decades, violent or disruptive escalation is the predictable outcome — not an anomaly. This pattern appears in geopolitical conflicts (occupied populations), market blow-ups (gated redemptions trapping capital), and democratic crises (norm-breaking when institutions ossify). The investment signal: identify systems where pressure is building with no legitimate release mechanism, because the timing is uncertain but the explosion is not.
