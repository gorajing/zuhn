More capital inflates prices, spreads talent thin, and compresses exit multiples. Record unicorn creation in 2020-2021 was driven by cheap capital, not proportional increases in transformational startups. A decade of zero rates inflated all returns uniformly, hiding which funds had real skill. Rising rates shake out weak competitors and force survivors to build sustainably.
