Biden barely cut any China tariffs and maintained aggressive semiconductor export controls despite appearing ideologically opposed to Trump. Trump's NSS is not isolationism but selective power projection — pulling back from Europe and Middle East to concentrate on containing China. The US has wanted to pivot to Asia since the 2000s but keeps getting stuck in the Middle East. The decisive factor is technological supremacy — particularly AI and standard-setting — not raw GDP. Musk warns China's economy will be 2-3x US, creating the same Athens-vs-Sparta dynamic that both sides saw coming and couldn't prevent. The containment strategy is invariant across administrations; only the rhetoric changes.
