Top AI companies grow 693% YoY while spending less on sales/marketing than SaaS predecessors, reaching $500K-$1M ARR per FTE vs the old $400K benchmark. Like iPhone caused consensus models to miss Apple's performance by 3x over four years, AI companies will massively exceed forecasts because the growth pattern is demand-driven, not sales-driven. Unlike the dot-com era, AI capex is financed by the most profitable companies in history, and 'there are no dark GPUs' — every deployed GPU gets fully utilized immediately. However, private credit entering the data center buildout and Oracle's massive cash-flow-negative bet are early warning signals worth watching.
