Pentagon officials write defense contracts, leave for contractor board seats, then return to government — creating a cycle where war-decision makers directly profit from perpetuating war. Pharmaceutical companies fund the FDA, banks staff financial regulators, and tech giants write platform regulations — the mechanism is always industry-funded oversight producing industry-favorable rules. W.R. Grace lobbied regulators to define asbestos contamination below 1% as 'asbestos-free' — controlling the definition was more effective than fighting individual regulations. The US Middle East involvement started with a single 1930s oil concession that created an oil-military-alliance feedback loop so embedded that withdrawal became structurally impossible. Farm bailouts flow 63% to the largest 7% of producers. Markets had priced in perpetual regulatory leniency, making the Google ruling a wake-up call.
