Beijing designates strategic industries from above while 300+ prefectural governments compete bottom-up to back local firms with subsidies and credit. This dual mechanism has produced dominance in solar (278GW installed in one year vs 177GW US all-time), EVs, batteries, and rare earths -- but also chronic overcapacity that dumps product at cost to shape global spot prices. The upstream mastery cascades: battery and motor manufacturing feeds into drones, autonomous vehicles, robotics, and military systems, creating an integrated 'Electric Tech Stack' that tariffs alone cannot counter.
