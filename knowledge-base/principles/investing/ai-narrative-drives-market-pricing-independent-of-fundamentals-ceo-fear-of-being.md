Companies miss earnings but surge 30% on compelling AI stories; Oracle gains more market cap in a day than McDonald's on AI promises rather than product merit. The same pattern that sustained big data and crypto spending now drives AI: Fortune 500 executives cannot afford to lack an AI strategy, creating defensive spending that continues regardless of demonstrated ROI. Each hype cycle references the genuine successes of predecessors (Google and Facebook for big data, Amazon for cloud) while thousands of imitators fail to replicate the actual value creation.
