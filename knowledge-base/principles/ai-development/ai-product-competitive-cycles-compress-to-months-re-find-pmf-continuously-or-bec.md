AI capability shifts make yesterday's differentiator today's commodity on roughly 3-month cycles. Every model improvement creates new product possibilities while simultaneously enabling every competitor to build faster, expanding customer choice from 2-3 options to 20-30. In this environment, being first to market makes you the first target rather than the winner. The only sustainable position is momentum itself -- launch speed, traction velocity, and the discipline to resist constant pivoting when AI makes cloning trivially easy.
