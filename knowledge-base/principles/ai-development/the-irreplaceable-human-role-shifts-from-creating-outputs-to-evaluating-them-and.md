When AI can perform any technical task, the critical skill shifts from doing to grading, from producing correct answers to evaluating whether answers are correct. As AI automates strategy, copy, design, and prototyping, the human role becomes orchestrating meaning — designing values, selecting which work matters, and defining scope of care. Employers will hire for trustworthiness and genuine care, not technical skill.
