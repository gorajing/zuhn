ChatGPT will be unbundled the same way Oracle and Excel were unbundled into 400-500 dedicated SaaS apps. The winning pattern is embedding AI into domain-specific workflows (loan servicing, creative production, industry operations) where it replaces labor costs, not software seats. AI only saves time when verification is cheaper than manual execution, so the verticals that win are those where output quality can be cheaply validated within the workflow itself.
