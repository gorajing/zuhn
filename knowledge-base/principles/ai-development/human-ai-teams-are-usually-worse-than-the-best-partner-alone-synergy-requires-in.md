  Human-AI teams are usually worse than the best partner alone — synergy

  Meta-analysis of 106 experiments shows human-AI teams average g = -0.23 vs

  When humans outperform AI, adding AI helps (g = +0.46). Only 3 of 106
  experiments tested intentional subtask delegation — those 3 showed positive
  synergy. Creation tasks show positive synergy while decision tasks show
  negative synergy. The default 'just use AI to help' destroys value; deliberate
  division of labor creates it.

Meta-analysis of 106 experiments shows human-AI teams average g = -0.23 vs best-of-either. When AI outperforms humans, adding a human hurts (g = -0.54). When humans outperform AI, adding AI helps (g = +0.46). Only 3 of 106 experiments tested intentional subtask delegation — those 3 showed positive synergy. Creation tasks show positive synergy while decision tasks show negative synergy. The default 'just use AI to help' destroys value; deliberate division of labor creates it.
