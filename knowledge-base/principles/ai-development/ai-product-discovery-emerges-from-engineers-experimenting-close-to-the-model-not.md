Anthropic's best products (Artifacts, MCP, Claude Code) all started as bottom-up prototypes from engineers working directly with the model, not as planned features. The same pattern appears across the industry: distributed experimentation with vendor accountability outperforms centralized approval, forward-deployed engineers who ship code while closing customer distance achieve 100% pilot-to-production conversion, and RL on outcomes produces more flexible agents than human-designed architectures. AI product roadmaps should be structured as experimentation portfolios, not feature backlogs.
