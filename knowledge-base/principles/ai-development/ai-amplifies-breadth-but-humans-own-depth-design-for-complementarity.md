title: AI amplifies breadth but humans own depth — design for complementarity
summary: >-
  Complementarity between humans and AI requires deliberate workflow design —
  naive combination destroys value. AI achieves depth in formal/verifiable
  domains (AlphaProof solved IMO P6 through cumulative multi-step reasoning)
  but humans retain depth advantage in open-ended domains (strategy, creative,
  social). The meta-analysis of 106 experiments shows human-AI teams perform
  worse than best-of-either (g = -0.23) unless tasks are intentionally
  decomposed: creation tasks show synergy, decision tasks show losses. The
  binding constraint is not transparency or explanations but structural design
  — which subtasks go to which partner.
