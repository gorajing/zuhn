The winning architecture for agentic AI is structured state machines with human-in-the-loop approval points, not end-to-end autonomous execution. Flow engineering -- explicitly designing the agent's decision graph -- outperforms relying on implicit LLM planning, and agentic workflows with iteration beat raw model capability (GPT-3.5 agentic > GPT-4 zero-shot). Even ambient agents operating at scale need inbox-style UX for human review, and real-world deployment in high-stakes domains like contact centers succeeds through human-agent blending rather than total automation.
