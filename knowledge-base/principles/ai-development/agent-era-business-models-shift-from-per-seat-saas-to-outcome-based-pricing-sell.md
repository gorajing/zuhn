Multiple AI customer service startups hitting $100M+ ARR with pay-per-resolution pricing indicates a fundamental shift from per-seat SaaS. Agents running entire standard operating procedures end-to-end replace BPO services completely, and the market has moved from AI-enhanced SaaS that helps humans to AI-native systems of action that autonomously execute entire workflows. 68% of large enterprises are already deploying agents, collapsing a timeline most predicted would take until 2030.
