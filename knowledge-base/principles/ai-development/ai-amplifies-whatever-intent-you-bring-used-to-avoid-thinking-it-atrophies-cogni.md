Some people use AI to avoid thinking; others use it to think more deeply than ever before. Students using AI to complete writing homework are skipping cognitive exercise, producing adults who cannot think logically or resist manipulation. The highest-value use of AI is building richer internal world models for better decision-making, not outsourcing output production. Meta-analysis of 106 experiments shows human-AI teams average -0.23 performance gap vs best-of-either alone. In the AGI era, the critical skill shifts from executing tasks to evaluating outputs — from doing the homework to grading it. When AI can perform any technical task, employers hire for trustworthiness and genuine care. The tool is neutral; the intent determines the outcome.
