AI replaced creative and white-collar jobs first, not physical labor as universally predicted, revealing that software engineering was always more routine cognitive work than the creative profession engineers believed it to be. Programmers are transitioning from master craftsmen to factory operators who set up and maintain AI systems. The role is shifting from low-level code writing to natural-language system design, and coding as a standalone skill is stratifying into depth levels just as literacy ranges from functional to literary.
