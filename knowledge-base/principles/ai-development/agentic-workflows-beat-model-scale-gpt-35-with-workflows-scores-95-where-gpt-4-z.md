Workflow design can matter more than model capability. The effective pattern is iterative prompting (prompt, see result, adjust, repeat) not perfecting a single massive prompt. Training agent swarms requires three separate reward functions to prevent serial behavior. The 'company of experts' trajectory (chatbot → chief of staff → team of 50 → 10,000) depends on workflow orchestration, not raw intelligence.
