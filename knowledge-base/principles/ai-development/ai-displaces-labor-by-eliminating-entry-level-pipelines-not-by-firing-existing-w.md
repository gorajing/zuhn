Companies are reducing new hires for junior roles rather than laying off existing staff, and replacing departing employees with AI agents delivering 70-80% of human output. This destroys the training ground that produces the next generation of senior experts. Product teams may shrink 25-50% (mainly fewer engineers) while the role composition shifts toward discovery and go-to-market. The long-term consequence is a hollowed-out middle of the workforce where experienced practitioners have no successors.
