When agents produce 1,300 PRs per week, human review alone cannot scale to verify correctness. CI pipelines, test coverage, and automated spec audits become the primary trust mechanism replacing the confidence that comes from knowing a human wrote the code. Investing in developer experience pays double because both human engineers and AI agents consume the same docs, CI, and tooling. Companies adopting AI engineers like Devin measure success by merged PRs and contributor rankings, not benchmark scores.
