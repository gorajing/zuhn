Products that start AI-light and add AI later end up with models trapped in sidebars that cannot access core functionality. True AI-native architecture exposes all product primitives to models, whether that means letting agents use your own frontend via computer-use, rebuilding performance-critical systems from scratch in appropriate technology, or designing text-based interfaces that naturally support agentic workflows. The architectural choice between AI-native and AI-bolted is a one-way door that determines the product's ceiling.
