Using AI to skip learning-stage work -- writing, reasoning, design judgment -- eliminates the cognitive development that work was meant to produce. Students who outsource homework become adults who cannot think independently; designers who defer to AI feedback lose creative identity; the 'AI clicker' who optimizes for efficiency without building underlying skills erodes the very human capacity that creates irreplaceable value. The distinction is not whether you use AI, but whether you use it to avoid thinking or to think more deeply.
