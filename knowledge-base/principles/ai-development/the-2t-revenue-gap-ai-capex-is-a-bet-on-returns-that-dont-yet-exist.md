AI labs are profitable on current models, but the bubble risk lies entirely in whether future capex on larger models will produce proportionally larger returns. The industry faces a massive gap between infrastructure spending ($400B+) and actual revenue generation, with AI application to specific industries having no proven playbook anywhere in the world. Leads that took years to build can be closed in months by competitors (DeepSeek proving capabilities can be replicated cheaply), making the capex bet even riskier since today's moats may not survive long enough to generate returns.
