AI-driven platform shifts don't just improve existing channels — they eliminate intermediation layers entirely. Traditional search (10 blue links), B2B sales funnels, content distribution, and enterprise software discovery are all being replaced by AI agents that evaluate, recommend, and purchase autonomously. This means product quality and machine-readable value signals replace marketing spend and human relationships as primary growth levers. Companies optimizing for human gatekeepers (SEO, sales teams, analyst rankings) will lose to those optimizing for AI agent evaluation.
