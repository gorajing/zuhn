The breakthroughs in AI mathematical reasoning, coding, and multi-step problem solving all trace to one mechanism: reinforcement learning with machine-verifiable reward signals. AlphaProof solved IMO-level problems by framing theorem proving as an RL environment with proof-state actions and verified rewards, adding 15 percentage points beyond what tree search alone could achieve. This same RL + verification pattern enabled tool use, agentic coding, and chain-of-thought reasoning -- each leap seeming 'too simple to work' until proven right.
