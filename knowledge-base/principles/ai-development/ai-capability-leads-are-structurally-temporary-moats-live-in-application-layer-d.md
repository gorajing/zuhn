Once a capability is demonstrated at the frontier, others replicate it with far fewer resources within months -- DeepSeek proved this definitively. Open models trail closed by a steady 6 months because the target keeps moving, model intelligence cost drops 4X/year while spending rises 10X/year, and the expertise to orchestrate training across tens of thousands of GPUs is the actual scarce resource, not compute alone. The durable competitive advantage shifts from model capability to compounding proprietary training data, domain-specific last-mile problem-solving, and using your own product as an RL environment.
