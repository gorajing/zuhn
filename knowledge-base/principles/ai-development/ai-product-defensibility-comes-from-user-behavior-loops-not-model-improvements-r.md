If your AI product is just a layer on an underlying platform, that platform can replicate it (Jasper, Zynga, SEO companies learned this). Every model improvement creates new product possibilities, making iteration speed the primary moat. AI product cycles compress to months. Defensibility comes from user behavior loops (pain → relief → habit → switching cost), not from AI model advantages that competitors replicate overnight.
