95% of enterprise AI pilots fail because they are designed top-down from organizational requirements rather than discovered bottom-up from individual user workflows. The pattern is consistent: workers adopt personal AI tools despite low corporate mandates, the most successful AI products (Artifacts, MCP) started as bottom-up engineer prototypes, and the 5% of orgs reaching production AI do so through distributed experimentation rather than centralized approval. Build from user behavior up, not org chart down.
