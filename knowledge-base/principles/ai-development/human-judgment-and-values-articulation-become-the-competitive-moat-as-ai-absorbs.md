As AI increasingly handles task execution, the durable human advantage shifts from doing to directing -- specifically to values articulation, taste, and knowing what to automate versus what to keep human. Automating processes you don't deeply understand creates slot machines, not efficiency. The next generation of AI will proactively suggest actions rather than waiting for prompts, making the human role one of editorial judgment over machine-initiated workflows. Meanwhile, optimization shifts from visual design to machine-readable structure and insight density as agents mediate between users and systems.
