Variable AI compute costs inside fixed-price subscriptions create an inverse relationship between user engagement and profitability that traditional SaaS economics cannot absorb. Product organizations need dedicated unit economics dashboarding, the discipline to stress-test every AI feature against power-user economics, and the willingness to kill margin-negative features. The 3x value-to-compute-cost rule and quarterly PMF reassessment are structural requirements, not nice-to-haves.
