AI uniquely undermines the information foundations of democratic society through multiple simultaneous channels: LLM content restrictions become de facto thought gatekeeping, AI-generated content floods drown truth in cheap fiction, and personalized persuasion surpasses mass propaganda in effectiveness. Meanwhile, AI could make totalitarian centralization viable for the first time because it processes the information volume that overwhelmed human bureaucrats. The structural defense is self-correcting institutions combined with open-source AI to prevent single-entity information chokepoints.
