Successful AI agent adoption starts with well-scoped engineering tasks then expands into QA, analytics, documentation, and customer experience. Guided agents that embed industry-specific guardrails and workflow sequences outperform generalized agents. Agents need three levels of business context (identity, strategic, enterprise) and should map to specific business operations stages. The regulated industries that are hardest to enter offer the strongest moats once accuracy and compliance thresholds are met.
