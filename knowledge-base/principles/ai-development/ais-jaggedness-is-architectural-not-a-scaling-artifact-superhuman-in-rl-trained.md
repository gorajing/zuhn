  Models simultaneously feel like a brilliant PhD and a 10-year-old. They're
  superhuman in domains with verifiable rewards (code, math, games) and
  unreliable outside them. This jaggedness doesn't improve with scale — it's an
  architectural property of how the models learn, not a data insufficiency.
  Generative AI can help with more tasks than past automation (more flexible)
  but takes over fewer tasks completely (less robust). AI's superpower is
  breadth (trying every technique on every problem), humans' is depth
  (cumulative progress from partial understanding). Karpathy's auto-research
  loop found hyperparameter improvements overnight that decades of ML experience
  missed — but only in the structured domain of training configs. The
  implication: restructure work to exploit AI's breadth while preserving human
  depth, don't wait for the jaggedness to smooth out.

Models simultaneously feel like a brilliant PhD and a 10-year-old. They're superhuman in domains with verifiable rewards (code, math, games) and unreliable outside them. This jaggedness doesn't improve with scale — it's an architectural property of how the models learn, not a data insufficiency. Generative AI can help with more tasks than past automation (more flexible) but takes over fewer tasks completely (less robust). AI's superpower is breadth (trying every technique on every problem), humans' is depth (cumulative progress from partial understanding). Karpathy's auto-research loop found hyperparameter improvements overnight that decades of ML experience missed — but only in the structured domain of training configs. The implication: restructure work to exploit AI's breadth while preserving human depth, don't wait for the jaggedness to smooth out.
