The person who deeply understands insurance underwriting and can direct an AI to build a tool is now more dangerous than the engineer who can build anything but doesn't know what to build. Non-engineers are becoming builders through AI tools, doing in hours what previously took days. Top developers now orchestrate multiple AI agents in parallel, elevating strategic direction over line-by-line coding. The winning career position combines domain knowledge with the ability to specify and evaluate AI output, not raw coding ability.
