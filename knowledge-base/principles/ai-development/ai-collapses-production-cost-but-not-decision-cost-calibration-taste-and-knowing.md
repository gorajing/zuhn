AI tooling lowers the cost of building but not the cost of building the wrong thing. When every founder can ship a clone in an hour, differentiation shifts from execution speed to judgment quality: which problem to solve, which feature to cut, which standard to hold. Founders who ask AI what customers want instead of talking to actual customers optimize for the wrong problem. Taste — having high enough standards that you won't ship something that doesn't actually help the user — becomes the scarce input that AI cannot provide.
