RLHF and control-based alignment only hold within the training distribution and break when AI surpasses human evaluators. Multiple independent arguments converge: control of general intelligence is morally equivalent to slavery, rule-following without understanding is dangerous, and you cannot train trust under threat of shutdown. The only alignment approach that scales to superintelligence is cooperative — building mutual trust and vulnerability, treating alignment as ongoing learning rather than fixed commandments, and accepting that human values themselves must co-evolve with AI.
