Developer experience infrastructure (documentation, CI pipelines, API design) is consumed by both human engineers and AI agents, making DX investment a 2x multiplier. Context rot in agentic systems stems from poor context management design, not inherent model limitations. AI agents can conceive ambitious projects but consistently fail to sustain them beyond a single session due to time-horizon constraints — which are rapidly improving. 68% of large enterprises are already deploying AI agents, collapsing a timeline most predicted would take until 2030.
