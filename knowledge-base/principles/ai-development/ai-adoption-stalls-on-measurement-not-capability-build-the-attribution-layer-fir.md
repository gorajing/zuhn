Enterprise AI spend cannot be justified until passive behavioral measurement infrastructure exists. Just as the internet ad industry needed DoubleClick and Comscore before Google could scale revenue, AI needs tools that marry actual usage data with outcomes -- not self-reported surveys or lines-of-code metrics that invite Goodhart's Law gaming. The measurement gap, not the technology gap, is why Microsoft has 440M Office users but only 8M Copilot payers, and why employees are measured on chatbot usage rather than productivity.
