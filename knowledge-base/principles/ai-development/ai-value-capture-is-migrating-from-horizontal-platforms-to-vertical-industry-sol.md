Vertical AI agents sell completed work rather than productivity tools, accessing the entire cost of labor as TAM instead of just the software budget. BPO in financial services alone is ~$400B annually, and AI agents can transform these from 10-15% margin services to 80% margin software businesses. The winning pattern is deep domain-specific integration — outcome-based legal services, logistics voice AI, manufacturing training data — rather than general-purpose chat interfaces. Incumbents like Accenture capture value faster than pure-play AI startups because they own the domain relationships.
