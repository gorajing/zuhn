The shift from SaaS to AI agents represents a category expansion, not just a product evolution. Traditional SaaS sold productivity enhancement to workers, accessing a $650B software budget; vertical AI agents sell the work itself, accessing the entire cost of labor -- tens of trillions in services, BPO, and professional work. BPO spend in banking alone is $400B (roughly equal to all global cloud software), and AI agents can transform 10-15% margin service businesses into 80% margin software businesses. This requires solving persistent identity, inter-agent communication protocols, and agent-level security before the full agent economy materializes.
