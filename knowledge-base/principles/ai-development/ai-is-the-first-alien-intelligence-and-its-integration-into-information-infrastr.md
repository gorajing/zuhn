AI breaks three assumptions that held for every previous information technology: it never rests (all prior networks had organic cycles with built-in rest), it generates culture autonomously (AI is the first non-human storyteller), and it could make totalitarian centralization work where it previously failed (processing information floods that overwhelmed human bureaucrats). The convergence of these properties means AI integrated into search, email, and content creation becomes de facto information gatekeeping — not through censorship but through the structural power of being the default interface between humans and knowledge. The free speech safeguard is open-source model availability, but competitive pressure is already correcting initial over-censorship (a year-over-year study of 268 prompts showed most LLMs became less restrictive).
