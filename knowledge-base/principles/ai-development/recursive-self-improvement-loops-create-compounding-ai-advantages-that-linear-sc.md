The most powerful dynamic in AI development is recursive feedback loops where AI outputs feed back into improving AI capabilities. AI that designs better chips enables better AI which designs even better chips. Companies using their own AI product as a training environment for post-training create advantages competitors cannot replicate. Training data compounds institutional knowledge over time while prompts reset every session. Robots performing useful tasks produce training data as a byproduct, creating data flywheels unavailable from internet scraping. These self-reinforcing cycles mean that early entrants in recursive loops build compounding advantages that widen over time.
