Modern frontier models are architecturally GPT-2 with minor tweaks. The jump from GPT-3.5 to GPT-4 came from hundreds of small improvements in data curation, optimization, post-training, and hardware-aware engineering multiplied together, not from an architectural leap. Progress in LLM development follows a compounding-marginal-gains pattern where the training pipeline — ablation methodology, data mixture strategy, optimizer choice, tokenizer alignment to hardware boundaries — is where all the differentiation lives. The architecture is a commodity; the pipeline is the moat.
