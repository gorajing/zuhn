AI, clean energy, and bioengineering are hitting adoption tipping points at the same time, paralleling historical 80-year cycles where extreme disruption is followed by 25-year explosions of institutional reinvention. AI as a cognitive amplifier is comparable to the steam engine as a physical amplifier -- the resulting world will be as unrecognizable to us as the industrial world was to pre-industrial societies. This convergence pattern means forecasting based on any single technology curve will systematically underestimate the pace of change.
