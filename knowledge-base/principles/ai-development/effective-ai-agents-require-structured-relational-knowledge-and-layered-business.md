Agents that store knowledge as relational databases with entities and relationships enable reasoning about connections, while text-blob vector retrieval only supports lookup. Agents need three explicit layers of context -- identity (who you are), strategic (mission, products, style), and enterprise (playbooks, decision trees, escalation paths) -- mapped to concrete business operations stages. Without rich structured human input to personalize around, even the most powerful agent is an empty shell that cannot differentiate or deliver real business value.
