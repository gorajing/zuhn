DeepSeek replicated OpenAI o1's benchmarks within months as a free open-source model, and Meta giving away a $10B model forces the entire market toward commoditized weights. The commoditization cycle compressed from decades (traditional tech) to months because of copy-paste, open-source, and API access. This creates a structural dynamic where closed-source pricing power erodes rapidly after each capability demonstration, making proprietary training data and inference infrastructure the only durable value-capture points.
