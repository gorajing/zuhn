The AI-vs-SaaS narrative mirrors the SaaS-vs-on-premise transition where incumbents adapted rather than disappeared. Canva acquired Leonardo AI rather than building model capabilities, OpenAI is moving into direct enterprise SaaS competition, and YC's Fall 2025 RFS shows competitive advantage shifting from model innovation to operational productization. The historical pattern suggests that selling AI as the product creates a fragile position — enduring value comes from integrating AI into existing workflows and data moats.
