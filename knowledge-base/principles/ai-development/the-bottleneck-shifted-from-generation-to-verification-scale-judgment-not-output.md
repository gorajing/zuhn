AI drove the cost of idea generation, code production, and content creation to near-zero. The bottleneck is now verification, evaluation, and quality filtering. Selection bias makes AI look like magic (1-2% success rate at scale looks like 50 wins). Soft time savings don't translate to hard value. The winning move is to scale your judgment (what to build, what to verify, what to trust) not your output volume. Domain expertise outweighs technical skill because knowing what's worth building matters more than building speed.
