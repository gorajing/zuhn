Using AI to complete learning-stage work (writing, reasoning, design) eliminates the cognitive struggle that builds capability. Students who outsource thinking produce adults who cannot resist manipulation. A designer quit not because AI replaced her work but because AI-generated feedback eroded her own creative judgment. The pattern: AI amplifies whatever intent you bring — those who use it to think deeper get smarter, those who use it to avoid thinking get weaker.
