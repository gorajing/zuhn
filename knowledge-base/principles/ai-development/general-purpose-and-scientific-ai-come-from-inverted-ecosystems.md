Frontier general-purpose AI is 91.58% industry-produced with declining transparency, while most AI foundation models for science come from academia and government through cross-sector international collaborations with open datasets and reproducibility norms. The two ecosystems have opposite governance cultures, incentive structures, and transparency defaults — Earth science datasets come entirely from government and academic sources, while industry dominates commercial AI. 'AI research' is not a monolith, and conclusions about one ecosystem cannot be transferred cleanly to the other without adjusting for these structural differences.
