LLMs training on AI-generated content degrades quality in a downward spiral that nobody is actively solving, while coordinated bot swarms using LLMs manufacture consensus and persistent fake personas at unprecedented scale. The combination of synthetic data contamination and weaponized misinformation means the information ecosystem that models depend on is being actively degraded by the models themselves — a structural problem that gets worse with wider deployment, not better.
