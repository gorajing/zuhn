Treating prompts as code-like instructions rather than search queries is the single highest-leverage shift for AI output quality. Follow-up prompts that reshape output are more effective than crafting one perfect initial prompt. Specific techniques compound: role assignment shifts output posture, step-by-step reasoning forces structured thought, tighter constraints force more creative outputs, and having AI interview you about preferences produces sharper configuration than writing instructions unprompted. This is a distinct skill that can be taught and practiced.
