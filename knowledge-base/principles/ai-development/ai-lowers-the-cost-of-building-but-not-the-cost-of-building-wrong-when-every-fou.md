If AI lets you build faster, it lets every other founder build faster too — a customer who had 2-3 options now has 20-30, making differentiation the real bottleneck. AI-generated code moves the constraint from production speed to decision quality. The temptation to constantly pivot when you can clone a TechCrunch article in an hour is exactly how startups die. 95% of genAI pilots yield zero P&L impact because enterprise tools become static science projects that never adapt to real workflows. The surviving constraint is knowing what to build and for whom, not the ability to build it.
