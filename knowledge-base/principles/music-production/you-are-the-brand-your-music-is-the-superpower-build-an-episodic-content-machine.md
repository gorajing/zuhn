Music marketing in 2026 requires treating yourself as the hero and your music as your superpower for getting attention. Your content feed must feel episodic like a TV show — consistent visual identity so viewers recognize you instantly. Behind-the-scenes content fails for unknown artists because strangers have zero context. The highest-leverage channel is TikTok Live (massive viewer supply, low creator supply) — musicians earn $10K+/month while building thousands of followers per week, getting paid to grow their audience. Remix trending songs to borrow existing demand before you have your own. Release on Fridays with 4-week lead time. The underlying principle: don't promote songs (temporary) — build the machine (permanent).
