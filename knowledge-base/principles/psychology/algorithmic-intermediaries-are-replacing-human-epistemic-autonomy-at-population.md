AI and algorithmic systems increasingly mediate what people see, believe, and know, concentrating epistemic power in whoever controls the intermediary. Engagement-optimized algorithms systematically surface extreme and divisive content, while AI-mediated search replaces direct information access with curated outputs. This creates a new form of reality distortion that operates at civilizational scale, far beyond what any previous propaganda system could achieve.
