The Calvin Cycle keeps 5 of 6 G3P molecules just to regenerate starting material; photosynthesis chains energy through multiple lossy intermediaries; ATP exists solely as a currency layer between raw fuel and usable work. Any multi-step value-extraction system has a reinvestment tax where the majority of gross throughput is consumed by cycle maintenance, making the visible output a small fraction of total activity. Ignoring this reinvestment overhead leads to systematically overestimating the true yield of any complex process.
