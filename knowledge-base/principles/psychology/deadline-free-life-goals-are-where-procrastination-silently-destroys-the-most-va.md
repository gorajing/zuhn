The Panic Monster -- the only force that reliably overrides the Instant Gratification Monkey -- never wakes up for goals without external deadlines, allowing procrastination on the most important life decisions to extend indefinitely. Deadline-based procrastination is contained and survivable, but non-deadline procrastination on career changes, relationships, health, and creative work accumulates into the primary source of long-term regret. Making finite time concrete through tools like a Life Calendar (one box per week of a 90-year life) can serve as an artificial Panic Monster for these unstructured goals.
