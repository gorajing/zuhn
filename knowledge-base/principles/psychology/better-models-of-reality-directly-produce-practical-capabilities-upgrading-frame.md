Upgrading from Newton's to Einstein's model of gravity wasn't just philosophical — it enabled GPS, accurate satellites, and Uber. Mathematical training is a perception technology that makes the world more complex, beautiful, and visible. Physics forces intuitions that apply at different distances and scales — a meta-skill transferable to any complex domain. Einstein's greatest leap was conceptual, not mathematical — connecting the undetectability of gravity to spacetime curvature required creative reasoning. The practical output of better understanding is not incidental; it's the primary return on investment.
