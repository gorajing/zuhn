Exponential growth always saturates when the growth medium is exhausted. The logistic S-curve is the correct mental model for any process with limiting factors — populations, startups, epidemics, content reach. The growth factor (today/yesterday) is the superior early-warning metric: near 1 means you're at the inflection point and total will roughly double then plateau.
