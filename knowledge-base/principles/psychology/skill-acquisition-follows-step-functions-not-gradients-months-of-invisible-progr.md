Destin practiced the backwards bicycle daily for 8 months with no visible progress, then suddenly could ride it one day. His 6-year-old son achieved the same in 2 weeks (~16x faster from neural plasticity). Even after mastery, a single distraction crashed his brain back to the old algorithm. The pattern: skill rewiring requires sustained practice through a plateau with no feedback, then transitions discretely. Knowledge of the rule doesn't help — only accumulated reps do.
