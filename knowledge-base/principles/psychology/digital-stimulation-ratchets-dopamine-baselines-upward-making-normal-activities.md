Every notification, scroll, and TikTok video trains the brain to expect that level of stimulation as normal, progressively making books, conversations, and deep work feel intolerably boring. Brain imaging confirms behavioral addictions from tech activate the same circuits as drug addictions. Heavy screen use doesn't just exploit weak impulse control — it actively stunts the brain's ability to develop that control, creating a vicious cycle. The 30-minute screen time limit doesn't produce 30 minutes of impact because craving and mental preoccupation persist all day.
