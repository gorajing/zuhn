Individual resilience factors like optimism, emotional suppression, or mindfulness each have only small predictive effects because their usefulness depends entirely on the situation. The real meta-skill is the iterative loop: narrow the problem to what is bothering you right now, choose a strategy from your toolbox, monitor whether it works, and switch if it does not. Shifting self-talk from declarative statements to diagnostic questions activates this flexible problem-solving mode.
