Thylakoid membranes, batteries, pricing arbitrage, and expertise moats all follow the same pattern: create an imbalance across a boundary, then harvest the equalization through a controlled channel. Useful work comes from engineered imbalances, not raw energy. Recognizing this pattern lets you identify leverage in any domain by asking: where is there a gradient, and who controls the channel?
