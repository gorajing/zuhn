Hedonic adaptation through social comparison ensures that no level of absolute wealth produces lasting satisfaction, because each wealth tier exposes you to a richer reference group. This operates from developer salary ladders to $50M+ net worth individuals who feel inadequate next to billionaires. Algorithmic feeds have supercharged this by replacing neighbor-comparison with influencer-comparison at global scale.
