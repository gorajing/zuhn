Avoidance and procrastination are not defeated by forcing action or summoning willpower. Instead, the reliable mechanism is to keep lowering the scale of the first step until you find a version you are genuinely willing to do right now. This works because the barrier to action is starting, not continuing — once the routine begins, the same cue-reward loop that maintained the old habit carries the new one. The complementary move is subtraction: cutting commitments, options, and information rather than adding motivational techniques.
