The same raw energy that fuels addiction, anger, and avoidance can power world-class performance when routed through disciplined structure. The mechanism is not suppression but redirection: keep the cue and the emotional charge while swapping the routine, lower the bar until the first step is actually tolerable, and turn toward rather than away from the dreaded task. Creativity, competitive drive, and violent intensity all survive — and often thrive — when given a structured channel.
