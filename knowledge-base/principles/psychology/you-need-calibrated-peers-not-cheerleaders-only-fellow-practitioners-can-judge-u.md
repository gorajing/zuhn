Indiscriminate encouragers teach you to discount praise. The right creative peers are fellow practitioners doing similarly ambitious work who can distinguish ugly ducklings from genuinely bad ideas. Great work clusters historically around institutions that concentrate these calibrated peers, making peer selection one of the highest-leverage decisions.
