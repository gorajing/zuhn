People overwhelmingly accept whatever option is presented as the default, whether that is a job path, a cup size, a subscription, or a political stance. The mind eliminates options pre-consciously based on framing, anchoring, and environmental nudges, meaning the architecture of choice determines outcomes more than the chooser's stated preferences. Most life trajectories are accumulated defaults, not deliberate decisions.
