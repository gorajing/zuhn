Effective persuasion requires mapping each stakeholder's incentives, concerns, and decision process before crafting your message. The same proposal pitched to different audiences must be reframed in each audience's own terms -- their pain points, their language, their revealed behavior. Self-oriented arguments (what you care about, what excites you) are noise; audience-oriented arguments (what they already want, what they already do with inadequate tools) are signal.
