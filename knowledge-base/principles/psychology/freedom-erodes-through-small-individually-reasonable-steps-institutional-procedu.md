Freedom is typically eroded through incremental steps that each seem too minor to resist, not dramatic confrontation. Institutional procedures that create the threat they respond to form closed loops where escalation appears internally justified. Unchecked institutional misconduct escalates predictably because lack of consequences signals that aggression is safe. National security framing activates fear circuits that override nuanced engagement.
