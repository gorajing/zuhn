There is no right answer in creative work or relationships, but total commitment to execution makes your choice become the right answer. Committing in advance to principled behavior yields better outcomes than optimizing each decision in isolation. Every love ends in death or divorce — go all in, because the joy of full commitment vastly outweighs the guaranteed pain. You can only do your best work doing what you want, the way you think it should be done. Reducing reverence while maintaining respect is what enables courage.
