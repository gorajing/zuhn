The subconscious drives roughly 90% of decision-making, and it is accessed through the body, not through conceptual understanding. Locating an emotion physically and feeling it for minutes diffuses its charge permanently; musicians at the highest level literally perceive reality through different sensory channels; environments where death is commonplace paradoxically produce deeper joy because mortality is processed somatically rather than intellectually. Transformation happens at the layer where thought dissolves but awareness persists.
