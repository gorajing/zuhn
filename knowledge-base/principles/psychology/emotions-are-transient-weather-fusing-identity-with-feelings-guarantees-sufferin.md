Watch any emotion long enough and it changes; annoyance now will not exist an hour from now. Pain is inevitable but the stories we construct about it — how unfair, how permanent — are optional suffering we inflict on ourselves. Separating the act of making a mistake from the identity of being a failure, and separating bad behavior from bad person, enables coaching instead of punishment. The optimal response to emotional turbulence is observation without fusion, not suppression or indulgence.
