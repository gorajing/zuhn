Emotional engagement with a subject accelerates learning 3-4x compared to detached study, and curiosity uniquely compounds because satisfying it generates more questions rather than satiation. Following genuine curiosity beats chasing hot fields because sustained obsession is required to develop frontier-level judgment. The implication is that optimizing for intrinsic interest rather than strategic career positioning produces better long-term outcomes.
