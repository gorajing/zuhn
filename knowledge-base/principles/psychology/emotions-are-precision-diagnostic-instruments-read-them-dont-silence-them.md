Each negative emotion carries a specific, actionable diagnostic message: regret says 'do it now,' jealousy reveals hidden desires, anger provides energy for change, and deep resistance signals that something matters deeply. The subconscious drives 90% of decision-making, and only by exploring it -- locating emotions physically in the body, feeling them for minutes rather than bypassing -- can you diffuse their charge and stop repeating destructive patterns. Loss and mortality strip away 'mendacities' to reveal what actually matters. The fastest emotional shift comes from removing what pulls you down, not adding new practices. Meaning-making is the brain's default mode, not an opt-in process, which means emotional signals are always broadcasting whether you listen or not.
