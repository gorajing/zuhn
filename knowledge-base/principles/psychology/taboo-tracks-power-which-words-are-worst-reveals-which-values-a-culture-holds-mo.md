Religious oaths were most taboo when the church held power, bodily words under Victorian propriety, and identity slurs now that social equality is the dominant moral framework. Taboo words become taboo through class-based avoidance as higher-status groups abandon terms associated with lower status, creating a euphemism treadmill. AI slop is being stigmatized through the same sociolinguistic mechanism.
