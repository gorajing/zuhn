What appear to be fixed personality traits — charisma, patience, confidence, people skills — are actually clusters of small, observable, teachable behaviors like smiling on entry, remembering names, and not interrupting. The practical implication is to break abstract success traits into the smallest observable behaviors until instructions are followable at your current skill level, then train each micro-behavior through deliberate repetition.
