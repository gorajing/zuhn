Humanity's capacity to affect planetary-scale change has grown exponentially across millennia, but the moral and philosophical wisdom required to wield that power has barely improved since ancient thinkers. The same pattern repeats across domains: those who control transformative technology restrict access to maintain power, from ancient priests hoarding literacy to modern companies hoarding AI. Brain-reading technology, AI intermediaries, and autonomous systems are advancing faster than any governance or ethical framework can track, creating a widening gap between capability and discernment.
