Cognitive scripts discovered in 1979 research efficiently handle routine decisions but silently take over important life choices — career, partner, identity — when left unexamined. The Epic Script (find your purpose) stigmatizes simple content lives, the Sequel Script (match your past) privileges narrative continuity over genuine fit, and the subconscious drives an estimated 90% of decision-making. The 250-year romantic script that love should follow pure instinct has produced centuries of relationship failure masquerading as liberation.
