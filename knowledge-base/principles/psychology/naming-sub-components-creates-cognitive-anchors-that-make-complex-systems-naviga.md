Complex systems like photosynthesis become learnable only when each intermediate product and sub-process gets a distinct name that serves as a cognitive anchor. CrashCourse succeeds by giving every sub-process a character; Vsauce makes Banach-Tarski accessible through chains of named analogies. The principle extends beyond biology: any overwhelming cascade becomes manageable when you can name its parts and talk about them independently.
