  widely cited autophagy — and charcoal, insoluble fiber, and avoiding resistant
  starches can achieve the same effect without fasting's cortisol cost.
  Autophagy occurs continuously through ubiquitination regardless of feeding
  state, and some nutrients like glucose actually trigger autophagy signals,
  undermining the 'autophagy requires fasting' narrative. Critically, fasting

  are especially vulnerable. The absence of food is powerful medicine but
  context-dependent: 1-2 meals per day lowers insulin more than food quality
  changes alone, prolonged fasting creates hostile environments for cancer
  cells, yet frequent small meals — the standard diabetic advice — worsen blood
  sugar by keeping insulin chronically elevated.

The real mechanism of fasting benefit is reducing gut endotoxin (LPS), not the widely cited autophagy — and charcoal, insoluble fiber, and avoiding resistant starches can achieve the same effect without fasting's cortisol cost. Autophagy occurs continuously through ubiquitination regardless of feeding state, and some nutrients like glucose actually trigger autophagy signals, undermining the 'autophagy requires fasting' narrative. Critically, fasting with a cortisol-to-DHEA ratio above 0.5 is counterproductive — it accelerates catabolism, suppresses immunity, and may promote cancer growth; older adults are especially vulnerable. The absence of food is powerful medicine but context-dependent: 1-2 meals per day lowers insulin more than food quality changes alone, prolonged fasting creates hostile environments for cancer cells, yet frequent small meals — the standard diabetic advice — worsen blood sugar by keeping insulin chronically elevated.
