Atherosclerosis requires multiple factors including LDL oxidation, inflammatory signaling, and immune activation — simply having high LDL is insufficient to cause plaque. When stratified by metabolic health markers, the LDL-heart disease correlation either massively attenuates or completely disappears. Heart surgeons increasingly recognize metabolic health markers as more important than LDL for cardiac risk. Statin drug benefits are overstated using relative risk reduction while absolute risk reduction is only 1-2%, and side effects including muscle pain, cognitive decline, and diabetes risk are systematically underreported. Dietary guidelines quietly reversed their cholesterol and saturated fat warnings after decades of wrong advice. Cholesterol is the bandage on endothelial damage, not the wound — and K2, nattokinase, and tocotrienols can redirect calcium from arteries back into bones without statins.
