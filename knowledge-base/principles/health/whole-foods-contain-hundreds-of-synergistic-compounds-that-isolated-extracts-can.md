Whole fruit containing fructose with hundreds of bioactive compounds does not cause insulin resistance, while isolated fructose does. Sunlight delivers vitamin D, nitric oxide, infrared healing, and circadian reset simultaneously — no supplement replicates all four. Honey's 300+ bioactive compounds improve insulin sensitivity and testosterone, while isolated sucrose harms both. One cup of raw sauerkraut provides 10x the vitamin C RDA plus natural K2 and diverse probiotics that no supplement matches. Grass-fed beef tallow contains stearic acid, fat-soluble vitamins, and only 1-2% linoleic acid in a matrix that activates mitochondria. The pattern is universal: extracting single compounds from whole foods destroys the synergistic interactions that make them beneficial, and often creates harms the whole food does not.
