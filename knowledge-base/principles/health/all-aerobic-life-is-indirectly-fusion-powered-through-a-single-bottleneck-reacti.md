Solar photons excite chlorophyll electrons, which through an elaborate transport chain produce ATP and NADPH, which fix carbon into glucose, which animals reverse-engineer back into ATP. The entire chain depends on a single reaction — PSII splitting water — which produces oxygen as literal waste. This one molecular event sustains all breathable oxygen and all aerobic metabolism on Earth.
