Seed oil linoleic acid (680-day half-life in cell membranes), endocrine disruptors from home products, microplastics from water bottles, and pesticide residues all have biological persistence that outlasts dietary changes. Cleaning up your diet is the first step, but full cellular turnover takes 2-3 years. During this period, stored toxins continue causing damage, especially during exercise or illness when fat stores are mobilized. Systematic environmental detox (home, water, skincare) must accompany dietary changes.
