Life stores and releases energy through a single recurring pattern: pump protons across a membrane to create a concentration gradient, then harvest that gradient through specialized turbines (ATP synthase). This identical mechanism appears in chloroplast thylakoids, mitochondria, neurons, and nephrons. Understanding the pattern once gives you the template for every major energy system in biology.
