Type 2 diabetes, heart disease, fatty liver, Alzheimer's, PCOS, cancer, hair loss, and obesity share insulin resistance as their common upstream cause. Every eating event spikes insulin, so reducing meal frequency (1-2 meals/day) and carbohydrate intake addresses the root cause across all these 'separate' diseases simultaneously. The medical system's specialty-by-organ approach misses this unified mechanism.
