The vitamin D RDA error (600 IU vs 8895 IU), the Flexner Report's elimination of natural medicine, pharma influence on dietary guidelines, and the antacid-heartburn cycle all follow the same pattern: an institution funded by industry produces guidance that protects industry revenue while harming patient outcomes, and corrections are delayed indefinitely because the correction threatens the funding relationship.
