Nearly 90% of ATP comes from the electron transport chain, making mitochondrial function far more important than glucose availability for energy production. Fructose irreversibly inhibits three mitochondrial enzymes, cumulatively degrading cellular energy over years, while seed oil linoleic acid causes proton leak across the electron transport chain. Ketogenic diets achieve psychiatric remission where 17 medications failed because mitochondria regulate neurotransmitters, hormones, inflammation, and gene expression -- they are the central node, not just the energy factory. Palmer's hypothesis that obesity is the consequence of brain dysfunction rather than a metabolic problem reverses the conventional causal arrow entirely.
