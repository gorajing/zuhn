The lethality of processed food is not any single ingredient but the synergy of refined starch (spikes blood sugar worse than sugar), seed oils (oxidize into toxic aldehydes when heated), and emulsifiers (strip the gut mucosal barrier like detergent). These three mechanisms attack the metabolic, inflammatory, and gut barrier systems simultaneously, explaining why eliminating processed food produces outsized health improvements compared to targeting any single nutrient.
