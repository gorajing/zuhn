The most common objection to nutrient-dense whole food eating — that it is too expensive — is systematically refuted by current retail availability. Costco provides the best value for grass-fed meat, organic eggs, and quality dairy in bulk. Aldi offers grass-fed ground beef, organic eggs, and quality butter at the lowest prices of any national chain. Target now carries grass-fed ground beef, pasture-raised eggs, and organic dairy. Budget animal-based eating using ground beef ($5-8/lb), whole chickens ($1-2/lb), eggs ($3-5/dozen), seasonal fruit, and whole milk provides complete nutrition for $10-15/day. Desiccated organ capsules serve as a practical substitute for fresh liver. The practical barrier to healthy eating is knowledge and habit, not cost.
