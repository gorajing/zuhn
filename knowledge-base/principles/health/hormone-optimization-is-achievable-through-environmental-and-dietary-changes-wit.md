Testosterone (eliminate seed oils + eat cholesterol + sleep + lift + sunlight), estrogen balance (cruciferous vegetables with DIM + zinc for aromatase inhibition + reduce visceral fat), growth hormone (protect deep sleep + fast + avoid late eating), and vitamin D as hormone (sunlight provides 4 mechanisms no supplement replicates). The pattern: hormones respond to environmental signals, not just age. Most hormone 'decline' is environmental disruption (seed oils, endocrine disruptors, poor sleep, high insulin) that is reversible without pharmaceutical replacement.
