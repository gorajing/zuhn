Your home environment exposes you to a constant background load of toxins through non-stick cookware, plastic containers, unfiltered water with microplastics, LED flicker disrupting circadian rhythms, indoor air 2-5x more polluted than outdoor, and wireless EMF radiation adjacent to the brain. Because these exposures are continuous and cumulative while supplements and exercise are intermittent, removing the baseline toxic load produces outsized health returns relative to effort. The intervention is subtractive -- stop poisoning yourself before adding optimizations.
