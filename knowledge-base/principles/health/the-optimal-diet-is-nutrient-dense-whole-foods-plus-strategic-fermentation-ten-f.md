A core diet of eggs, wild salmon/cod liver, grass-fed beef, arugula, sauerkraut, Brazil nuts, extra virgin olive oil, dark chocolate/cacao, quality cheese, and L. reuteri yogurt provides the complete spectrum of macronutrients, micronutrients, probiotics, and protective compounds. Each food was selected for a specific irreplaceable function (eggs for bioavailable protein, arugula for nitric oxide, Brazil nuts for selenium, sauerkraut for vitamin C/K2/probiotics), and together they address every major nutritional gap without supplements.
