Cortisol is not merely a stress marker but a central mediator that disrupts sleep architecture through nocturnal blood sugar crashes, suppresses testosterone and thyroid function, and accelerates biological aging via the cortisol-to-DHEA-S ratio. Many seemingly unrelated symptoms — 3AM waking, low testosterone, keto-induced false energy, and chronic inflammation from social isolation — trace back to unmanaged cortisol as the common upstream cause. Optimizing cortisol is as foundational as diet, yet almost never addressed first.
