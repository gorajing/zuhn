  Multiple insights converge on a specific animal-based dietary template: organ
  meats (or desiccated capsules) for micronutrient density, raw grass-fed dairy
  for odd-chain fatty acids and live enzymes, pastured eggs for choline and DHA,
  grass-fed ground beef as the cheapest quality protein, seasonal fruit and raw
  honey for carbohydrates with bioactive compounds, and bone broth or collagen
  for connective tissue. This template naturally produces balanced macros around
  30-35% each from protein, fat, and carbohydrates. The most common mistake is
  eating too few carbohydrates from fruit and honey, causing elevated cortisol,
  suppressed thyroid, and lower testosterone. Budget implementations run
  $10-15/day at mainstream retailers like Costco, Aldi, and Target, with ground
  beef and desiccated organ capsules as the key cost optimizations.

Multiple insights converge on a specific animal-based dietary template: organ meats (or desiccated capsules) for micronutrient density, raw grass-fed dairy for odd-chain fatty acids and live enzymes, pastured eggs for choline and DHA, grass-fed ground beef as the cheapest quality protein, seasonal fruit and raw honey for carbohydrates with bioactive compounds, and bone broth or collagen for connective tissue. This template naturally produces balanced macros around 30-35% each from protein, fat, and carbohydrates. The most common mistake is eating too few carbohydrates from fruit and honey, causing elevated cortisol, suppressed thyroid, and lower testosterone. Budget implementations run $10-15/day at mainstream retailers like Costco, Aldi, and Target, with ground beef and desiccated organ capsules as the key cost optimizations.
