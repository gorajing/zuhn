Heart palpitations (potassium/magnesium), anxiety (B1), burning feet (B1/B12), depression (vitamin D), hair loss (zinc/selenium), nighttime urination (electrolytes), kidney stones (magnesium), and insomnia (magnesium) are routinely treated as diseases requiring specialist workups when they are nutrient deficiencies costing pennies to correct. The pattern: a depleted nutrient creates dysfunction that mimics a disease, leading to expensive diagnostics and pharmaceuticals that treat downstream symptoms while the deficiency persists.
