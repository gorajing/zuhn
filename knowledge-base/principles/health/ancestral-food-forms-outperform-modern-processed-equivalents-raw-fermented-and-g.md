Raw cheese retains enzymes and K2 that pasteurization kills. Grass-fed tallow provides CLA and fat-soluble vitamins that seed oils lack. Whole food vitamin C includes bioflavonoids that synthetic ascorbic acid misses. Raw sauerkraut has live probiotics that shelf-stable versions don't. Organ meats provide nutrient density that muscle meat cannot match. The pattern: industrial food processing systematically strips the most valuable components (enzymes, bacteria, heat-sensitive vitamins, structural cofactors) while retaining calories and shelf life.
