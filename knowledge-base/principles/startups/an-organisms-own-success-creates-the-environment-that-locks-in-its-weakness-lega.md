RuBisCo is the most abundant protein on Earth at 40 billion tons, yet it confuses O2 for CO2 half the time. Plants' own light reactions changed the atmosphere to make this flaw permanent — and 450 million years of built infrastructure makes replacing it impossible. The same dynamic traps companies on legacy systems: your success reshapes the environment in ways that make your original compromises permanent.
