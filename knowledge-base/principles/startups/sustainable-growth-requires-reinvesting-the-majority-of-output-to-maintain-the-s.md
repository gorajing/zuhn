The Calvin Cycle produces 6 G3P molecules but reinvests 5 to keep running, netting only 17%. Healthy businesses mirror this biological pattern: most output must be recycled into maintaining operations, talent pipelines, and infrastructure. Founders who extract too much margin too early starve the regenerative loop that sustains growth.
