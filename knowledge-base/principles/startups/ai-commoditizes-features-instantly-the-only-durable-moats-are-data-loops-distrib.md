When AI makes building cheap and copying trivial, code and features cease to be competitive advantages. The survivors are companies that embed into workflows so deeply that ripping them out is costlier than staying, accumulate proprietary data that improves with usage, or own distribution channels that competitors cannot replicate. Features get cloned in weeks; these structural moats compound over years.
