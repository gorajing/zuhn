The belief that pursuing multiple opportunities hedges risk is a fallacy. Depth in one domain compounds returns far faster than breadth across many, and the opportunity cost of starting a second venture is the continued growth trajectory of the first. Millionaires built wealth by going all-in on one stream, then diversified to preserve it -- never the reverse.
