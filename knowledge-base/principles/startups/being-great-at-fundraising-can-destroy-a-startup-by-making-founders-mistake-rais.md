Curate investors who share your long-term vision rather than taking whatever money is available. Prolonged investor indecision is more lethal than outright rejection because it consumes months while the company stagnates. The fundraising skill trap: founders who raise easily stop building, and the company optimizes for investor narratives instead of customer value.
