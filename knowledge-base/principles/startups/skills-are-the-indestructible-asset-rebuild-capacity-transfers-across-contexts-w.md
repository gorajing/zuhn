After losing everything, Hormozi made $100K the next month, proving the skills were the real asset. A skilled wealth-creator dropped on a random street would rebuild within 5-10 years because the capability transfers. Two complementary skills compound disproportionately, and investing $10K in human capital beats $10K in the S&P 500 at early stages. The corollary is that ignorance is not a one-time cost but a recurring debt that compounds every period you do not learn.
