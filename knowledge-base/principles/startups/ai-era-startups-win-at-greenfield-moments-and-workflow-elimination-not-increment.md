AI-native startups win most reliably at greenfield moments where customers must switch systems anyway, or by eliminating workflows entirely rather than augmenting them. Incumbents will always win copilot implementations because they have the data and distribution. The startup opportunity is in creating previously-impossible value, not preserving old revenue models with AI wrappers. Adding a GPT wrapper to your existing product is a dead end.
