We only study winners, but losers often executed the identical playbook -- luck, timing, and market randomness separated them. Duncan Watts' Music Lab proved the same song ranks #1 or #40 depending on social dynamics, and power-law markets mean the seventh player trends toward zero, not a stable fraction. Founders must actively seek disconfirming evidence and failed case studies instead of pattern-matching exclusively to survivors.
