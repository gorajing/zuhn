Costco's maze layout and IKEA's DIY model make customers feel like active participants who earned their reward rather than passive consumers. When companies present cost-cutting measures as features — deal-hunting excitement, exclusivity, accomplishment — they create emotional bonds that pure convenience competitors cannot match.
