65% of startups fail from co-founder interpersonal tensions, yet almost no one tracks relationship health as a KPI or treats it with the same rigor as product or fundraising. Conflict is co-created: one founder's withdrawal provokes the other's over-functioning, avoidance builds pressure that explodes during scaling, and every dispute traces back to three root causes -- power, care, and recognition. The proven interventions are containment (schedule specific times for disagreements instead of reacting), ownership (acknowledging your role stops escalation cycles), and treating the founding relationship as the load-bearing structure it actually is.
