The highest-value AI market entries target lagging analog processes where existing tools still rely on docs and spreadsheets. In emerging markets, targeting broken essential industries provides both large market size and natural urgency that overcomes adoption friction. Nearly 10% of YC W26 builds in healthcare and 4% in legal tech, targeting regulatory complexity as a feature for AI-native defensibility. High mobile penetration plus low workflow digitization signals a ripe market entry window. Evaluate potential niches on pain severity and willingness to pay, then focus exclusively on the high-pain/high-willingness quadrant.
