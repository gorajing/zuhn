AI and no-code tools collapsed the cost of startup operations so dramatically that the founder-VC coupling quietly decoupled. The shift only becomes visible when funding dries up and founders discover they don't need it. This gives founders structural leverage: higher equity retention, more board control, and the freedom to build resilient 'camel' startups instead of unprofitable unicorns.
