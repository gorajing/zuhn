The algorithm is a free A/B testing engine: post organically, measure what resonates, then amplify only proven winners through paid channels. Rella never runs ads on purpose-built ad content, only boosting posts that already performed organically. Combining free launch access for social proof with delayed paid activation, matched spending credits for new ad accounts, and rigorous per-channel cost-per-customer tracking creates a system where you never bet on unvalidated creative.
