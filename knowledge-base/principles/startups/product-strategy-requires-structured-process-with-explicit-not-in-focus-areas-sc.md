Effective product strategy is not a document but a structured 8-12 week process. Strategic pillars should be scored across four dimensions: expected impact, certainty, clarity of levers, and differentiation. Explicit 'not in focus' areas force real tradeoffs that vague prioritization cannot. Aspirational 5-10 year strategy should run in parallel with tactical strategy, using concept prototypes to test distinct future scenarios rather than extrapolating linearly.
