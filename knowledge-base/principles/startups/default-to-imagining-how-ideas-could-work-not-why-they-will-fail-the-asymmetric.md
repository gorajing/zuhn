In a power-law world, the cost of prematurely killing a winner vastly exceeds the cost of entertaining a loser. Productive idea evaluation requires a trained polarity switch from flaw-finding to possibility-imagining. Silicon Valley's cultural custom of treating early work charitably is not naive optimism but a practiced skill with outsized financial returns, and any community can develop it.
