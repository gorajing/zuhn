Fixed costs like headcount jump in steps, not linearly, and controlling when those steps happen is a key founder lever. Plugging real metrics into an MRR model before hiring prevents premature scaling. Cutting back-half GTM hiring makes current efficiency look great while guaranteeing a growth collapse the following year. Effective scaling requires decomposing revenue targets into weekly unit metrics, tracking burn multiple alongside growth, and planning fully loaded compensation with month-by-month ramp timing.
