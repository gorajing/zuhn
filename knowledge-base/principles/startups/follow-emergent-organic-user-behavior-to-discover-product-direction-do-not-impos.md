The most successful product pivots come from observing how users organically adopt and repurpose your product, then following that signal aggressively. Insta360 discovered their action-camera opportunity by watching skiers and cyclists spontaneously adopt their 360-degree camera. Gamma built AI to solve a 95% drop-off rate and accidentally discovered they had solved the universal blank page problem. Insta360 found that 70%+ of users had content collecting dust because editing was too hard, making editing software their primary moat. The pattern is consistent: watch what users actually do (not what they say), find the unexpected behavior, and build for it.
