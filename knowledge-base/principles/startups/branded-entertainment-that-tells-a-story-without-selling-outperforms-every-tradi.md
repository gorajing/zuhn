Roomies created a comedic series about NYC apartment hunting instead of promoting their rental software and drove millions of views in their target demographic. Podcasts operate on two axes simultaneously -- distribution and credibility -- which is why their returns feel disproportionate. The pattern is consistent across food influencers, content pyramids, and Navy SEAL recruitment after the movie 300: entertainment that inspires or entertains first and markets second creates both attention and trust, while content that markets first achieves neither.
