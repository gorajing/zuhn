Building around a hard-to-access vertical database of record creates stronger competitive positioning than superior features alone. When vertical SaaS bundles financial and operational workflows, it becomes nearly impossible to rip out. Live ops and continuous data-driven iteration build compounding personalization advantages. Replit's decade of integrating editor, database, and hosting into one container exemplifies how deep integration creates moats competitors cannot quickly replicate.
