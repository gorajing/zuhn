Companies that own the entire pipeline from intake to outcome -- not just one step -- generate proprietary data at every stage that public models and single-feature competitors cannot access. Disney (film to parks to merchandise to streaming), LVMH (materials to manufacturing to retail), Intuit (accounting to invoicing to payroll to credit), and Palo Alto Networks (firewall to full-stack security) all demonstrate that vertical integration along the customer workflow creates a self-reinforcing advantage where each layer makes every other layer more defensible.
