Four insights from Coconote's journey to $6.7M ARR converge: conversion
happens when you match the audience's mental model, not when you project
your own.

Use customers' own words: "How would you describe us?" → "Never miss a key
detail" → App Store headline. Frame as solution, not toy: 41M views on a
fun video, terrible conversion. Same product framed as study solution
converted. Target the buyer: students use it, moms pay for it. Content
quality over followers: zero-follower TikTok got 8M views.

The meta-pattern: every failed marketing experiment projected the founder's
model. Every success extracted the customer's model and amplified it.
