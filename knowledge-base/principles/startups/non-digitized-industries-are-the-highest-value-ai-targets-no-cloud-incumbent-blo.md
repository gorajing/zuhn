Legal, manufacturing, construction, and other non-digitized industries have no SaaS incumbent to displace, making entry easier. Markets with insufficient human labor supply offer AI startups a structural advantage because the alternative is no solution at all, not a cheaper human. The combination of greenfield opportunity and genuine labor shortage creates the ideal conditions for AI-native companies to build without facing platform taxation.
