There's a massive gap between $20K seed checks and $2M Series A. Super-angels emerged specifically for the $200K-$600K range. Angel investors can only come from successful founders reinvesting locally — governments can fund VCs but cannot manufacture angels. New investor archetypes fill early-stage vacuums because existing VCs cannot change their embedded risk profiles.
