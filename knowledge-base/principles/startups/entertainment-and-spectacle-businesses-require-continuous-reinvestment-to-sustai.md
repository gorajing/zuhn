Businesses whose value proposition is spectacle or novelty face a structural treadmill: each new attraction, event, or experience generates declining visitor impact over time. Theme parks must continuously invest in new rides. Cruise lines sell tickets at or below cost, then profit from onboard captive-market spending. Nightclub economics are driven by selling status through bottle service and exclusivity rather than actual drinks. The multi-stakeholder entertainment ecosystem (publishers, teams, leagues, broadcasters) collapses catastrophically when any single participant defects, as the esports industry demonstrated when no team or league ever turned a profit despite audiences rivaling traditional sports.
