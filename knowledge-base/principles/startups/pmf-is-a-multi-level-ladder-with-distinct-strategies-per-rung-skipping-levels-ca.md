Product-market fit is not a binary threshold but a four-level sequence: customer-problem fit, problem-solution fit, business-model fit, then scale. Level 1 requires just 3-5 deeply engaged customers. The majority of founders stall at Level 2 where they can find initial customers but cannot generate repeatable demand. Each level takes years and demands different strategies — the 40% 'very disappointed' test and flattening retention curves are the measurable gates between levels.
