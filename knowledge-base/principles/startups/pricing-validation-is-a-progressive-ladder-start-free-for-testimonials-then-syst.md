Start free to collect testimonials and case studies that make subsequent sales dramatically easier. Then systematically reduce discounts in batches of five customers until you reach and exceed full price. High-ticket with few customers is easier to execute than low-ticket at scale. Define your ideal customer as one hyper-specific person to unlock premium pricing. Get to paid users as fast as possible because they tell you the truth about shortcomings — free users optimize for politeness.
