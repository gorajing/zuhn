Revenue scales fastest by restructuring how you charge rather than what you build. The Value Equation (dream outcome x perceived likelihood / time delay x effort) reframes pricing around buyer psychology. Premium tiers targeting different customer archetypes, latency reduction as the strongest purchase motivator, and outcome-based framing (sell the score increase, not tutoring hours) consistently outperform cost-plus pricing. Starting high and iterating down beats chronic underpricing, and experimenting with pricing models (usage-based, recurring, API-based) can multiply revenue without any product change.
