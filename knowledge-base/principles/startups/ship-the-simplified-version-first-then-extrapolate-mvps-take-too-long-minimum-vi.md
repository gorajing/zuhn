Boston Dynamics reduced complex locomotion to a bouncing pogo stick. Wikipedia switched from a 7-stage academic review that produced nothing in 2 years to open editing that produced more in 2 weeks. Pincus argues MVP takes too long to fail — founders should test minimum viable ideas and build failure machines. Ship the game editor alongside the game (ZZT/Epic pattern) to let users become creators. The pattern: reduce the problem to its simplest testable form, ship that, and let real feedback determine direction. The fastest path to product-market fit is having an audience you can test with, not building in isolation. ElevenLabs' advantage comes from product teams talking directly to research teams — the feedback loop IS the advantage.
