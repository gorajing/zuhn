Breakthrough products become viable not when one technology improves but when multiple independent enabling systems — often developed in unrelated fields — simultaneously reach sufficient maturity. EUV lithography required fusion mirror research plus laser plasma plus precision manufacturing; AI gaming needs inference speed plus cost plus model quality plus media generation. The actionable signal is tracking convergence across multiple prerequisites, not watching any single technology curve.
