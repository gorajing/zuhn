When quitting is not an option, every obstacle becomes a design problem instead of an exit signal. Founders who remove safety nets -- burning ships, refusing to script a fallback career, treating the startup as a complete identity reset rather than a career move -- consistently outperform those who keep one foot in the safe world. The activation energy barrier to startup commitment is the actual filter, not intelligence or resources.
