When technological or market uncertainty is high, portfolio approaches with multiple parallel experiments outperform concentrated bets. The key is pursuing few enough radical bets to maintain conviction and execution quality, sizing them so failure is survivable, while deploying redundant strategies across different media and approaches because no single method survives all failure modes.
