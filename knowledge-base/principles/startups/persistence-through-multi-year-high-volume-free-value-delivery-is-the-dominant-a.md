The primary barrier to audience growth is premature quitting, not talent or strategy. Multi-year daily content output is the only reliable audience-building approach, with the first year functioning as skill development rather than growth. Sustained high-volume free value delivery builds trust capital that converts into business opportunities at rates impossible through cold outreach, and durable growth comes from word-of-mouth driven by consistently high quality, not paid acquisition.
