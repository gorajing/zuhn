Cruises can't compete on travel speed (8 days vs 8 hours by plane), so they compete on water parks, Broadway shows, and bottomless pizza — the spectacle IS the product. Steakhouses dominate the top 100 highest-grossing restaurants because customers arrive expecting to spend $50-100 on a predictable premium experience. Nightclubs make money from men buying $1,000 tables to impress women, with promoters as invisible infrastructure manufacturing demand. Haunted houses gross 7+ figures in just 4-6 weekends by manufacturing intensity that transcends the underlying commodity of 'being scared.'
