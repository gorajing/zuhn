YC's best success predictor is whether founders consistently ship small increments between check-ins. Teams that got new things done at every check-in were the strongest signal of eventual success, and the gap between high and low performing teams is defined by speed from idea to deployment, not by budget or headcount. In fast-moving markets, a 95/5 execution-to-vision ratio beats the traditional 70/30 because strategies expire faster than implementation cycles.
