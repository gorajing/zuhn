Daily multi-hour cross-functional calls where every discipline presents constraints together catch trade-offs that organic communication systematically misses. Shared visual frameworks like the Business Model Canvas eliminate translation overhead between departments. The cost of these rituals feels excessive but is far cheaper than the compounding misalignment that silos produce at scale.
