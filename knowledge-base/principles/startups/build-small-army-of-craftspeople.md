Three insights reveal that creator relationship quality matters more than
scale. Hire direct, not agencies — the arbitrage is already gone. Keep 5-10
creators max and meet each 1:1 to share what converts. And don't assume
viral UGC works as paid ads — organic and paid are different crafts requiring
different craftspeople.

This mirrors the Composition Over Monolith Principle: small specialized
units outperform large undifferentiated ones, whether in AI pipelines or
content teams.
