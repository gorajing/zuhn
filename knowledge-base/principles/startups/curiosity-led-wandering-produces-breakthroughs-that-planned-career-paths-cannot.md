Linguistics to NLP to chip design produced AlphaChip; 6,000 hours of EverQuest produced Overwatch; Bezos leaving physics found lateral-thinking invention. Planned paths optimize for legible progress while curiosity optimizes for surprising intersections that create entirely new categories.
