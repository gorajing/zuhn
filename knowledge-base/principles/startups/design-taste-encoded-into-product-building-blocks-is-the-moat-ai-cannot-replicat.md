When AI commoditizes functional capability, the surviving differentiator is opinionated design taste baked into the product's fundamental building blocks. Gamma built their moat by making one-third of their team designers who crafted building blocks that AI selects from. Apple's 48-hour design review rhythm forced constant refinement that produced best-in-class products through taste-based judgment calls, not A/B tests. Products that express genuine personality create emotional connections that functional superiority alone cannot match. This taste advantage compounds through thousands of iterations and cannot be shortcut.
