Every technology eventually hits a commoditization inflection point where differentiation must shift away from the tech itself. What wins long-term is always the thing that accumulates invisibly because the impressive-looking variable commoditizes. Decades of accumulated context, proprietary infrastructure investments, and operational complexity that competitors avoid all create durable defensibility through accumulated expertise that no amount of capital can fast-track.
