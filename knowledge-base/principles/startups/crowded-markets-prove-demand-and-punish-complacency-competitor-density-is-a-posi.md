A crowded market proves demand exists and no solution is good enough, while competitor-free markets are a red flag. Texas has the most BBQ spots per capita but lower failure rates than less competitive states. Twitter makes AI agent competition feel intense, but the market is so huge companies rarely encounter actual competitors. Well-funded startups are often too busy spending money to ship, making the landscape far less threatening than it appears.
