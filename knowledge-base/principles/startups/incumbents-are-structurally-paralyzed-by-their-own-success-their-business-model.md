Large organizations compete against their own internal decay curve, not against you. Channel conflict, bonus-driven executives who optimize extraction over building, committee design processes, and the innovator's dilemma all mean incumbents will protect profitable lines even when survival requires cannibalizing them. The same focus that builds a monopoly prevents the company from destroying it.
