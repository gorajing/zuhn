Products that sell themselves through user adoption still need salespeople to navigate enterprise deployment, competitive evaluations, and organizational buying processes. The pattern repeats across Slack, Dropbox, and ChatGPT Enterprise: build consumer-quality tools that spread through individual users, then deploy consultative reps to help organizations handle security, control, scalability, and procurement. Free trials should be replaced with annual agreements plus 30-60 day cancellation windows to get real commitment. Technical founders who sell also filter impossible requests before they reach engineering, preventing wasted cycles in novel product categories.
