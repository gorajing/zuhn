One A+ hire at $170K outperforms five B-players at $100K each in knowledge work. To scale without working more, accept lower short-term profitability to hire people who pull the business beyond what you could do alone. At scale, recruiting needs the same metrics-driven rigor as customer acquisition. If you lead an industry, you must expand the entire market, not just compete for existing share.
