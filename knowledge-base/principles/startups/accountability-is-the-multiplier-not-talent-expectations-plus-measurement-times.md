50% of businesses fail in 2 years because leaders cannot hold teams accountable -- the failure is internal culture, not external markets. If your competitor's team member supports $400K in revenue and yours supports $200K, accountability is the gap, not talent or strategy. The formula is Expectations + Measurement x Reinforcement = Accountability: without reinforcement, expectations and measurement produce zero. Leaders who stall on feedback to gather 'objective evidence' let emotional resentment compound, and the readiness paradox (90% of CXOs claim best-in-class tech while 44% of infrastructure is end-of-life) shows how accountability gaps hide behind self-assessment.
