Self-sabotage, financial avoidance, the martyr archetype, and clinging to bad situations all stem from the same mechanism: the ego protecting itself from perceived threats to identity, self-worth, or acceptance. Burnout worn as a badge costs $300K/year in lost high-earning years, cognitive depletion from misaligned work makes side-building impossible, and the 'breakthrough moment' of clarity never arrives — you must act despite fear. The fix is identifying the hidden core need your coping behavior protects, then replacing it with tiny daily actions (15 min/day) that expand your comfort zone without triggering nervous system rebellion.
