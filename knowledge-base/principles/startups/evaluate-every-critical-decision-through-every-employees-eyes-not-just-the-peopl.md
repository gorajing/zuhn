The true measure of a leader is the quality of decisions on ordinary days, not crisis performance. Novice leaders derive identity from being busy and indispensable, which caps team capability. Leaders who hire weaker people to stay the smartest lower talent density. The pattern: leadership scales by making your people better, not by being better than your people.
