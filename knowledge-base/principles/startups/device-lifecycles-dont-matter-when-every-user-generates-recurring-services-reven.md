Apple embraced longer device lifecycles because every iPhone user still generates services revenue through apps, subscriptions, and licensing deals. BuzzFeed ($1.7B to penny stock) and VICE (bankruptcy) prove that views without subscription revenue are worth nothing. Shrinkflation generates 3-5% revenue growth for years but hits a ceiling when customers stop buying. The durable model is recurring revenue from an installed base, not one-time transactions.
