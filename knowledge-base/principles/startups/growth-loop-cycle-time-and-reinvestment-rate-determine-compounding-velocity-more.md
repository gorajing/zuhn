A growth loop completing in 30 minutes compounds exponentially faster than one taking 30 days, making cycle time the primary optimization target. Five interconnected metrics -- K-factor, cycle time, drop-off rate, loop retention, and reinvestment rate -- form the diagnostic system for loop health. Copying competitor features without understanding the underlying loop mechanics and timing conditions produces poor results because loops are systems, not features.
