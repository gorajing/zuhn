PayPal took 25-30% of eBay power sellers in months, Facebook went 0 to 60% at Harvard in 10 days, then both expanded concentrically. Starting with a niche so small that big companies won't bother creates a stronghold from which to expand, and the narrower the initial market, the faster you achieve the density that creates network effects and word-of-mouth.
