Pre-PMF sales is fundamentally entrepreneurial — it requires the founder's vision, domain expertise, and conviction that no hired rep can replicate. A repeatable sales motion means a non-founder can sell and close with a known ICP, pitch, lead sources, and predictable funnel before a sales leader is hired. Founders who cannot build their own product lack the intuition to sell it, and those who delegate selling before packing their calendar with calls and learning every objection will never successfully train their first seller. The CEO's number one job is sales — selling to customers, employees, investors, and partners above everything else.
