Industries that require publishers, talent, leagues, broadcasters, and sponsors to all invest simultaneously are structurally fragile because withdrawal by any one group cascades through the entire ecosystem. Esports drew audiences rivaling traditional sports but collapsed when broadcast deals never materialized at scale, triggering publisher pullback, team revenue loss, player departure, and sponsor flight in sequence. When the product is inseparable from individual talent who can easily leave, no amount of capital or brand can hold the ecosystem together.
