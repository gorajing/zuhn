Costco's membership fee, warehouse layout, and bulk-only format appear to be customer-hostile barriers but actually select for affluent households ($100K income) and business buyers who generate disproportionate revenue. The mechanism works because competitors who see 'problems' won't copy them, creating an unreplicable moat from seemingly negative attributes.
