The fastest path to product-market fit is shipping the smallest possible version, watching real users interact with it silently, and letting their behavior — not your roadmap — decide what gets built next. Adding features to a product nobody uses makes diagnosis harder, not adoption easier. Narrowing your target audience to a painful niche (ophthalmology patients, not 'all Koreans') and resisting horizontal expansion compounds focus into revenue.
