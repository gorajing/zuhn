The CEO role is defined by two irreplaceable functions: setting product vision and communicating it to the team. This requires the ability to zoom from strategy to product detail in seconds, not sustained deep work (which drains the same finite resource as leadership). Easy decisions never reach the CEO desk, so experience matters most for navigating close calls. The highest-performing CEOs develop firsthand understanding of every function, focus only on CEO-only tasks during crises, and build teams that can run without them -- proving the multiplier is working.
