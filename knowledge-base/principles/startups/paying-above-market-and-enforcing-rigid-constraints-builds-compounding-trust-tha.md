Costco pays double the retail average and gets triple the productivity per employee, sustaining 90% retention through a 15% max markup rule that forces operational innovation. McDonald's demands more from franchisees than competitors yet attracts $1-2M investments because constraints signal safety. The pattern is counterintuitive: higher pay plus stricter rules produces better economics than lower pay with loose standards.
