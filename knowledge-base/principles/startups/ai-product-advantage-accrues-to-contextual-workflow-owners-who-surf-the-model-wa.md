Competing with OpenAI and Anthropic on models is capital-intensive and futile — the winning strategy is building applications on top of improving models so that every foundation-model upgrade automatically makes your product better. The unsolved AI product challenge is retention and daily habituation, not capability. Enterprise AI will evolve as composite systems with guardrails and explainability layers, not monolithic AGI, which means the builders who own the workflow context and the trust architecture will capture the value.
