Most founder-customer conversations generate false positives because questions are designed to confirm existing assumptions. Friends give polite feedback, users say 'yes that sounds great' without switching, and interviewees give aspirational answers to please the interviewer. Structured de-biasing techniques — never mentioning your solution, asking about past behavior not future intent, focusing on jobs-to-be-done rather than your product — are required to extract truthful signal.
