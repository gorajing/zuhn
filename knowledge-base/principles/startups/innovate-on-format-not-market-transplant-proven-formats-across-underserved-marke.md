Markets (topics people care about) rarely change and can't be invented. Formats (creative vessels for delivering value) are born daily. The highest-leverage strategy is to identify a format proven in Market A and apply it to Market B where nobody has used it yet. Write proven formats on one axis, all possible markets on the other — empty cells are your blue ocean. Channels with 3,000-5,000 subscribers are hitting 2-3 million views by applying this formula. This works because format-market fit, not follower count, determines algorithmic reach.
