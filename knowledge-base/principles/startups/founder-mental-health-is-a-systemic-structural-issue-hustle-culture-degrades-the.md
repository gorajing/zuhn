72% of startup founders experience mental health issues, and productivity declines sharply after 50 hours/week. Grinding 18-hour days degrades the judgment quality that actually determines startup outcomes, making hustle culture self-defeating. Running out of money is the immediate cause of startup death, but demoralization and loss of focus are the underlying killers. Recovery periods are essential inputs to sustained creativity, not downtime to minimize. People who are having fun sustain crazy levels of effort for years; those who are not eventually burn out and quit.
