RuBisCo is a terrible enzyme that conquered the planet because it was first, and now 40 billion tons persist because switching is harder than coping. Photosynthesis, x86, and QWERTY all demonstrate that being adequate first creates path dependency so deep that objectively superior alternatives cannot displace the incumbent. The strategic implication is that speed to adequate beats delay to optimal.
