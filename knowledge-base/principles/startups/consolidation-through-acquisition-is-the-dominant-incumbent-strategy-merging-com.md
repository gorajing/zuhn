Rockefeller consolidated 90% of oil refining before expanding vertically. Morgan merged fragmented railroads into monopolies. Facebook bought Instagram for $1B and WhatsApp for $19B to eliminate competitors. The historical pattern is clear: incumbents build monopolies by absorbing threats rather than outbuilding them, and antitrust law that only measures consumer prices creates a blind spot for this strategy.
