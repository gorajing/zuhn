As AI and no-code platforms make building easy, competitive advantage shifts from what you can build to how you distribute it and the taste you apply. Software IP is nearly worthless because the field evolves faster than patents protect, while bio/deeptech IP remains genuine. The moat is no longer technical — it is workflow ownership, proprietary data from serving customers, and the cultural taste embedded in product decisions.
