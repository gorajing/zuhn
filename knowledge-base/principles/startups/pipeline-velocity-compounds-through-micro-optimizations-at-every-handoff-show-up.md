A 20% improvement in show-up rates produces the same revenue impact as a 20% improvement in close rates but is far easier to achieve. Sub-60-second lead response creates a 4x close rate multiplier. BAMFAM (booking the next meeting before hanging up) prevents the pipeline leak that kills most deals — the one where momentum dies between calls. Routing best leads to best closers can 5x output. These are all infrastructure-level optimizations that multiply the effectiveness of everything else. Most salespeople over-invest in closing technique and under-invest in pipeline mechanics, which is like optimizing your engine while your fuel line leaks.
