The universal product-strategy entry pattern is finding the smallest valuable problem incumbents ignore, nailing the UX for that narrow slice, then expanding to adjacent problems over time. Redpoint's framework demands a narrow high-urgency use case with real user love that naturally expands into larger product surfaces. Starting broad diffuses effort; starting narrow with intense user love creates the foundation for durable expansion.
