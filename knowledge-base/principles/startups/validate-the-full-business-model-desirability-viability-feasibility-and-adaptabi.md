Complete business model validation requires testing four dimensions independently: do customers want it, can it make money, can it be built, and can it adapt. Explore dozens of business model canvases before writing a single business plan. Categorize gains as expected, desired, or surprising to reveal whether your value proposition is merely adequate or genuinely differentiated. Use the Value Proposition Canvas per segment, and create distinct value logic for each customer role and stakeholder.
