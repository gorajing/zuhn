Business model innovation exists on a continuum from incremental to radical, and the best time to search for new models is while the existing one still generates surplus capital. Standalone innovation activities disconnected from strategic follow-on investment are theater. Companies must build structured systems for exploration alongside exploitation, expecting the most valuable output to be unintended.
