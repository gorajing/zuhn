Three insights describe a phase-dependent strategy: early-stage velocity
beats early-stage efficiency. Ship something that "sort of works," charge
from day one ($100K ARR in 45 days), ignore cost optimization until you
have the growth problem, and shelter the idea from smart skeptics who'll
tell you it won't work based on different products.

This connects to the Progressive Refinement Principle: start rough, refine
iteratively. The first filter isn't quality — it's market signal.
