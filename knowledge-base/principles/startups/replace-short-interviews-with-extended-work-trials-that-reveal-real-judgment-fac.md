Traditional interviews optimize for performance, not productivity. Work trials give hiring managers direct observation of actual work output, eliminating the performance-vs-productivity gap. Replace short interviews with 6+ hour shared activities because facades crack after 2-3 hours, revealing the real person. Give trial candidates more work than they can finish to see how they triage, scope, and communicate what they chose to skip. Candidates who invest days in a work trial are simultaneously demonstrating interest level and gaining enough visibility to make informed decisions themselves.
