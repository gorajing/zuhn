Sales objections are cognitive distortions that map to CBT's three core patterns (circumstances, others, self), and the remedy is reframing rather than counter-arguing. Third-party stories (straw man variants using anonymous customers, past clients, or authority figures) deliver uncomfortable truths without personal insult. Labeling prospects with positive identity traits during objection handling creates commitments they feel compelled to honor at close time. The 3A framework (Acknowledge, Associate, Ask) restates concerns, connects them to successful customer behavior, then asks targeted questions — keeping the seller in control without damaging rapport. Answering objections with questions about their questions maintains rapport across repeated asks.
