Plarium maintained startup speed by giving each game studio full autonomy with its own targets and incentives. Nvidia operates like multiple startups with division-level survival pressure. YC broke at 80 startups until they sharded into dedicated groups. The pattern is consistent: autonomous 2-6 person pods or divisions that each own their own roadmap, deployment, and quality control preserve the accountability and speed of small teams within a large organization.
