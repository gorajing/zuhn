Young founders benefit from not knowing what is impossible, having stamina to sustain intensity, and thinking natively in new paradigms. Fresh graduates generate the best new AI ideas precisely because they grew up vibe coding. These advantages decline with age, making the early 20s with no obligations an irreplaceable window. The corollary: investing those years in big-tech ticket-fixing squanders a non-renewable resource.
