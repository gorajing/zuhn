The highest-leverage growth decision is choosing a single narrow category and committing all resources to becoming the definitive player in it. Rella nearly died serving everyone, then hit $2M ARR by narrowing to social media managers only. Intuit discovered that treating small businesses as consumers -- not enterprises -- unlocked product-market fit, while a Brazilian chat app succeeded purely through deep localization. Subscription models amplify this by compounding on the existing base rather than hunting new customers, but only when the product genuinely delivers for its specific audience.
