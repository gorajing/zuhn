As AI answers informational queries directly (over half of Google searches already end without a click), generic top-of-funnel content loses value while being cited by AI models becomes the new distribution. This demands a structural shift: lead with falsifiable claims AI can reference, not keywords it can summarize away. Content rooted in real experience — case studies, implementation guides, product comparisons — retains value because AI cannot replicate lived judgment.
