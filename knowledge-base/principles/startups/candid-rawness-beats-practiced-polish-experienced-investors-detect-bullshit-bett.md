When VCs probe fragile parts, honest acknowledgment with a clear mitigation plan builds trust faster than spinning. Never say 'we're passionate' — audiences ignore or penalize assertions while reading posture and directness. Pony Ma's brutal honesty made investors trust him more than typical overconfident founders.
