Ruthless time discipline and daily exercise are the actual differentiators at the highest founder level, not intelligence or strategy. High-turnaround physical activities like jiu-jitsu and surfing teach the crucial lesson that some things cannot be forced -- a lesson that business cycle times are too long to teach. Competitive backgrounds in any domain build discipline, risk tolerance, and resilience that directly transfer to founding, and ambiguity tolerance compounds over decades of deliberately pushing into uncomfortable situations.
