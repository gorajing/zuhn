Tariffs, sanctions, and sovereignty concerns create enterprise spending categories that were not on any buyer's budget last year. Deeptech startups become sovereignty infrastructure with government-backed demand, and enterprises overwhelmingly buy rather than build AI tools. The companies positioned at the intersection of regulatory mandate and technological capability win without competing on features.
