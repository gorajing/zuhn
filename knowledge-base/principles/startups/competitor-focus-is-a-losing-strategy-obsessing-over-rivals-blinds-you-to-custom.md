Winners focus on customers while competitors focus on winners, creating a vicious cycle where copiers become progressively blind to what actually matters. Competitor tactics are outputs of invisible context you don't have. Deep customer understanding makes competitor tracking largely irrelevant. The company that copies is always one cycle behind, and each cycle spent looking sideways is a cycle not spent looking at customers.
