A week is long enough to validate ideas and ship products; ten years captures true strategic direction. Quarterly planning is a deeply useless middle ground where cohorts do not bake and real outcomes are not visible. Every day of polish without user data is self-deception, and TwoSpace's 600 door-knocks proved that each rejection teaches market objections no survey could reveal. Founder speed is not just iteration velocity -- it is a behavioral signal that predicts fundraising, acquisition, and survival.
