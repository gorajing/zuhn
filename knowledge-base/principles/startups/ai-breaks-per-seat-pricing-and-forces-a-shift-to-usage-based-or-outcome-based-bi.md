Per-seat pricing creates an inverse relationship between AI product effectiveness and revenue: the better your AI works, the fewer seats customers need. Usage-based pricing is displacing seat-based licensing as the dominant SaaS monetization model. The right pricing model depends on what the AI does: agentic AI that replaces human work should charge per outcome, while assistive AI that augments humans should charge per seat or usage tier. Consumer AI companies are achieving over 100% net revenue retention through usage-based pricing on top of subscriptions, a pattern never seen before in consumer software.
