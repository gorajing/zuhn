In consumer-facing markets, emotional resonance, aesthetics, and brand identity consistently outperform raw product quality. Under Armour had objectively better shoes but lost because they looked like clown shoes. Disney charges 2-5x over Six Flags because branded experiences create emotional value. Even in AI, OpenClaw beat well-funded competitors by having personality and not taking itself too seriously. UNIQLO wins not by being cheapest but by proactively exceeding design expectations.
