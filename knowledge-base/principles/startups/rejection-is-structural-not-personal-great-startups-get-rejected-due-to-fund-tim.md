Deals falling through is the norm, not the exception. The real constraint on top-tier VCs is that each investment eliminates a competitive category and consumes one of roughly 10-12 board seats per partner. Investors signaling future interest without making an offer are declining. After silence, send concise milestone updates rather than chasing.
