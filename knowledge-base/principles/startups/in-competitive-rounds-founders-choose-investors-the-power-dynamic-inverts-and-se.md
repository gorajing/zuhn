The best startups set fundraising norms because VCs compete hardest for them. Founders with oversubscribed rounds allocate based on non-capital value like distribution, expertise, or network — not check size. In a world of abundant capital, VCs who offer distribution and media reach win allocations over those who only offer money. Capital is commoditized; the relationship and alignment determine outcomes.
