Innovation programs stall at middle management because busy managers resist adding new methodologies to full plates. Compliance processes designed for operational safety create multi-month bottlenecks that kill lean experimentation cycles. The transfer stage — where validated ideas must shift from exploration teams to execution teams — is where most innovation initiatives actually fail, not at ideation or C-suite buy-in.
