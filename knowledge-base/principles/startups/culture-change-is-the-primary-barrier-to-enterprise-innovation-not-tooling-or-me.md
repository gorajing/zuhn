The cultural shift from planning-execution to experimentation-iteration is the hardest part of enterprise innovation adoption. Innovation culture manifests as observable employee behaviors driven by structural enablers like idea meritocracy, dedicated resources, and safe-to-fail spaces — not leadership speeches about valuing innovation. Risk officers should help teams experiment safely rather than blocking experiments, and innovation should live in every job rather than be siloed into a dedicated department.
