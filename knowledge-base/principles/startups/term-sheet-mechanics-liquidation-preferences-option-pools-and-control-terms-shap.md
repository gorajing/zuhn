Participating preferred lets investors take money back first AND share remaining upside. VCs request option pool expansion pre-money to dilute founders instead of themselves, costing 5-10% in one line item. Whether the pool expands before or after investment determines who bears the dilution. Board votes are nearly always unanimous because the losing side adjusts before the vote happens.
