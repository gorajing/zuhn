Flat org structures work perfectly until approximately 20-25 employees, then collapse overnight with no gradual degradation. Companies must plan for organizational breakage at every headcount doubling and install structure before dysfunction forces it. The failure mode is a phase transition, not a slow decline, which means founders who wait for signals are already too late.
