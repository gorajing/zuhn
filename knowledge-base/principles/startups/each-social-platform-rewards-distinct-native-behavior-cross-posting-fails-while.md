Algorithmic distribution on short-form platforms is based on content quality, not follower count — a zero-follower account can get millions of views. This means each platform must be treated as its own medium with its own vibe, taste, and spirit, which is why a16z hired an 18-year-old specifically for Instagram. The winning formula is verticalized authority in one domain combined with platform-native adaptation — structuring content pyramids per platform, letting employee authenticity outperform CEO personal brand, and balancing consistency with experimentation to avoid both plateaus and sugar-crash virality.
