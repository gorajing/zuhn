The CEO's optimization mindset inherently conflicts with the exploration mindset needed for new ventures, so innovation leadership requires a dedicated C-level role. CEO time allocation to innovation is the single strongest predictor of whether innovation programs produce results. A Chief Entrepreneur needs C-suite level power and prestige to protect innovation from the execution engine's organizational antibodies. Leaders must actively protect teams' time for innovation by adapting workloads and deadlines, not just verbally endorsing it — verbal endorsement without structural change produces zero innovation.
