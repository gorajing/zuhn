BuzzFeed and VICE built their entire businesses on Facebook distribution, then Facebook deprioritized publisher content and destroyed them. Vine died because it couldn't pay creators. GameStop became obsolete when digital storefronts let publishers sell directly. A gatekeeper between you and your customers holds existential power over your business, and the historical pattern is that platforms always eventually extract or redirect the value they initially enabled.
