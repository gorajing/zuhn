Products that win long-term embed their advantage in the workflow layer around the core offering: editing software for action cameras, embeddable players for video platforms, design taste baked into building blocks, game editors shipped alongside the game. Making a powerful thing controllable and accessible (Nobel with nitroglycerin, Insta360 with mobile editing) creates more value than making the powerful thing more powerful. The moat is never the raw capability — it is the interface that makes that capability habitual.
