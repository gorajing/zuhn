Businesses typically have far more untapped leverage in their existing offer and customer base than they realize. Eight specific levers can make any single customer worth more: raise price, decrease cost, increase frequency, cross-sell, increase quantity, upgrade quality, downsell, and offer economy tiers. Price increases that reduce conversion by less than the price multiplier always increase total profit, and pricing is far more inelastic than most founders assume. Before exploring new growth channels, exhaust 10x potential in your current working channel first. When your first sale covers acquisition plus fulfillment for the next customer, growth becomes self-funding.
