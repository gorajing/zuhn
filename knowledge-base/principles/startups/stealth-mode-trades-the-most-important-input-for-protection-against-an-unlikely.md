Stealth startups sacrifice user feedback to protect against a competitive threat that may never materialize. The real value of stealth is protecting founder attention from premature public scrutiny, not protecting IP. Partial stealth -- selective disclosure to customers and partners while avoiding press -- captures most of stealth's benefits without the feedback cost. Ego, not strategy, drives most stealth decisions.
