SMBs failed to adopt SaaS tools not due to cost but because the time investment to learn and monitor them was unaffordable. Background-running AI agents eliminate this barrier, opening markets where no prior tool vendor could succeed. The defensible position in vertical AI is owning the control point that facilitates the transaction, not just saving time. Multi-user AI coordination creates compounding network effects unavailable to single-player tools, and AI enables marketplaces to capture the full buying cycle from awareness through purchase.
