Branding is not surface decoration but a worldview methodology that connects a specific concept to a name and implants community memories. Less than 10% of brands succeed, and the differentiator is preserving a single undiluted message through every growth stage — which requires the CEO to personally demonstrate the standard alongside new employees so sensibility transfers through shared experience rather than documentation. The character must be real and sustainable because manufactured personas always collapse, and the pitch must frame a mindset shift in the world rather than product features.
