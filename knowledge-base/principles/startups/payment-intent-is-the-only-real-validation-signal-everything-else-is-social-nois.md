Friends give positive feedback because they want to be nice, not because they want your product. Free signups mask product-market fit failure until the money conversation starts -- Fireship got 3,121 free signups but only 0.3% converted to paid. Recognizable problems and painful problems are completely different categories, and when everyone agrees your idea is great, someone has probably already built it. The only validation that matters is whether strangers in pain will pay, discovered through 3-5 daily customer conversations with 24-hour follow-up cycles, not weekly check-ins.
