Founders' biggest regrets are moments they were not bold enough, not moments they overreached. People stay in situations they hate because familiarity feels safer than the unknown, while cognitive depletion from value-violating work makes it impossible to build on the side. The breakthrough moment where fears dissolve will never come -- if you do not choose to change, life eventually forces change when staying comfortable becomes unbearable. Crisis and failure are often the catalysts that create the discipline for the next phase.
