Freemium works because it routes adoption through the user, not through procurement, and prediction markets reached mainstream scale by distributing through platforms with 100M+ existing accounts. Second-time founders bake GTM strategy into week one of building, treating distribution as the product rather than an afterthought. Bundling financial services raises ARPU enough to unlock previously uneconomical marketing channels, while AI products that cut process time dramatically drive organic adoption because the switching cost of NOT adopting exceeds the cost of change.
