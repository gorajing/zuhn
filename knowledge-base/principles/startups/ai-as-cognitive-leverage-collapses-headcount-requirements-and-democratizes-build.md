AI functions as a force multiplier that collapses the headcount needed for operational execution. Two people can manage 500 community members through AI agents, a 13-year-old can ship a paying product, and everyone gains access to a context-aware assistant that was previously available only to leaders with dedicated staff. The winning skill is coaching and delegating to AI — providing implicit context explicitly — not technical prompting.
