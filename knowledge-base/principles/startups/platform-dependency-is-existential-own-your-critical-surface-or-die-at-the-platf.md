Companies that build their business on another platform's distribution are perpetually one algorithm change or API restriction from extinction. Facebook destroyed BuzzFeed/VICE by deprioritizing publishers, digital storefronts killed GameStop overnight, and home consoles killed Chuck-E-Cheese's arcade pivot. The mitigation is either owning the surface entirely (Cursor forking VS Code) or diversifying across enough platforms to face taxation rather than existential risk.
