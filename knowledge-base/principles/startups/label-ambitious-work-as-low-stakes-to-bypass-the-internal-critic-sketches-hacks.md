Calling serious work 'just a hack' or 'just a sketch' lets you start by applying lower standards, then sneakily converting it once momentum builds. This works because we have no cultural customs for evaluating early ambitious work, and the default is harsh judgment that kills ideas before they can develop. BioCity's reframing of business model validation as 'scientific experimentation' made business concepts accessible to scientists — the same content, reframed, bypassed resistance.
