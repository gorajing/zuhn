Growth equals conversion minus churn, and reducing churn by 1% has the identical mathematical effect as increasing conversion by 1% but costs significantly less. Each churned customer destroys not just current revenue but years of future compounding profit, and win-back campaigns recover under 10% of lost users. Aggregate churn rates mask divergent cohort performance, hiding both critical problems and meaningful improvements -- only cohort-level analysis reveals the truth.
