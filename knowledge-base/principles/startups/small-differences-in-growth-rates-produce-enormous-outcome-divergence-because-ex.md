A small reduction in daily growth rate (e.g., from 15% to 10%) produces dramatically different outcomes over time, making rate optimization the highest-leverage intervention. Increasing growth rate by even 1% per week vastly outperforms doubling revenue extraction in startups with superlinear returns. Halving revenue on a 5%-weekly growth curve only delays you 15 weeks regardless of timeline, but missing rate improvements compounds into permanent position loss.
