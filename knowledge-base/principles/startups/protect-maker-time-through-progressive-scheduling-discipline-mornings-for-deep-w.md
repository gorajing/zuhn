The solution evolves through stages: stolen hours when solo, morning-first with back-to-front scheduling at mid-stage, and full maker days at scale. Clustering meetings at end-of-day converts interruptions into bounded compression without fragmenting deep work, and quarterly meeting audits compound into significant time recapture.
