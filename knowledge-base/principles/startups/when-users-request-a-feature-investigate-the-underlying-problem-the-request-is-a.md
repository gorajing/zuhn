Don't build what users ask for. Investigate what underlying problem triggered the request. The most valuable byproducts often exceed the intended output in impact — oxygen was photosynthesis's 'useless byproduct' that became the foundation for all animal life. In product development, the side discovery from investigating user problems frequently reveals a better product than what was requested.
