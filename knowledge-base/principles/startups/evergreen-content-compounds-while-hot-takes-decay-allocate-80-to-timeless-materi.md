Content strategy should follow an 80/20 rule: 80% evergreen material (how-to guides, frameworks, reference) and 20% timely pieces. Every piece should exist in at least five formats: audio, video, short clips, newsletter, social thread. Timeless how-to content like 'Good Product Manager Bad Product Manager' compounds in value over years while opinion pieces about market bubbles are forgotten quickly. A podcast episode operates on two axes simultaneously — distribution AND credibility — which is why its returns feel disproportionate compared to single-purpose content. The corollary: each platform has its own vibe (a16z hired an 18-year-old for Instagram, up 35% MoM) — crossposting is malpractice. Invest in platform-native experts for each channel.
