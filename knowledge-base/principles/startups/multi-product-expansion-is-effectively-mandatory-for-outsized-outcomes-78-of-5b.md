Single-product companies face structural growth ceilings. 78% of public software companies above $5B market cap are multi-product. However, multi-product moves must be earned through exceptional core reliability first — HubSpot reached IPO on GTM excellence alone, with additional products only crossing 20% of ARR five years later. Mission-critical products like Datadog must achieve exceptional core reliability before expanding, which delays but ultimately strengthens the multi-product position.
