Structured toolboxes with experiment libraries and progress mapping enable world-class innovation regardless of company size. Innovation platforms that bundle methodology and training outperform feature-rich tools lacking frameworks. Building this capability requires diagnosing organizational maturity first, investing in behavioral science to ensure skill transfer, and leveraging peer practitioner networks that scale adoption faster than top-down mandates.
