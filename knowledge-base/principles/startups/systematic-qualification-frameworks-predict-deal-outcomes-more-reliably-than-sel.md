Data from MEDDPICC implementations shows over 80% of won deals had economic buyer engagement while 80% of lost deals lacked it — the correlation between rigorous qualification and outcomes is overwhelming. Decision criteria decompose into three distinct dimensions (technical, economic, relationship) weighted differently by each stakeholder, and the paper process (procurement, legal, security) is the single biggest cause of deal slippage, which is why it must be tracked separately from the decision process. Elite sellers who recognize that competition includes internal build teams, adjacent budget competitors, do-nothing, and inertia qualify more accurately than those who only track rival vendors.
