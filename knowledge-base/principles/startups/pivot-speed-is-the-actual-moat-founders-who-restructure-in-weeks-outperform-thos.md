In high-uncertainty environments like AI, the companies winning are those that completely reinvent themselves within weeks of a landscape shift, not those with the best initial strategy. Genspark hit $36M ARR in 45 days by pivoting entirely when ChatGPT changed the landscape; YouTube pivoted from a dating site with zero uploads to general video sharing overnight. Altman explicitly rejects backward-from-vision planning because he has never seen elaborate backward-chain strategies produce massive success -- the biggest regret is always not pivoting three months earlier.
