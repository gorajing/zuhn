When introducing breakthrough ideas or unproven technology, persuasion through argument or single demos fails against entrenched skepticism. AlphaChip won over hostile chip designers by showing data weekly for years; Conrad's job at Rippling was preventing the team from quitting during two years of no traction; Nota AI survived 7-8 years of market mismatch through conviction. The pattern is that resistance dissolves through accumulated, undeniable evidence — not through pitching or one dramatic proof point.
