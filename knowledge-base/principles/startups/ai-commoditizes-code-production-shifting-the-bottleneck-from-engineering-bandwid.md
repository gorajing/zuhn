When functional prototypes cost minutes instead of weeks, the constraint on product discovery shifts from engineering bandwidth to hypothesis quality. AI simultaneously lowers the cost of building and raises the bar for what satisfies users. AI-native companies embed AI directly into the product so it works without human involvement, scaling with compute instead of headcount. The competitive moat in AI-era product development is not the model (you will never build better AI than OpenAI or Anthropic) but contextual knowledge of a specific workflow, design taste encoded into building blocks, and the speed of hypothesis-test-iterate cycles.
