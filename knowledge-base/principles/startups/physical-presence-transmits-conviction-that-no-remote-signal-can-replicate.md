Getting on the plane, showing up in person, and demonstrating shameless urgency create a commitment signal that consistently outperforms remote pitches of equal or higher quality. The medium is the message: when someone flies across the country for a 15-minute meeting, the effort itself becomes the most persuasive argument. Trust batteries start higher and deplete slower when charged through embodied interaction.
