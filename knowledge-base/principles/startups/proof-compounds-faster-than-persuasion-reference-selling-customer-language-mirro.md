Each customer success story unlocks an entire industry vertical because prospects want proof, not promise. The pattern repeats: OpenAI's Moderna deal unlocked life sciences, Morgan Stanley unlocked financial services. Invest in capturing and displaying proof rather than crafting elaborate promises. Mine book reviews and customer conversations for their exact language — matching their words creates automatic credibility. Feature comparison matrices fail because savvy buyers ignore them; instead, explain architectural decisions and lead with proof-point metrics from similar customers before attempting discovery.
