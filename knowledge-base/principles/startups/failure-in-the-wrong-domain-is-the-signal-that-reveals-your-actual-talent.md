Bezos left theoretical physics when he saw people who could effortlessly do what was hard for him -- then discovered his genius was lateral-thinking invention. Jeff Kaplan received 170 rejection letters and fell into deep depression before realizing writing was blocking the door to game design, where he thrived. Talent only reveals itself when the environment activates it, and a person can go their whole life without entering their real domain. Following curiosity from linguistics to NLP to reinforcement learning to chip design produced AlphaChip -- a breakthrough no planned career path would have created. The implication is that serial failure is not a character flaw but a search algorithm -- each wrong domain narrows the space toward the right one.
