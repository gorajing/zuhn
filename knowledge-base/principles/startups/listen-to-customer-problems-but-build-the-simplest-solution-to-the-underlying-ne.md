The product builder's job is to hear the problem behind the customer's request and build the simplest possible solution, which is often not what the customer literally asked for. Focus groups discover stated preferences, but great designers satisfy expectations customers did not know they had. Palantir builds what clients ought to want rather than what they explicitly ask for, accepting initial friction for long-term loyalty. The arrow hits the target precisely because it bends — rigidity would cause it to deflect.
