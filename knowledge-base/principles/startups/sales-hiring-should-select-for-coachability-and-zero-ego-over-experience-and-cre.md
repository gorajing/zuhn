The highest-performing sales organizations hire for character traits — coachability, hard work, team orientation, and ego absence — rather than resume credentials or industry experience. OpenAI's Maggie held the hiring line during hypergrowth because one exceptional hire beats three average ones, and her first hire was someone who accepted an SDR role despite being overqualified. No-commission cultures at Slack, OpenAI, and Stripe create collaborative builders instead of selfish closers, and cross-functional listening (sales hearing CS calls and vice versa) saves 5-10% of deals that would otherwise churn from misaligned expectations.
