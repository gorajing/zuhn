Extreme early frugality is not about the savings amount but about escaping survival mode so you can think long-term. Low personal expenses let founders fail more times without going broke, increasing the probability of eventual success. This compounds with the insight that at low savings levels, an extra $1K/month in income matters infinitely more than choosing the right index fund -- the priority is human capital investment over financial market returns.
