When eBay switched affiliate payouts from confirmed registrations to activated users, registrations dropped 20% but active users only dropped 5%, then growth accelerated. A small reduction in daily growth rate (15% to 10%) produces dramatically different outcomes over time. Every dollar not reinvested into growth is a dollar a competitor uses to catch up. Rate optimization is the highest-leverage growth intervention.
