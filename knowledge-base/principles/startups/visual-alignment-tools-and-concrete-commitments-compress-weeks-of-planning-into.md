Moving participants from seated discussions to standing around wall-mounted visual tools increases engagement and forces concrete commitments. Translating unit objectives into explicit joint commitments creates cross-unit accountability, and using available low-tech tools beats waiting for proper systems when speed of alignment matters. Team alignment through visual, physical, and explicit commitment mechanisms consistently outperforms verbal planning and strategy decks.
