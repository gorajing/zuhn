Every wealthy entrepreneur traces their start to years of high-volume sales conversations with strangers. Reaching out to 100 people daily through free channels (social media, email, communities) using personalized messages — not mass blasts — generates first customers through pure volume while building the pattern recognition and resilience that compound into long-term selling ability. The math is unforgiving: with typical B2B conversion rates you need ~800 cold emails per customer, and Everett Rogers' adoption curve means innovators are only 2.5% of any market, so massive volume is required to find willing early buyers.
