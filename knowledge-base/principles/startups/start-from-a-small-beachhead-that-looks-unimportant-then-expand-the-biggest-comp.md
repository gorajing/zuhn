History shows that dominant companies started by doing something small and unimportant, then expanded from a working beachhead. Shrinking an industry's total revenue by 95% is viable if you capture all of what remains, because incumbents can't follow you down. Positioning where the market naturally wants to go beats trying to force the market somewhere else.
