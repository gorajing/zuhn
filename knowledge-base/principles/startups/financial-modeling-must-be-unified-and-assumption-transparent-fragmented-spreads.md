When hiring plans live in Notion, runway models in Excel, and compensation decisions in separate conversations, misalignment silently erodes runway. Only a fully linked three-statement model reveals true cash position, and centralizing assumptions in one tab prevents Jenga-tower fragility. Structure revenue models so that wishful assumptions produce visibly broken outputs, forcing honest confrontation with reality.
