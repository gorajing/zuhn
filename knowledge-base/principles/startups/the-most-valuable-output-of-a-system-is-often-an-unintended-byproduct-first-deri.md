Union Pacific's real money came from land sales, not rail transport. Google's value comes from ads on search, not search itself. Shopify monetizes payments on commerce, not commerce tools. Shade balls bought to block bromate formation turned out to save 80-90% of evaporating water. This pattern — where the first derivative of a product captures more value than the product — recurs because the core product creates attention, data, or infrastructure that enables higher-margin adjacent plays. The implication: build your core product, but actively look for what it produces as a side effect.
