The vast majority of subscription conversions happen during onboarding, not during product usage. A/B testing onboarding screens, paywalls, and commitment psychology with tools like PostHog and RevenueCat yields far higher ROI than product improvements. Removing even a few screens can collapse conversion to zero, while offering free access at launch to build social proof before turning on paid ads creates a compounding advantage.
