When infrastructure commoditizes (AWS, Stripe, open-source models), every competitor gets the same building blocks and the only remaining differentiator is how fast you ship. Evolutionary simulations confirm the pattern: the highest replication rate and lowest death rate wins, not the best individual organism. In AI specifically, five-year plans are nonsensical when capabilities change monthly -- the companies that iterate in weeks while competitors plan in quarters capture the market before anyone else finishes their roadmap.
