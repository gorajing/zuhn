Domino's centralized ingredient preparation into hub facilities, enabling smaller and cheaper individual stores. McDonald's franchise realty model gave control over operators, a new revenue stream, and balance sheet assets from one structural move. In-N-Out's single-day-drive distribution constraint deliberately caps expansion to preserve quality. The pattern extends to tech: Alphabet's restructure, mullet org structures, and timezone-based handoffs all separate strategic centralization from execution distribution.
