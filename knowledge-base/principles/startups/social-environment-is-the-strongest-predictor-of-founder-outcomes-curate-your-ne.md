Your probability of success directly correlates with whether your closest friends care about and push you toward what you want to achieve. Birth zip code outpredicts IQ, race, and gender for lifetime earnings because environment shapes ambition through social comparison. Tight-knit alumni networks from breakout companies compound over decades to produce the next generation of founders. The practical implication is surgical: for each relationship, ask whether this person increases or decreases your probability of hitting your goal.
