SAFEs dominate early-stage ($10.4B across 50K+ in 2025) but stacking them without modeling conversion leads to shock dilution at the priced round. Post-money caps give investors exact ownership clarity at founders' expense. Convertible notes' apparent simplicity masks predatory terms like ultra-low caps and forced liquidity triggers. Discuss SAFEs in ownership percentage terms, not cap terms.
