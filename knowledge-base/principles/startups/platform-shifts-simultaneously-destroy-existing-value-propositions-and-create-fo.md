When underlying technology platforms shift, they invalidate current strategies completely — the iPhone and AWS destroyed P2P CDN economics overnight, and China's Bitcoin mining ban forced infrastructure rebuilding from scratch. But these forced pivots often create larger opportunities: the Bitcoin-to-AI-infrastructure pivot, building for next-generation model capabilities instead of current ones, and the collapse of existing playbooks in frontier tech all show that platform destruction is also opportunity creation. The winning response is building for the next platform generation while competitors optimize for the current one.
